The ingredients needed to make My Christmas Stuffing to go with Turkey:
  1. Get 3 Cups Sage + Onion mix
  2. Take 1/4 pint Boiling water to mix in the sage + onion
  3. Get 1 dried Chicken stock cube
  4. Make ready 3 tbls whole Cranberry sauce
  5. Take 2 tbls diced red onion
  6. Get 6 tbls pork Sausage meat
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp Black Peppper
  8. Take 1/4 cup Turkey juice when cooked
Instructions to make My Christmas Stuffing to go with Turkey:
  1. Ad the sage + onion stuffing mix to a bowl and pour on the boiling water and mix if it's still dry add a little more but not too moist, as your adding Turky juices and sausage meat later.
  2. Next add the red onion mix in.Next add the dried chicken stock mix in.
  3. Add the black pepper and cranberry sauce mix.
  4. Add the sausage meat and mix in well. Cover until you have the turkey juices.
  5. Add the turkey juice mix in and Grease the oven dish then cover with foil and add to preheated oven 180¬∞C for 30 minutes half way through the time mix it around then put back in the oven.
  6. Take off the foil and cook for 10 more minutes so its crisp on top.
  7. Serve with your Christmas Dinner. Garnish with 1 tbls Cranberry.
