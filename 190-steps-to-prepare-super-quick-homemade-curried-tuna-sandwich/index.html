The ingredients needed to make Curried Tuna Sandwich:
  1. Get 1 can drained tuna
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp sultana raisins
  3. Take 1 tsp curry powder
  4. Get 1 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  5. Take 2 slices toasted whole wheat bread
  6. Make ready 1 small carrot
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  8. Make ready 1/8 tsp salt
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp black pepper
  10. Take Juice from ½ a lemon
Steps to make Curried Tuna Sandwich:
  1. Finely dice and peel a small carrot and set aside. Drain a can of tuna and place in a small bowl. Add the mayonnaise and using a fork, mash the tuna and mayo together. Add in the salt, pepper, curry powder, and cinnamon then stir together. Then add in the diced carrot and raisins.
  2. Roll out a lemon with the palm of your hand to make it juicier then slice lengthwise. Take out the seeds and squeeze in half a lemon to the tuna mixture. Stir together once again. Toast 2 pieces of whole wheat bread and add the tuna mixture to one of the slices. Place the other slice on top and cut diagonally. I had this with a cup of oolong tea and quartered lemon slices. Enjoy!
