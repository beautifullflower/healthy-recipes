The ingredients needed to make Pizza:
  1. Get Flour
  2. Get 1 tbsp sugar
  3. Get Pinch salt
  4. Prepare 1 tsp yeast
  5. Make ready Bell peppers
  6. Get Onions
  7. Make ready Mushrooms
  8. Take Mozzarella cheese
  9. Prepare Olive oil
  10. Take Tomato ketchup
  11. Get Pizza sauce(u can also use chili sauce)
  12. Make ready Sausages
  13. Get Beef
  14. Get Corn
Steps to make Pizza:
  1. Put ur flour,olive oil,salt, sugar and water and mix. Knead ur dough and keep aside and let it rise until it doubles it size.
  2. Get ur other ingredients(bell peppers,onions,sausages,mushroom,Mozzarella cheese. Slice all ur ingredients
  4. Get ur pizza pan. Get ur dough and knead it. Put d dough on d pan. Rub ur ketchup and chili sauce or pizza sauce. Put d onions, bell pepper, sausages, mushroom, grilled beef, corn and shredded Mozzarella cheese. Preheat ur oven and bake.
