The ingredients needed to make Jacket Potato with sausage mix:
  1. Prepare Potatoes
  2. Make ready Corn
  3. Take Mushrooms chopped
  4. Get Sausages chopped
  5. Prepare Butter
  6. Make ready Salt
  7. Get Pepper
  8. Make ready Smoked paprika
  9. Get Mozzarella cheese
  10. Make ready Sauce
  11. Take 3 tbsp Mayonnaise
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp ketchup
  13. Make ready 2 tbsp french dressing
  14. Take 1 tbsp mustard
  15. Prepare Sprinkle of sugar
  16. Get 1 tsp vinegar
  17. Make ready 1 tsp onion powder
  18. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
Steps to make Jacket Potato with sausage mix:
  1. Scrub your potatoes. Rub olive oil all over. Sprinkle salt. Cover with aluminum foil. Roast in oven till soft
  2. Cut the potatoes in half. Scoop out the I side and place it in a bowl. Add butter and spices and cheese. Mix then return to the potatoes
  3. Fry mushrooms and sausage in butter
  4. Add corn and mix
  5. Add the mix to the potatoes, then the sauce, then the cheese
  6. Put the potatoes back in the oven till the cheese melts
