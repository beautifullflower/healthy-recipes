Instructions to make Quick pasta, veg and Tinned tuna in sunflower oil:

  2. Chop/dice all the vegetables. Add rapeseed oil to heated frying pan. Cook veg for 5 mins.
  3. Open Sweetcorn and Tuna. Drain both. Add to the vegetables. Add all the seasoning at this point. Fold through and heat well for 2 minutes.
