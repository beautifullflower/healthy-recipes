The ingredients needed to make Tuna pasta:
  1. Prepare 150 grams pasta
  2. Get 1 small red onion sliced
  3. Make ready Olive oil
  4. Make ready 1-2 tins tuna or salmon
  5. Make ready 2 cloves diced garlic
  6. Take 1 handful Herbs fresh of dried
  7. Prepare Chilli / either dried or fresh
  8. Make ready Rind and juice of a lemon
  9. Make ready A few mushrooms sliced
  10. Take Hand full of spinach I used two blocks of frozen spinach
  11. Get 1 tbsp capers
  12. Make ready Sweet chilli sauce optional
  13. Prepare Wedge of lemon to serve
  14. Get to taste Salt and pepper

Instructions to make Tuna pasta:
  1. Heat a pan of water add salt and once boiling throw in the pasta for 10 - 12 minutes.
  2. Whilst the pasta is boiling chop the veggies! When chopped pour in some olive oil in a large pan, heat it and throw in the red onion first for it to soften.
  3. Next add the garlic and mushrooms. Give a stir if need be add more oil and the rest of the veggies, herbs and spices.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked drain and throw into the veggies. Grate the lemon and squeeze over the juices.
  5. Mix together well and finely add the tuna mixing it in but keeping it in chunks. I finely drizzled sweet chilli sauce over and served with a wedge of lemon. Season well!
