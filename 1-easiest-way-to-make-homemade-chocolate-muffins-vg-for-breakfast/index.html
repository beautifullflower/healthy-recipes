The ingredients needed to make Chocolate muffins - vg:
  1. Take 200 g flour
  2. Take 200 g sugar
  3. Take 3 teaspoons baking powder
  4. Take 75 g cocoa
  5. Prepare 450 ml vegan mylk
  6. Make ready 3 teaspoons oil (not infused)
  7. Make ready Topping of squares of choc

Steps to make Chocolate muffins - vg:
  1. Sieve together flour, sugar, baking powder, cocoa in one bowl. Preheat oven to 160 degrees.
  2. In another bowl whisk together mylk and oil
  3. Mix wet and dry together in a bowl with a wooden spoon but don’t over mix
  4. Pour into muffin baking trays and then add a little chocolate onto the top as decoration
  5. Put in oven for 30 mins or until risen and if knife comes out clean
