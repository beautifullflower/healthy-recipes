The ingredients needed to make Reuben Biscuit Sandwiches:
  1. Get For biscuits:
  2. Make ready 4 oz full-featured cream cheese
  3. Get 1 lage egg
  4. Take 1 ts caraway seeds
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp onion powder
  7. Make ready 2 cloves garlic, minced
  8. Prepare 1-1/2 c almond flour
  9. Take 1 c shredded Swiss cheese
  10. Prepare 1/4 c heavy cream
  11. Take 1/4 c water
  12. Make ready Other ingredients:
  13. Make ready 10 slices deli corned beef, warmed
  14. Get 5 slices Swiss cheese
  15. Prepare 1 c sauerkraut
  16. Prepare 1/3 c Thousand Island dressing
Steps to make Reuben Biscuit Sandwiches:
  1. To make biscuits:
  2. Preheat oven to 350°. Line baking sheet with parchment paper or silicone baking mat.
  3. In mixing bowl, beat cream cheese and egg until well combined.
  4. Add remaining biscuit ingredients and mix until well combined.
  5. Using a large spoon, drop dough in 5 heaping mounds onto baking sheet. Bake 25 min. or until golden brown.
  6. Let biscuits cool completely on baking sheet before handling. Carefully slice biscuits in half.
  7. Assembly:
  8. Layer bottom halves of biscuits with 2 slices of beef, 1 slice cheese, heaping spoonful of sauerkraut, 1 T of dressing and top biscuit halves.
