Steps to make My chocolate doughnut style muffins:

  2. Mix 350g flour 200g sugar baking powder and salt in large bowl
  3. Mix wet ingredients and add to the dry.don't over mix
  4. Spoon into muffin cases and bake for 20 mins
  5. Allow to cool for 5mins.
  6. Melt butter in a bowl and put 2 tbsp of granulated sugar onto a plate
  7. Brush each muffin with butter and press into the sugar and that's it.enjoy
