The ingredients needed to make Quick calzone:
  1. Take 3 cup flour
  2. Prepare Base
  3. Prepare 1 cup warm water
  4. Make ready 1 tsp yeast
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
  6. Get pinch salt
  7. Prepare Filling
  8. Make ready 2 cooked chicken breast
  9. Prepare 1 can mushroom
  10. Take 1 cheese (as much as you want)
  11. Take 1 pizza sauce (as much as you want)
Instructions to make Quick calzone:
  1. In a bowl mix the flour and salt.
  2. Mix the yeast with the warm water and wait 5 minutes.
  3. Mix the water with the oil and put it in the dry mixture.
  4. Let the dough rise in a warm place for about 1 hour in a warm place.
  5. Knead everithing for about 5 minutes.
  6. Fill half of a circle with your filling (you can use a lot more filling or totaly diffrent fillings from mine).
  7. Divide the dough in 3 pieces and rool each piece in a circle.
  8. Fold the other half and crease the edge. Make two holes at the top.
  9. Put the calzone on the tray and in the praheated oven to 200°C for about 20 to 30 minutes.
  10. Let it cool and enjoy.
