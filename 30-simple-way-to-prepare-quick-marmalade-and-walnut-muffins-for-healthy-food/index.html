The ingredients needed to make Marmalade and walnut muffins:
  1. Make ready 300 g self raising flour
  2. Take 125 g butter in cubes
  3. Get 125 g golden caster sugar
  4. Prepare 75 g walnut pieces plus extra for decoration
  5. Take Zest of lemon
  6. Take 2 eggs
  7. Prepare 125 ml milk
  8. Take 125 g marmalade
Instructions to make Marmalade and walnut muffins:
  1. Rub the butter with the flour until it resembles breadcrumbs. Add the lemon zest and walnut pieces.
  2. Mix the eggs milk and marmalade
  3. Add the sugar and marmalade mixture into the crumble and mix together
  4. Pour spoonfuls of mixture into prepared muffins tin.
  5. For decoration you can use some walnut pieces with a bit of melted butter and a splash of maple syrup. Spoon over muffins and bake on a moderately hot oven. 180°C for 20 to 25 minutes
  6. I forgot to add the decorations so baked muffins nearly to the end and added walnuts for the last 3 minutes
