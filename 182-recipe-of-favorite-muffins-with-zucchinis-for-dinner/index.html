The ingredients needed to make Muffins with zucchinis:
  1. Prepare 2-3 zucchinis, grated
  2. Take 150 g strained yogurt
  3. Take 2 eggs, beaten
  4. Get 1/2 cup corn oil
  5. Make ready 1 cup self rising flour
  6. Make ready 150 g grated feta cheese
  7. Prepare salt (a little)
  8. Take 1-2 tsp spearmint and parsley
Steps to make Muffins with zucchinis:
  1. Mix all the ingredients together to make a thick batter.
  2. Brush your muffin tin with oil or use mufiin cups and sprinkle them with breadcrumbs.
  3. Pour 2-3 spoonfuls of the batter into each cup and bake at 180°C until the top is golden brown (about 50 minutes depending on the oven).
