Step-by-Step Guide to Prepare Ultimate Summer Pita lunch

To get started with this particular recipe, we must prepare a few ingredients. You can have summer pita lunch using 9 ingredients and 9 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Summer Pita lunch:
  1. Make ready pita bread
  2. Make ready chicken breasts
  3. Make ready olive oil
  4. Get mixed vegetables salad
  5. Prepare roman tomatoes
  6. Make ready Garlic and herb salad dressing
  7. Make ready tspn pepper
  8. Make ready Salt
  9. Get Fresh parsley to garnish
Steps to make Summer Pita lunch:
  1. Cut the chicken breasts into small strips.
  2. Heat the pan and add oil,and add chicken strips. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook until the chicken is browned but not dry, remove set aside.
  4. Heat a grill pan, and place the pita bread,cook 2 minutes each side of each pita.
  5. Next make a pocket unto each pita using a knife, set aside
  6. Next, in a bowl add cooked chicken, vegetables, add the dressing and combine everything together.
  7. Next use a tablespoon to add the salad to the pita,making a pocket like.
  8. Next on top of each pita add 2 to 3 tomatoes cut into half.
  9. Garnish with parsley
