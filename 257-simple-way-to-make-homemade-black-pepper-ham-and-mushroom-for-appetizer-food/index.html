The ingredients needed to make Black Pepper Ham And Mushroom:
  1. Prepare 2 cup bak choy
  2. Get 10 slice black pepper flavoured ham or any ham
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp oil
  4. Make ready 3 small onion
  5. Take 1 can button mushrooms
  6. Get 1 tbsp fine dice garlic and shallot
  7. Take BLACK PEPPER SAUCE MIXTURE
  8. Get 4 tbsp store bought black pepper sauce or diy
  9. Take 2 tbsp water
Steps to make Black Pepper Ham And Mushroom:
  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil then add some salt and quick blanch the bak choy for 1 minutes then drain dry
  2. Baked the black pepper ham slice with(store bought black pepper sauce or diy black pepper sauce)with some water for 10 minutes then add slice button mushroom and baked for another 10 minutes - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/366296-black-pepper-sauce
