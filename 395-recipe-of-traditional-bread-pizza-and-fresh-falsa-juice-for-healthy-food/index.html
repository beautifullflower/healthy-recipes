The ingredients needed to make Bread pizza and fresh falsa juice:
  1. Take 4 medium size slice
  2. Take 3-4 tbsp Pizza sauce
  3. Take 50 gm ready chicken or beef tikka boti
  4. Get 2 piece Mushrooms
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp black olves
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp green olives
  7. Make ready 1 onion medium square cut small
  8. Take 1 large shimla mirch cut in small cubes
  9. Prepare 1 cup Mozzarala cheese
  10. Prepare 1 cup Chedder cheese
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp red chilli flaks
  12. Get 1 tbsp mix gerb (italian seassonig)
  13. Take Oil
  14. Take For falsa juice
  15. Take 250 gm falsa
  16. Make ready 1/2 cup Cheeni
  17. Prepare Ice cubes 6-7
  18. Get Kala namak
  19. Make ready Lemo juice
Steps to make Bread pizza and fresh falsa juice:
  1. Non stick fry pan ko preheat chulhe pe garam hon rkhdein.
  2. Ab slice pe pizza sauce spread karein.sath hi
  3. Ab sauce ke baad chicken or beef tikka boti rakhein sath hi all thing step by step rakh dein
  4. Atlast cheese dono cheese spread karein or red chilli crushed and mix herbs sprinkle karein.
  5. Ab tayyar bread ko 10 min halki anch pe pakae opper se total covered kar dein.
  6. After 10 min pizza ready ha
  7. For falsa juice.
  8. Falson ko achi tarah wash karein or blender mein ghuma kar strainer mein chan kein.
  9. Ab ready juice mein sugar kala namak or lemon juice or ice cubes dal kar blender mein 1 dafa ghuma lien.
  10. Chilled or yummy fals juice ready ha.
