The ingredients needed to make Leftover Pita Bread Pizza:
  1. Take 1 leftover pita bread
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp pizza sauce
  3. Get 1/2 chopped onion
  4. Prepare 4 chopped mushrooms
  5. Take 1/2 chopped capsicum
  6. Make ready 1/2 chopped tomato
  7. Get 100 grams mozzarella cheese
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp dried herbs
  9. Get 1/2 tsp olive oil
Instructions to make Leftover Pita Bread Pizza:
  1. Take a pita bread and lightly toast in a pan till slightly brown on both sides.
  2. Apply 1/2 tsp olive oil on one side of the pita bread. This will help to prevent pita from getting soggy because of the sauce and cheese.
  3. After applying oil, put pizza sauce, vegetables and cheese over it.
  4. Place it in a pan with lid and cook on low flame for 5-8 mins, until cheese melts.
  5. Take off the heat, add dried herbs, cut into slices and serve hot.
  6. Note: if you do not want to cook it in a pan, cook it in oven at 200° C for 10 mins, until cheese melts.
  7. Serve hot with ketchup and mustard sauce.
