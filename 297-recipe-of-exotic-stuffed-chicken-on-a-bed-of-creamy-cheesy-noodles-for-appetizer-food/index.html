The ingredients needed to make Stuffed chicken on a bed of creamy cheesy noodles:
  1. Take 2 chicken breasts
  2. Take 2/3 ball of mozzarella
  3. Make ready 2 pinch chilli flakes
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon thyme
  5. Get 1/2 teaspoon hot chilli powder
  6. Take 1 teaspoon gram masala
  7. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon ground chilli powder
  8. Take 1 teaspoon ground sweet paprika
  9. Take Salt and pepper seasoning
  10. Get Drizzle of chilli oil
  11. Make ready Knob butter
  12. Take 1 diced onion
  13. Get 1/4 teaspoon garlic (or 1 clove)
  14. Make ready 10 cherry tomatoes
  15. Take 5 small mushrooms (quartered)
  16. Prepare 1/2 tub mascarpone
  17. Get 100 g mature cheddar
  18. Prepare 1 Packet spinach
  19. Get 1 packet fresh egg noodles
Instructions to make Stuffed chicken on a bed of creamy cheesy noodles:
  1. Insert knife into each chicken breast at the end, moving around to create a hole to stuff
  2. Taking half the mozzarella for each chicken breast, stuff inside, filling entire cavity evenly
  3. Combine all dry ingredients (thyme, chilli powder, paprika, gram masala and 1 pinch of chilli flakes) add salt and pepper to season
  4. Rub half of the dry ingredients over each chicken breast
  5. Place chicken on oven dish and drizzle with chilli oil
  6. Cook chicken at 200c for 20 minutes
  7. Turn over chicken at 20 minutes and cook until noodles are ready (approx 10 minutes)
  8. Melt butter in sautÃ© pan and add onion. Brown for a little while then add garlic. Stir together
  9. Cook spinach to packet instructions whilst following next instructions
  10. Add remaining chilli flakes, then whole cherry tomatoes. Cook for a little while then add mushrooms
  11. Add mascarpone and cheddar, stirring until it melts
  12. Add cooked and drained spinach, stir in then add the noodles
  13. Heat up the noodles for a few minutes, check the chicken is ready and cooked through - then serve!
