To begin with this particular recipe, we have to prepare a few components. You can have pizza using 5 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Pizza:
  1. Get 1 pizza base
  2. Prepare 2 tsp tomato sauce
  3. Take 2 tsp pizza sauce
  4. Take 1 cup mix vegetables(capsicum, Paneer,onion, Mushroom)
  5. Take As needed mozzarella cheese
Steps to make Pizza:
  1. Take pizza base and apply both sauces.add onion.
  2. Then add paneer, capsicum.
  3. Spread mozzarella cheese nicely.
  4. Keep it in the microwave oven and cook for ten minutes.
  5. Pizza is ready to serve.
  6. Serve hot.
