The ingredients needed to make Corn Bread Muffins:
  1. Make ready 1 Cup Flour
  2. Prepare 1 cup Yellow Cornmeal
  3. Take 2-3 Cups granulated Sugar
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon Salt
  5. Take 3 1/2 Teaspoons baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/3 Cup Cooking Oil or melted Butter
  7. Get 1 large Egg
  8. Get 1 Cup Milk
Instructions to make Corn Bread Muffins:
  1. Grease a 9 inch cake pan, or 24 Muffin tray, and set aside. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees(200 degrees or gas mark 6)
  2. Add all dry ingredients to a large bowl and mix well using a hand whisk.
  3. Make a well in the center and pour the Oil, Milk and eggs. Stir until the mixture comes together with no lumps.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 20-30 minutes, until the top is golden brown, and a toothpick,inserted, comes out clean.
  5. Can be made into Muffins(24 ?). Or into smaller cake tins, only 10-15 minutes for the Muffins and 15-20 minutes for in between sizes.!!
