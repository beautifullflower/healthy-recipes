The ingredients needed to make Egg ham & cheese muffin:
  1. Make ready Muffin
  2. Take 1 egg
  3. Take 2 slices cheese
  4. Get 1 slice ham
  5. Get Black pepper
  6. Get Salt or garlic salt
Instructions to make Egg ham & cheese muffin:
  1. Cook egg which ever way you like your eggs add some salt and black pepper at your taste
  2. While your egg is cooking open up the muffin and the cheese slices and ham like so
  3. Place your muffin in the oven just enough to where the cheese is melted and the ham looks cooked enough. Roughly about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Once done toasting add egg and if you like add some sauce or leave it plain.
  5. Enjoy your egg ham & cheese muffin!
