The ingredients needed to make Zax Salesman Tupperware Lunch:
  1. Get Spinach
  2. Prepare Corn
  3. Get Ground Italian Sausage
  4. Take Carrots
  5. Take Celery
  6. Make ready Onion
  7. Get Garlic
  8. Make ready Eggs
  9. Make ready Chicken broth
  10. Get Chipotle Hot Sauce
Steps to make Zax Salesman Tupperware Lunch:
  1. Heat a tablespoon of butter in a wok or large saucepan.
  2. Toss in onion and garlic, both diced. Let cook until opaque. Low and slow is best.
  3. Now add the sausage, breaking up as much as possible. Stir around until you feel happy on medium heat. Don't worry about cooking meat through yet.
  4. Add celery and carrots (peeled and chopped.) Salt, pepper and hot sauce it now. Toss/stir and cover on low for 5 mins.
  5. Mix in spinach, corn and broth and stir until its evenly mixed.
  6. Let the mix steam out some of the wateriness for about 7 mins, mix, add the eggs (scrambled or not is up to you) and let simmer until egg is cooked.
  7. Let cool some then seperate into 5 tupperware containers for the week. You'll hate it by Wednesday.
  8. Remember: Never follow a recipe 100%. Put your own spin on it, have fun and ENJOY!
