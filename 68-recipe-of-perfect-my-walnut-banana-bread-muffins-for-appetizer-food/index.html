The ingredients needed to make My walnut banana (bread) muffins:
  1. Get 2 ripe bananas mashed
  2. Take 1 1/2 cup plain flour
  3. Get 1 tsp bi carb of soda
  4. Get 1 cup caster sugar
  5. Take 1/2 cup butter melted
  6. Make ready 2 eggs beaten
  7. Take 1/2 cup walnuts
  8. Take 1/4 cup milk
  9. Prepare Oats to sprinkle
Steps to make My walnut banana (bread) muffins:
  1. Mash bananas add eggs and mix well
  2. Add flour,butter,bi carb walnuts and milk mix together
  3. Spoon mixture into 12 muffin cases sprinkle with oats
  4. Bake in 160° oven for 30 mins
