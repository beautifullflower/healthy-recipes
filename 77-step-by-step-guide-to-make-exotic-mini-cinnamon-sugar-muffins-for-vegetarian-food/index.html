The ingredients needed to make Mini Cinnamon Sugar Muffins:
  1. Get For the muffins:
  2. Prepare 100 g caster sugar
  3. Make ready 190 g gluten free self raising flour
  4. Prepare 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  5. Prepare 1 egg beaten
  6. Take 120 ml dairy free milk
  7. Take 75 g dairy free spread melted
  8. Make ready For the topping:
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp caster sugar
  10. Get 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  11. Prepare 30 g dairy free spread melted
Steps to make Mini Cinnamon Sugar Muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 oC - Grease a mini mould muffin tin
  2. Mix together the caster sugar, gluten free flour, cinnamon, egg, milk and melted dairy free spread
  3. Half fill the muffin tin - Bake in the oven for 20 minutes
  4. Make the topping by mixing together the caster sugar and cinnamon - Brush the top of each muffin with the melted dairy free spread and sprinkle on the cinnamon sugar
  5. Serve while still warm
