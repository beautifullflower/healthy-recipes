The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Crispy Chicken:
  1. Take 1 piece Crispy Chicken or Chicken patty
  2. Make ready Mozzarella cheese
  3. Take Brocolli
  4. Prepare Mushroom
  5. Prepare Sweet Corn
  6. Make ready Bolognese sauce
  7. Prepare Dry oregano
  8. Prepare Onion
Steps to make Cheesy Crispy Chicken:
  1. Spread bolognese sauce on chicken
  2. Add mozzarella cheese, broccoli, mushroom, sweet corn, onion and oregano
  3. Baked it. After it done, you can enjoy it with white rice.
