The ingredients needed to make Healthy Breakfast (Pizza omelette):
  1. Take 4 eggs
  2. Make ready 1 small diced bell peppers (I used yellow and red)
  3. Prepare 1 small diced red onion
  4. Take 3-4 sliced any type of mushroom I took can one
  5. Take Small bunch of baby Spinach or regular Spinach (fined chopped)
  6. Prepare 4 small cube of pineapple
  7. Take 4 small black olive
  8. Make ready 2 TBSP milk
  9. Take Black pepper (2tsp)
  10. Get Salt (to taste)
  11. Take 1/4 cup Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  12. Get Butter (to taste)
Instructions to make Healthy Breakfast (Pizza omelette):
  1. Mix all the ingredients in the bowl
  2. Take a pan and put 1/2 Tbsp of butter and let it heat
  3. And pour the eggs And cover with the lid and let it cook on low medium heat for 5 minutes.
  4. After 5 minutes when the omelet half done then decorate it with some thin slice of Red bell pepper and some olives and cover it again. Let it completely cook on low flame.
  5. After 10 minutes take out the omelet on the plate.
  6. Serve with the whole bread or pancake and enjoy it.
