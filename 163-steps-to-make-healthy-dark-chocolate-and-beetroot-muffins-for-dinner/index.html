The ingredients needed to make Dark chocolate and beetroot muffins 🍫:
  1. Prepare 2 eggs
  2. Take 125 ml vegetable oil
  3. Prepare 250 ml semi skimmed milk
  4. Take 250 g golden caster sugar
  5. Make ready 400 g self raising flour
  6. Get 5 small cooked beetroots
  7. Make ready 1 heaped tbsp dark chocolate cocoa powder
Instructions to make Dark chocolate and beetroot muffins 🍫:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180c and line a muffin tray with paper cases
  2. In a bowl beat the eggs lightly, then add the oil, milk and sugar and whisk until smooth
  3. Sieve the flour into the bowl and whisk till smooth
  4. Purée the beetroot
  5. Then add the beetroot and dark cocoa powder. Taste and adjust quantities. Whisk in some air to get a good rise.
  6. Fill the muffin cases and bake for 25-30 mins. The muffins should be firm to touch and when a knife inserted comes out clean
  7. Serve and enjoy
