The ingredients needed to make Minty Chicken:
  1. Get 800 gm chicken
  2. Take as needed curd
  3. Prepare 4-5 inch ginger
  4. Prepare 10 green chilli splits
  5. Take 10-15 almond
  6. Take 1/2 tsp sugar
  7. Get to taste Salt
  8. Make ready 5 tbsp butter
  9. Make ready 1 cup coconut milk
  10. Take 1 beetroot
Steps to make Minty Chicken:
  1. Marinated chicken with salt. fried their each n every parts for 5-6 minutes into butter.
  2. Coarsely grinded ginger garlic & 2 greenchilli.Then added thz spices into chicken & stirring it for 2 minutes.
  3. Now added curd & stirring it. Covered it for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Make a paste of almond and added thz into chicken.
  5. Again stirring it. spread greenchilli splits & sugar. Covered it again for 10 minutes. When it'll be ready spread Mint leaves.
