The ingredients needed to make Egg muffin#ramadan special#:
  1. Take 2 eggs
  2. Get 1/2 cup milk
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup paneer/cheese
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup finely chopped onion and tomatoes. you can also add corn
  5. Take 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp mixed herbs
  7. Take 1/2 tsp each baking powder and baking soda
  8. Take to taste salt
Instructions to make Egg muffin#ramadan special#:
  1. Beat eggs with milk.
  2. Now add paneer/cheese, vegetables, black pepper powder, herbs and salt. Mix everything well.
  3. At last add baking powder and baking soda mix gently.
  4. Grease muffin mould pour the above batter in moulds.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes at 180 deg celcius.
  6. You can also bake it in cooker. Remove the ring and whistle. Pour some salt at the bottom of cooker. Heat the cooker for 5 minutes.
  7. Now keep a stand in it and keep muffin moulds in it
  8. Close the cooker and bake it for 15 minutes.
  9. Now the muffins are ready to serve.! HAPPY COOKING!
