The ingredients needed to make Pizza rolls:
  1. Make ready 2 rolls of ready rolled puff pastry
  2. Make ready Tomato pizza sauce
  3. Take Black olives
  4. Get Artichokes
  5. Prepare Mushrooms
  6. Prepare Dairy free cheese
Steps to make Pizza rolls:
  1. Unroll the base and spread on your pizza sauce.
  2. Add the toppings and cheese, roll back up and slice up placing in a muffin tin.
  3. Pop in the oven for 20 minutes at 180 degrees.
