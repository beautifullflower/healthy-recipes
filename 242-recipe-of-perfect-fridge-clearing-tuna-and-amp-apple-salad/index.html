The ingredients needed to make Fridge Clearing Tuna & Apple Salad:
  1. Get 1 tin tuna
  2. Prepare 1 tomato, diced
  3. Make ready 4 raw Brussels sprouts, thinly sliced
  4. Get 3 spring onions, sliced
  5. Prepare 1/2 small apple, cored and diced
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp mayo
  7. Take Sprinkling of paprika
Steps to make Fridge Clearing Tuna & Apple Salad:
  1. Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl, season to taste with salt and pepper, and sprinkle with paprika to serve.
