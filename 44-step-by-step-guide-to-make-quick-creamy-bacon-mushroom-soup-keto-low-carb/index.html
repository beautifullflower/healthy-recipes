The ingredients needed to make Creamy Bacon Mushroom Soup (Keto/Low Carb):
  1. Prepare 2 cups chicken broth
  2. Prepare 227 g Mushrooms (small pkg)
  3. Make ready 3 slices bacon
  4. Make ready 1/3 cup almond flour
  5. Take 200 g cream cheese
  6. Prepare 1/2 small onion
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp avocado oil
  8. Prepare 1 tbs heavy cream
  9. Take 1/2 tsp Italian spice
Instructions to make Creamy Bacon Mushroom Soup (Keto/Low Carb):
  1. Add bacon, onion and mushroom to frying pan. Cook to until brown add to soup pot.
  2. In soup pot (concurrent with step 1), add broth, cream cheese, oil, pepper, Italian spice together.
  3. Simmer 15 mins add almond flour and heavy cream. Stir well.
