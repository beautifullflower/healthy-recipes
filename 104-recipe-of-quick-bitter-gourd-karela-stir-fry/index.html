The ingredients needed to make Bitter gourd/karela stir-fry:
  1. Get 500 gms bitter gourd/karela
  2. Prepare 4 tablespoon olive oil/vegetable oil
  3. Get 2 onion sliced
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup tamarind pulp
  5. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  6. Get 1 tsp red chilli powder
  7. Take 1 pinch turmeric
Instructions to make Bitter gourd/karela stir-fry:
  1. First peel and cut the bitter gourd/karela with remove inside pulp.now rub 1 tsp of salt and wash with cold water.discard water and pat dry it with paper towel.now heat 4 tbsn oil in a pan add in all chopped bitter gourd/karela
  2. Stir-fry till its golden brown and remove on paper towel to drain excess oil.
  3. Now same pan with same oil,stir-fry onions till translucent,then add chilli powder and mix well.
  4. Now add turmeric powder with salt and mix well.
  5. Now add in stir-fry bitter gourd/karela with tamarind water and saute on medium heat till well combined.serve hot with whole wheat chapati.This recipe is very healthy and tasty.tamarind water takes away the bitterness.
