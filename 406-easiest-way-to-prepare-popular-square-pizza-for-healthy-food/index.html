The ingredients needed to make Square pizza 🍕🍕🍕🍕:
  1. Make ready 3/4 cup water
  2. Prepare 2 tsp sugar
  3. Take 1 tsp yeast
  4. Prepare 2 cups all purpose flour
  5. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Take 2 tbs oil
  7. Get 2 tbs water if required
  8. Prepare For filling:
  9. Take 300 gm chicken strips
  10. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  11. Take 1 tsp salt or to taste
  12. Make ready 2 tsp red chilli powder
  13. Make ready 1 tsp red chilli flakes
  14. Get 1 tsp oregano
  15. Prepare 2 tbs lemon juice
  16. Get 1/2 cup mushrooms
  17. Make ready 1 medium onion sliced
  18. Take 1/2 capsicum julienne
  19. Get Pizza sauce as required
  20. Make ready 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese grated
  21. Get 1/2 cup cheddar cheese grated
  22. Make ready Black olives if u like
  23. Get 2-3 tbs cooking oil
  24. Prepare Corns as required
Instructions to make Square pizza 🍕🍕🍕🍕:
  1. Sb se pehle lukewarm water ma yeast and sugar dal kar 10 minutes ke lye cover kar ke rakh dein….an 1 bowl ma flour add karein and salt add kar ke mix kar lein… an is ma yeast mixture dal kar mix karein ar goondh lein ab is ma pani required ho to add kar ke goondh lein… now add oil and make soft dough… cover it at warm place for 2 hours… u can make 2 medium pizza from this dough… but I made only 1…
  2. 1 pan ma oil dal kar chicken fry karein jab colour change ho jaye to garlic powder add kar ke mix karein ab baqi spices add kar 2 -3 minutes cook karein then add lemon juice and mix it..now add mushrooms and cook 1 minute…. after this add onion and capsicum and stir fry for 2 minutes…
  3. After 2 hours dough rise ho jaye to is ma punch mar ke goondh lein… ab thora sa maida sprinkle kar ke dough ko bail lein and baking tray ke size ke according set kar lein…
  4. Now spread pizza sauce cooked chicken, mozzarella and cheddar cheese and black olives on it… I don't like black olives so I used corns coz I love corns..
  5. And bake it in preheated oven at 200°c for 15 minutes…. when pizza is ready sprinkle some red chilli flakes and oregano on it…. yummy pizza is ready….
