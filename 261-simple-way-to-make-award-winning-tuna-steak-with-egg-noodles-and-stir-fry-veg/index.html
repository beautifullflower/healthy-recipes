The ingredients needed to make Tuna steak with egg noodles and stir fry veg:
  1. Get 2 Tuna steaks
  2. Prepare 2 nests of egg noodles
  3. Prepare 1 pack stir fry veg packet
  4. Prepare 1 lime
  5. Make ready Soy sauce
  6. Get Mirin
  7. Get Vegetable oil
Instructions to make Tuna steak with egg noodles and stir fry veg:
  1. Cook stir fry veg in frying pan or wok using a dash of vegetable oil
  2. Cook egg noodles per packet instructions
  3. Fry tuna steaks in soy sauce and mirin to taste
  4. Dress vegetables with soy sauce and squeeze lime over tuna steaks
  5. Bon apetit!
