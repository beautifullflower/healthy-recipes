The ingredients needed to make Pizza:
  1. Prepare For Pizza Dough
  2. Get 1 cup All-purpose Flour(maida)
  3. Make ready 1 tsp Yeast
  4. Take 1 tsp Sugar
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp Salt
  6. Take Water as required
  7. Prepare 1 Egg
  8. Take 2 tbsp Oil
  9. Prepare For topping
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup Pizza sauce
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup Cooked Chicken chunks
  12. Get 1/2 cup Mozarella cheese
  13. Take 2 tbsp Black Olives sliced
  14. Take 2 tbsp Mushroom sliced
  15. Take 1 capsicum small
Instructions to make Pizza:
  1. Mix flour with salt, sugar, yeast. Make a well in centre. Add a beaten egg. Add oil and knead dough with water
  2. Roll dough in round ball. Keepin airtight box in some warm place to rise for approx 1 hour.
  3. After an hour dough has risen. Punch dough to release air. Take out from box and roll in round shape. Grease a mould with oil and flour.
  4. Place dough in tin. Prick dough with fork from some places. Spread pizza sauce over dough
  5. Spread cooked chicken chunks over sauce. Add cubes of capsicum slice mushroom and spread cheese. Add black olives on top
  6. Bake in preheated oven at 180¬∞c for 25-30 min. Do check in between.
  7. When the base is cooked & cheese melts at top. Turn off heat. Take out from oven. Serve hot and enjoy healthy homemade pizza
