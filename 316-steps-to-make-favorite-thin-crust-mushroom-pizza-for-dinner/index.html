The ingredients needed to make Thin Crust Mushroom Pizza:
  1. Prepare 500 grams flour (A)
  2. Make ready 250 grams water (A)
  3. Get 1 tablespoon sugar (A)
  4. Make ready 1 TSP salt (A)
  5. Take 1 TSP instant dry yeast (A)
  6. Prepare 2 tablespoons olive oil (A)
  7. Take 1 package fresh large white mushrooms (B)
  8. Prepare 30 grams butter (B)
  9. Take 1/2 TSP salt (B)
  10. Prepare 2 medium sized tomatoes (C)
  11. Get 1/2 medium sized red onion (C)
  12. Take 2 cloves garlic minced (C)
  13. Get 1/2 TSP salt (C)
  14. Prepare 1 handful parsley leaves (C)
  15. Make ready 1/2 TSP black pepper (C)
  16. Take 2 tablespoons tomato paste (C)
  17. Make ready 250 grams grated mozzarella cheese
  18. Take can oil spray
Instructions to make Thin Crust Mushroom Pizza:
  1. To prepare the dough add the solid ingredients, mix them well and add the water. Max dough into a single ball; but don't knead for a long time. Cover and let sit for hours.
  2. Melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms in a single layer. Turn to the other side when brown. Add salt after they get cold in a side dish.
  3. Add all (C) ingredients in a blender. Check the desired consistency and taste. Set aside.
  4. Take half the dough after it has risen and roll on a floured surface with a rolling pin into a thin layer to cover up the whole area of your pizza stone.
  5. Now we assemble everything. First spray the pizza stone with the oil. Spread the dough. Add the tomato sauce then the mozzarella cheese and finally the mushrooms.
  6. Stick in a preheated oven at 180 degrees C for 20 minutes or until the dough moves easily on the stone and the surface of the pizza is golden brown.
  7. Slice the pizza and serve after 10 minutes rest.
