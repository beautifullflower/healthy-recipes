The ingredients needed to make Keto Breakfast Sandwich:
  1. Make ready Buns
  2. Take 4 Large eggs
  3. Get 2 Cups Shredded Colby Jack Cheese
  4. Take 1 Tsp Imitation Vanilla Extract
  5. Get 1 Tsp Cinnamon
  6. Make ready 1 Tsp Salt
  7. Make ready 1 Tsp Black Pepper
  8. Take Sausage Patty
  9. Get 1/2 Lb Ground Pork Sausage
  10. Get 1 Tsp Salt
  11. Prepare 1 Tsp Pepper
  12. Take Egg w/ Cheese
  13. Prepare 1 Slice Cheddar Cheese
  14. Take 1 Large Egg
Steps to make Keto Breakfast Sandwich:
  1. In a bowl whish 4 large eggs with 2 cups of Shredded Colby Jack Cheese and seasonings. I use a cast iron burger press piece to pour egg mix into to form buns. Cook until ready to flip and then flip and remove cast iron and continue to cook until firm.
  2. In a bowl mix ground pork sausage with seasoning and form patties. Cook until done all the way through.
  3. Cook an egg over medium and melt cheese over top. Then assemble the sandwich and enjoy!
