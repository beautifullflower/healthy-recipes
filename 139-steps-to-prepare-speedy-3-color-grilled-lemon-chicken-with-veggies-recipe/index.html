Instructions to make 3 color Grilled lemon chicken with veggies Recipe:
  1. Take 3 chicken fillets and cut it into half now grate lemon zest all over and sprinkle black pepper powder n apply it all over..
  2. Add ginger powder,salt and garlic paste and apply it well add thyme also
  3. Squeeze lemon juice and marinade it well let it rest for an hour..
  4. Now Grease the grill pan with butter or ghee add marinated chicken. Flip sides in between till its done..
  5. Add olive oil in wok or pan,add garlic paste and saute it,
  6. Add cabbage and stir fry it Sprinkle thyme,black pepper powder and salt and mix it well.. than add blanched spinach and cook it for few seconds..
  7. Take out chicken pieces in serving dish, add prepared veggies on top or at side.. - Grilled lemon chicken with veggies is ready to serve..
