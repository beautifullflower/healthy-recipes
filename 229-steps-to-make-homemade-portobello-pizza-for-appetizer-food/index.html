The ingredients needed to make Portobello Pizza:
  1. Prepare 4 large Portobello mushrooms
  2. Prepare 1 onion finely diced
  3. Make ready 1 large tomato chopped
  4. Make ready 4 tsp tomato paste
  5. Prepare Shredded cheese to your liking
Instructions to make Portobello Pizza:
  1. Pre heat air fryer with grill pan, place mushrooms, let it toast for 2-3 minutes, set aside
  2. Now dress up mushrooms, spread tomato paste on base, drizzle onions, cheese, place tomatoes, you can sprinkle some oregano and bake for 6-8 minutes until cheese has melted and the top is slightly crusty. Enjoy!!!
