The ingredients needed to make Tuna Frittata:
  1. Prepare 2 eggs
  2. Take 2 Tablespoon milk or unsweetened almond milk
  3. Get 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  4. Get 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese
  6. Take 1 can tuna drained and fluffed
  7. Make ready 2 Tablespoon pickled jalapenos chopped (optional)
  8. Make ready 2 Tablespoon chopped basil optional
  9. Prepare 4-6 cherry tomatoes halved
Instructions to make Tuna Frittata:
  1. In a large bowl, beat the egg, milk, salt and pepper then stir in the rest of the ingredients.
  2. Pour the mixture into a greased shallow bake ware. Air fry at 360F (180C) for about 10 minutes or until the egg is set.
