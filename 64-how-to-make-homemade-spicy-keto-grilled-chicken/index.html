The ingredients needed to make Spicy keto grilled chicken:
  1. Get 500 gm chicken casserole pieces
  2. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  3. Get 1 tsp ginger powder
  4. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  5. Prepare 1 tsp pepper powder
  6. Get 1 tsp chilli powder
  7. Get 1 tsp curry powder
  8. Get 1 tbsp water
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup almond meal
  11. Get 1/2 cup breaded pork rinds
  12. Prepare 1 egg

Steps to make Spicy keto grilled chicken:
  1. Marinate the chicken with all the spice powders and water. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200 C°, fan forced mode.
  3. Mix the parmesan cheese, almond meal and breaded pork rinds.
  4. Beat the egg. Dip the marinated chicken pieces with the egg wash.
  5. Coat the chicken pieces with the breading mixture.
  6. Grill the chicken pieces for 25 mins turning once midway through. Enjoy!
