The ingredients needed to make Easy egg muffins:
  1. Prepare 4 eggs
  2. Take 200 ml milk
  3. Make ready 4 spring onions, sliced
  4. Take 1/2 grated courgette
  5. Make ready Feta cheese
  6. Prepare slices Chorizo
  7. Take Seasoning
Steps to make Easy egg muffins:
  1. Mix everything in a plastic bowl
  2. Pour into a muffin tin & bake for around 15 mins (or until brown)
  3. Enjoy or store for a later date!
