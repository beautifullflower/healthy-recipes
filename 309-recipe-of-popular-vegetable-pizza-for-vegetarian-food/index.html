The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Pizza:
  1. Make ready 500 Grams pizza dough (see recipe)
  2. Get 2,3 Tablespoons pizza sauce (see recipe)
  3. Get Filling
  4. Prepare 1 Onions medium size
  5. Prepare 5,6 Mushrooms medium size
  6. Prepare 1 Artichoke frozen
  7. Make ready 3 Bell Pepper 3 colour small size
  8. Make ready 6,7 Cherry tomatoes
  9. Get 2,3 Thai red chilli fresh
  10. Make ready 1 Tablespoon Parmesan Cheese
  11. Get 2 Tablespoons Emmental cheese
  12. Make ready 2 Tablespoons mozzarella cheese
  13. Prepare Olives as required
Steps to make Vegetable Pizza:
  1. Chopped the red Chillies Finley and add in pizza sauce,
  2. Make a ball with a pizza dough, dust the flour on baking tray, flatten the dough to make the shape you want I make heart shape,spread the pizza sauce on the pizza dough.
  3. Step by step Add onion rings then mushrooms, bell pepper.and some olives
  4. Then add cherry tomatoes,sprinkle parmesan cheese,
  5. Then Emmental cheese and then mozzarella cheese in last
  6. Preheat oven on 250 for 20 minutes, then put pizza tray in oven - Low the heat on 200° and let it cook for 20 minutes - After 20 minutes check the pizza and increase the heat to 250 and let it cook more 10 to 15 minutes until cheese melted and turn into golden colour, cut it and serve with your favourite sauce
