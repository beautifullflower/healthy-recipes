To get started with this particular recipe, we have to first prepare a few ingredients. You can have pizza with shawarma bread using 8 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you cook it.

The ingredients needed to make Pizza with shawarma bread:
  1. Take 8 Shawarma bread
  2. Take 1 pack chicken chunks
  3. Take Pizza sauce
  4. Get Peri peri hot sauce
  5. Take Olives
  6. Make ready Mushrooms
  7. Get Capsicum chopped
  8. Take Cheese
Instructions to make Pizza with shawarma bread:
  1. Chicken chucks ko microwave main garam kar lein 2 minutes.
  2. Pher aik shawarma bread lyn or us ko pan main dono taraf sy paka lyn.
  3. Ab bowl main pizza sauce or peri peri hot sauce mix kar ke rakh lyn.
  4. Sb sy pehlay shawarma bread par sauce lagalyn pher chicken chunks rakhayn pher shimla mirch, olives or mushrooms spread karayn or ab cheese daal kar pher sy oven main 1 minute cheese melt honay tak garam karayn. aur serve karyn.
