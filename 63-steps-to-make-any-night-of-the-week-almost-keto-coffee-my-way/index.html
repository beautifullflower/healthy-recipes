The ingredients needed to make Almost Keto ☕ Coffee My Way:
  1. Prepare Brew coffee of your choice
  2. Get 1 Tbsp Butter (I only had salted butter and it was fine)
  3. Make ready 1 Tbsp MCT oil
  4. Get 1 Tbsp Coconut Cream
  5. Prepare Optional:1-2 Shakes or Dash of your favorite spice (ex Cinnamon)
  6. Prepare Optional: 1 packet Stevia
Instructions to make Almost Keto ☕ Coffee My Way:
  1. Pour 2 cups fresh brewed coffee into blender. (Or just below max line in a magic bullet, or Personal Ninja)
  2. Shake in optional spice, Add stevia packet
  3. Add butter, MCT oil, coconut cream
  4. Blend!
  5. It will get frothy and emulsify which makes for a creamy rich warm beverage!
  6. Pour into your favorite mug and enjoy!
