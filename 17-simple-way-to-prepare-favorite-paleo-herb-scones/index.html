The ingredients needed to make Paleo Herb Scones:
  1. Take 2 1/2 cups ground almonds
  2. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  3. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Make ready 2 eggs
  5. Get 1/2 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  6. Get 1/2 tsp dried tarragon
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
Steps to make Paleo Herb Scones:
  1. Combine the almonds, salt and baking soda in a bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the coconut oil, eggs and honey. Then stir in the herbs.
  3. Slowly stir the dry ingredients into the wet.
  4. Bring the dough together in a sticky ball. Then roll gently into a sausage shape in a sheet of baking paper.
  5. Cut the dough into rounds and place the rounds on a tray lined with baking paper.
  6. Bake at 190 C for 15 mins or until golden brown and risen.
