The ingredients needed to make Leek and Cheese Muffins:
  1. Prepare 2 eggs
  2. Prepare 125 ml vegetable oil
  3. Prepare 250 ml semi skimmed milk
  4. Prepare 50 g golden caster sugar
  5. Take 400 g self raising flour
  6. Take 1 tsp salt
  7. Take 1 large leek (finely chopped)
  8. Take 100 g cheddar (grated)
Instructions to make Leek and Cheese Muffins:
  1. Heat oven to 180c fan.
  2. Line a muffin tray with paper cases
  3. Beat the eggs in a large bowl
  4. Add the oil and milk and beat until just combined
  5. Add the sugar and whisk until smooth
  6. Finely chop the leek
  7. Grate the cheese
  8. Add the leek and Cheese to the bowl and mix. The mix should be a smooth consistency- add a dash more milk if not.
  9. Fill the muffin cases 2/3 full and bake for 25-30 minutes
  10. Leave the muffins in the tin to cool for a few minutes and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
