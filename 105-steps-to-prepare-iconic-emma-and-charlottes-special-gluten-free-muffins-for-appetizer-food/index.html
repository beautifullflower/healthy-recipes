The ingredients needed to make Emma and Charlotte’s ‘special’ gluten free muffins:
  1. Take 150 ml coconut milk
  2. Take 2 over ripe bananas
  3. Take 225 g gluten free self raising flour
  4. Take Quarter teaspoon xanthan gum
  5. Make ready Tablespoon baking powder
  6. Prepare 150 g granulated sugar
  7. Prepare 60 ml olive oil
  8. Get Teaspoon vanilla extract
  9. Prepare 100 g chocolate chips
  10. Take 10 pitted dates chopped
Instructions to make Emma and Charlotte’s ‘special’ gluten free muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 200c/gas 6. Line a 12 hole muffin tin with paper liners. Blend bananas and coconut milk until smooth.
  2. Mix flour, xanthan gum, baking powder and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Make a well and pour in banana mixture.
  4. Mix in oil and vanilla essence.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips and dates. The mixture should be thick like a banana loaf.
  6. Divide evenly between the paper liners. (Suggested 6 large muffins or 12 small muffins)
  7. Bake for 15-20 mins.
  8. Place on a wire rack to cool.
