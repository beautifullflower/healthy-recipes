The ingredients needed to make Greek yogurt blueberry muffins:
  1. Make ready 280 g flour
  2. Get 85 g sugar
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp baking powder
  4. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking soda
  6. Take 2 egg yolk
  7. Get 95 g oil
  8. Make ready 200 g plain yogurt
  9. Get 60 g milk
  10. Prepare 200 g blueberries

Instructions to make Greek yogurt blueberry muffins:
  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder and cinnamon in one bowl.
  2. Combine the lightly beaten eggs, oil, yogurt, and milk in another bowl.
  3. Mix the wet and dry ingredients together. Stir until combined and fold in the blueberries. Batter should be of soft dropping consistency.
  4. Spoon batter into muffin cases until almost full. Bake for 17-20 minutes until rise and a skewer comes out clean.
  5. Cool on wire rack and enjoy.
