Steps to make Bread pizza (dhara kitchen recipes):
  1. METHOD : - FOR THE PIZZA SAUCE: - - We will prepare a quick tomato sauce for the bread pizza. You can use ready made pizza sauce or tomato ketchup instead of this sauce for this bread pizza.
  2. Blanch tomatoes,peel the skin and grind it to a puree. Keep all the ingredients ready.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan Add finely chopped garlic and saute for a few minutes. Then add tomato puree.
  4. Add chilli powder, chilli flakes, basil, oregano and salt needed. Cook until the raw flavor of the tomato goes and the sauce thickens.
  5. This is the sauce we are going to use for this bread pizza.
  6. FOR THE PIZZA TOPPINGS: - - Keep all the pizza toppings ingredients  onions, sliced capsicum (red and yellow optional) sliced mushrooms and sweet corn. black  olives, seeds removed etc.
  7. Apply a little olive oil or butter and toast the bread slices slightly.
  8. Spread 1-2 tsp of the prepared sauce or tomato ketchup evenly on the bread slices. For one bread slice alone.
  9. Oregano the toppings and chaat masala, sprinkle grated mozzarella cheese all over the toppings.
  10. Grill in a preheated oven at 180 degree C until the cheese starts to melt.
  11. If you do not have an oven, you can make these bread pizza on tawa also.
  12. Heat a tawa, reduce the heat to low and  place the prepared bread slice with the toppings on the tawa.
