Instructions to make Paleo non-fried ‘fried’ chicken - glutenfree almondchicken:
  1. Mix all spices in a bowl - taste to check if you like the mix. Add extra spices accordingly
  2. Mix spice mix into almond flour, stirring so it’s spread evenly
  3. Mix two eggs in a bowl
  4. Coat chicken in the eggs and then coat fully in spices.
  5. Arrange on a baking paper and cover with aluminium foil
  6. Bake for 20-45 min, depending on the size of the pieces (20 min for chicken nuggets, 45 min for whole thigh or breast). Remove aluminium foil half-way through so the coating gets crisp.
