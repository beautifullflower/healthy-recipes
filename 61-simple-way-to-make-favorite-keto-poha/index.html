The ingredients needed to make Keto Poha:
  1. Make ready 300 gram Cauliflower
  2. Prepare 60 gram Diced Red Onion
  3. Get 30 gram Peanuts
  4. Take 2 Tbsp coconut oil
  5. Take 1 Tsp Mustard Seeds
  6. Take 1 Tsp Turmeric powder
  7. Take 1 Green Chilli
  8. Get 8 Curry Leaves
  9. Get to taste Salt
  10. Make ready 2 tsp Cilantro/Coriander leaves For garnish
  11. Take 3 tsp Lime Juice
  12. Take handful pomegranate seeds (optional)
  13. Make ready 2 tsp olives
Instructions to make Keto Poha:
  1. Start by preparing the cauliflower. Separate it into florets and the stalks. Wash thoroughly.
  2. Shape and cut the stalk into cubes to resemble potatoes and rice the florets in a food processor.
  3. Boil the stalks in salted water for 10 minutes or till tender - Dry roast the peanuts in a frying pan till they get a nice golden brown colour and set aside - In the same pan heat up 2 tbsps coconut oil. - Then add in the mustard seeds and once they start popping add in the curry leaves and the onions.
  4. Season with some salt and turmeric and fry out the spice - Then add in the peanuts and green chillies and cook for a further minute or so. - Add in the cauliflower rice, season with salt, add a splash of water and cover and cook till tender.
  5. Add in the cauliflower rice, season with salt, add a splash of water and cover and cook till tender. Once the cauliflower has cooked add in the fake potatoes and give it all a good mix.
  6. Finish with coriander and a squeeze of lime juice,pomegranate seeds and olives. - Serve hot by garnishing according to your taste.
