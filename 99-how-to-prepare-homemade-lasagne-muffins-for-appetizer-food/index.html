How to Prepare Homemade Lasagne muffins for Appetizer Food

To get started with this recipe, we must prepare a few components. You can cook lasagne muffins using 8 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Lasagne muffins:
  1. Prepare Fresh lasagne sheets (about 3-4 per person)
  2. Make ready 150 g mince
  3. Get Chopped onion, carrots and celery
  4. Prepare Passata sauce (100 ml or so)
  5. Take Olive oil
  6. Get to taste Salt
  7. Get Bechamel sauce (didn't have time to make it, shop bought)
  8. Get Parmesan
Instructions to make Lasagne muffins:
  1. Fry veg gently in oil for 3-4 mins. Add mince and brown. When mince is brown, add passata and cook on low for about 20 mins, add salt to taste. Meanwhile…
  2. Turn on oven to 180. Grease muffin tray with olive oil. Cut lasagne sheets in half. Alternate first 2 to create base.
  3. Add spoonful of mince to each. Add a spoonful of bechamel to each. Add sprinkle of Parmesan to each
  4. Layer up with another layer of everything. For decoration, I cut off a few edges
  5. Bake at 180 for about 25-30 mins :)
