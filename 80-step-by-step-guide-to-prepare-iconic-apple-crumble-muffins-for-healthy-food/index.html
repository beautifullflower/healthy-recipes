The ingredients needed to make Apple Crumble Muffins:
  1. Get Batter
  2. Prepare Half cup of milk
  3. Take 1/3 cup coconut oil (or other vegetable oil)
  4. Get 1 egg
  5. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Make ready 1.25 cups flour
  7. Take Half cup sugar
  8. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
  9. Get Pinch salt
  10. Take 2 apples
  11. Make ready Crumble
  12. Prepare Half cup flour
  13. Take Half cup brown sugar
  14. Prepare 1 tsp cinnamon
  15. Make ready 1/4 cup butter
  16. Prepare Baking
  17. Prepare 10-12 muffin paper cups
  18. Prepare 1 muffin baking tray
Steps to make Apple Crumble Muffins:
  1. Pour the milk into a large bowl. Add the coconut oil, 1 egg and a teaspoon of vanilla extract. Whisk to combine.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the flour (1.25 cups) with the sugar (half cup), 2 tsps baking powder and a pinch of salt. Set aside.
  3. Peel and cut the apples into small pieces (about 1.5 inch). Add the cut apples into the flour mixture and stir well.
  4. Pour over the wet mixture and stir until combined.
  5. To prepare the crumble, put flour (half cup) into a medium-sized bowl. Add brown sugar (half cup) and butter (1/4 cup) and a teaspoon of cinnamon. Mix with your hands until crumbly.
  6. Place muffin paper cups in a muffin baking tray. Scoop the batter into the cups, then sprinkle the crumble on top.
  7. Bake for about 25-30 minutes in 350F/175C. Let the muffins cool before serving.
