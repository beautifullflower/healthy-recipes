The ingredients needed to make Eggplant Masla:
  1. Take 500 gm small eggplant/brinjal/bengan, chopped length wise
  2. Prepare 1 large onion chopped lengthwise, finely
  3. Get 2 green chill,slit length wise
  4. Get 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  5. Make ready 2 medium tomatoes chopped
  6. Prepare 2 dry red chillers (not so hot)
  7. Make ready 1/3 ponch phoran
  8. Prepare 1/4 turmeric powder
  9. Make ready Salt as required
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
  11. Get 1 tbsp cooking oil
Instructions to make Eggplant Masla:
  1. Heat oil in a kadhai. Add dry chillies and ponch phoran. Allow phoran to splutter.
  2. Add chopped garlic and slit green chillier. Saute for 1 minute.
  3. Add chopped onion then. Mix salt and turmeric powder and saute till onions and transparent.
  4. Add chopped eggplant and tomatoes. Mix well. Cover and cook till brinjal get cooked on low heat.
  5. When it's done bring down from stove and mix well the roasted cumin powder. Serve as a main /side dish in lunch /dinner. - Thanks
