The ingredients needed to make Cream Cheese Fettuccine sauce:
  1. Get 8 oz cream cheese
  2. Take 1/2 cup butter
  3. Get 1 1/2 cup milk
  4. Make ready 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
  5. Take 6-8 oz parmesan cheese
  6. Get Ground white pepper to taste
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp basil, optional
Steps to make Cream Cheese Fettuccine sauce:
  1. Melt butter and cream cheese, till smooth
  2. Add next 4 ingredients and simmer for 5 minutes or until thickened
