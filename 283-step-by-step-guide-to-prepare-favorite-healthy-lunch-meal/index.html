The ingredients needed to make Healthy lunch meal:
  1. Get Chicken fillet
  2. Get mustatd paste
  3. Prepare soya sauce
  4. Make ready black pepper powder
  5. Make ready tbp vinegar
  6. Make ready hot sauce
  7. Get Salt
  8. Prepare Flour and egg for coating
  9. Prepare tbp mayonnaise
  10. Make ready tbp ketchup
Steps to make Healthy lunch meal:
  1. Marinate chicken with mustard paste, vinegar, soya sauce, salt, hot sauce and black pepper powder.
  2. Coat fillet in flour and dip in egg amd again coat in flour.
  3. Fry until golden brown.
  4. Prepare sauce by adding mayonnaise and ketchup.
  5. Toast buns and spread mayonnaise mixture. Put fillet and cover with another bun.
  6. Add any seasonal fruit, dates and almond.
