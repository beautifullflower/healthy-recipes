The ingredients needed to make Easy Rotisserie Chicken Stir Fry:
  1. Take Stir fry veggies
  2. Take Mushrooms
  3. Get Chopped up rotisserie chicken lunch meat
  4. Prepare I tsp of olive oil
  5. Take 1 single shot glass full of oj
  6. Prepare 1 cup prepared instant rice
  7. Prepare Fresh ginger root to taste(I use alot)
  8. Prepare Orange zest to taste(I use alot of this too LOL)
  9. Prepare 1 tablespoon diced garlic
Steps to make Easy Rotisserie Chicken Stir Fry:
  1. Sautee mushrooms and garlic and ginger and chicken together in oil
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients and simmer untill all liquid has boiled out
  3. Enjoy!
