The ingredients needed to make Quick Vegan Pizza:
  1. Get 1 jus roll ready pizza kit
  2. Prepare Sun dried tomatoes in oil
  3. Get Dairy free pesto
  4. Get Mushrooms, sliced
  5. Make ready Green olives, halved
  6. Prepare BBQ sauce
Steps to make Quick Vegan Pizza:
  1. Roll out the pizza bases on two trays lined with lightly greased foil.
  2. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the base of each pizza
  3. Shred apart each piece of sun dried tomato and scatter them over the pizza base.
  4. Lightly fry the mushrooms and olives in a little olive oil, then add them to the pizza.
  5. Dot with pesto and/bbq sauce. Bake in the oven at 190 C for 10 minutes.
