The ingredients needed to make Microwave Pizza:
  1. Take 1 pizza base
  2. Take 1 capsicum sliced
  3. Take 1 mushroom sliced
  4. Take 2 spring onion sliced
  5. Prepare 2 tsp sweetcorn kennel
  6. Make ready 2 tsp pizza sauce
  7. Take To taste salt
  8. Make ready 2 pinch dry oregano
  9. Prepare 2 pinch black pepper powder
  10. Take 1 cup grated mozerella cheese
Steps to make Microwave Pizza:
  1. Take a pizza base apply pizza sauce then apply all veggies,sweet corn,sprinkle salt,pepper,then garnish mozerella cheese.
  2. Then bake in microwave for 2 or 3 minutes.
  3. Then cut in 4 slices and serve.
