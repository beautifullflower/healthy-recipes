The ingredients needed to make Pistachios and walnut oatmeal muffins:
  1. Take 1 cup brown sugar
  2. Get 1 1/2 cup oats
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cup wholemeal flour
  4. Get 1 1/4 cup milk
  5. Make ready 2 tablespoon ground flaxseed
  6. Take 2 apples shredded
  7. Get 2 teaspoons baking soda
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon baking powder
  9. Get 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  10. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  11. Get 1/4 cup oil
  12. Get 1 teaspoon cardamom powder or vanilla essence
  13. Take 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  14. Take 1/2 x 2 cups chopped walnuts and pistachios
  15. Get Few strands of saffron

Instructions to make Pistachios and walnut oatmeal muffins:
  1. Preheat your oven to 180C. - In a bowl add milk and vinegar and leave for 10 minutes.
  2. In a bowl combine the flour, oats, brown sugar, ground flaxseed, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt. - Add the milk, oil, cardamom and mix well. Add the shredded apples and the nuts. - With a icecream scoop fill the muffin case with the batter and sprinkle nuts on top of muffins.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes at 180 C and then reduce the temperature to 160 C and bake for another 10 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
