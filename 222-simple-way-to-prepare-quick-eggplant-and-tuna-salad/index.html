The ingredients needed to make Eggplant And Tuna Salad:
  1. Get 1 big eggplant
  2. Get 20 cm spring onion
  3. Take 10 cm cucumber
  4. Make ready 5 cm carrot
  5. Get Chopped green onion
  6. Take 1 tin tuna
  7. Get 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  8. Prepare 1/2 lemon
  9. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  10. Prepare 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  11. Get 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
Instructions to make Eggplant And Tuna Salad:
  1. Cut eggplant half and boil it for 3 minutes.
  2. Shred vegetables and put in cold water for 5 minutes. Chop green onion.
  3. Slice eggplant and put tuna and vegetables on it.
  4. Make lemon soy sauce.
  5. Garnish with green onion. Enjoy üíñ
