The ingredients needed to make Easy Thai chicken and vegetable curry:
  1. Prepare 1 cup chopped leeks
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  3. Get 1/4 cup chopped cauliflower
  4. Take 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup chicken (raw)
  6. Take 1 spoon curry paste
Instructions to make Easy Thai chicken and vegetable curry:
  1. In a pot, add vegetable broth
  2. Once vegetable broth is hot, add veggies and chicken
  3. Add curry paste
  4. Stir until the chicken is tender and veggies are soft
  5. Enjoy!
