The ingredients needed to make Lemon and Lime muffins:
  1. Make ready 400 grams Castor Sugar
  2. Prepare 400 grams Self Raising Flour
  3. Make ready 400 grams Butter or margarine
  4. Prepare 6 medium Eggs
  5. Take Juice and zest of 4 large Limes
  6. Get Juice and zest of 2 large Lemons
  7. Make ready Few drops Yellow Food Colouring
  8. Prepare Few drops Green Food Colouring
Instructions to make Lemon and Lime muffins:
  1. Cream butter and sugar together into a smooth paste. Mix in flour bit by bit, making sure there are no lumps. Divide into two bowls and set to one side.
  2. Beat 3 eggs gently together, adding Lemon juice, zest and yellow colouring, set to one side.
  3. Beat 3 eggs gently together,adding the Lime juice, Lime zest and Green colouring, set to one side.
  4. Add the lemon / yellow to one bowl and mix together until silky smooth and just drops the spoon.
  5. Repeat the procedure with the Lime / green mix to second bowl.
  6. Using two large “ pudding” spoons, add each mix to muffin cases, filling to 3/4 full.
  7. Cook in medium hot, gas mark 5-6, 190-200 Celsius, for about 15 minutes, then turn trays around for a further 5 minutes until golden brown on top.
  8. When cool, i topped mine with buttercream and sprinkles in two colours for each variety..
  9. If you try this, please take a photo for Cookpad!
