The ingredients needed to make Quick Tomato, Tuna and Avocado Salad:
  1. Make ready 1 tomato
  2. Get 1/4 of onion
  3. Get 1 Avocado
  4. Prepare 1 small tin of tuna
  5. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  6. Take Olive oil
  7. Prepare Oregano
Steps to make Quick Tomato, Tuna and Avocado Salad:
  1. Cut the tomato in slices and distribute the slices on the plate
  2. Then cut the onion and add it to the plate
  3. Cut the avocado and it to the plate
  4. Open the tuna tin and add it to the salad
  5. Season with olive oil salt and oregano
