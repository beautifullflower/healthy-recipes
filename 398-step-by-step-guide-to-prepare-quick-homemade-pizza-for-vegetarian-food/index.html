The ingredients needed to make Homemade pizza:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Maida
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp oil
  3. Take As required water
  4. Get As per taste Salt
  5. Take 2 tsp curd
  6. Prepare As per taste Pizza sauce/tomato ketchup
  7. Get 2 tbsp Maida for sauce
  8. Get 1/2 cup milk
  9. Take 1/2 tsp Pepper powder
  10. Get As per taste Chilli flakes
  11. Get 1 tsp Oregano
  12. Get 2 tsp Butter
  13. Take 1 onion
  14. Prepare 1 tomato
  15. Get 1 yellow, green,red capsicum
  16. Make ready 3-4 mushroom
  17. Take 1/2 cup corn
Steps to make Homemade pizza:
  1. For the crust, first In a bowl add flour, oil, salt, curd and knead well and close it with a wet cloth.
  2. For white sauce take a pan add butter, garlic,salt, pepper, oregano and then add 2tbsp maida stir it well, by the way, add milk and after it gets thick off the flame.
  3. Roll the dough with the rolling pin, do not roll the dough thin nor thick.it should be with a medium thickness.
  4. First apply the pizza sauce in the crust,after that add the white sauce all over,then add chopped capsicum,onion,tomato and olives,sprinkle some oregano,chilli flakes.
  5. Now the pizza is ready to bake,bake the pizza for 20 mins in low flame.
  6. Pizza Ready for serve.
