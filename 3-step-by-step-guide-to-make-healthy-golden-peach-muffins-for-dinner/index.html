The ingredients needed to make Golden Peach muffins:
  1. Prepare 4 large peaches (or 1 large tin about 490 g)
  2. Make ready 2 1/2 cup self raising flour
  3. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  4. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  5. Prepare 2/3 cup brown sugar
  6. Get 1 tsp cinnamon
  7. Take 1/2 tsp ginger
  8. Prepare pinch salt
  9. Make ready 1 large egg
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup sunflower (or olive) oil
  11. Get powdered sugar (optional)
Instructions to make Golden Peach muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 180c (400 F), prepare 16 muffin cases. In a large mixing bowl combine all DRY ingredients.
  2. In food processor or blender puree 3 peaches, and cut remaining peach into 1 cm (1/2" pieces) and set aside.
  3. Make a well in the center of flour mix and pour in the peach puree, oil, and egg, combine gently then fold in the chopped peaches.
  4. Fill each muffin cases with batter, and bake for 25 minutes or until pin inserted in the middle comes out clean. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and enjoy. A nice cup of peach tea will compliment this delicious "treat".
