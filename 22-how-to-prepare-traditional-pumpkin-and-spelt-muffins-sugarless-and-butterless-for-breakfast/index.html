The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin and spelt muffins (sugarless and butterless):
  1. Get 350 g drained pumpkin puree (I used thawed puree)
  2. Get 225 g Spelt flour wholemeal/ wholemeal plain flour (I used mix)
  3. Take 2 tsp Baking powder
  4. Prepare 100 g Raisins
  5. Take 70 g Mixed seeds
  6. Get 125 ml olive oil
  7. Get 2 eggs lightly beaten
  8. Prepare 1 tsp mixed spice
  9. Get 1/2 tsp sea salt fine
  10. Get 4 tbspn natural yogurt
  11. Make ready 2 tbspn maple syrup
  12. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
