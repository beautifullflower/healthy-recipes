The ingredients needed to make Veg Soup:
  1. Get 1 tsp olive oil
  2. Take 1/4 chopped cabbage
  3. Take 1/4 chopped carrot
  4. Prepare 1/2 chopped capsicum
  5. Prepare 1/2 tomato
  6. Make ready 1/2 chopped onion
  7. Take 2-3 garlic cloves
  8. Take 1 green chilli
  9. Make ready 1 tsp grated ginger
  10. Get 1 tsp soya sauce
  11. Prepare 1/4 tsp vinegar
  12. Take 1 tsp pasta sauce (optional)
  13. Take to taste salt
  14. Make ready 1 tsp black pepper powder
  15. Prepare 2 cups water
Instructions to make Veg Soup:
  1. In a pan add oil, chilli ginger & garlic. sauté it for a minute.then add all the veggies & cook on high flame for 3-4 mins
  2. Add salt, black pepper powder & all the sauces to it.
  3. Cook it for a minute then add water to it.
  4. Let it boil for around 5-7mins. Ready to serve. enjoy
