The ingredients needed to make Creamy Spinach Soup:
  1. Take 200 gm spinach
  2. Make ready 6-8 garlic cloves
  3. Make ready 1 onion (chopped)
  4. Make ready 2 green chilies
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp pepper seeds
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp white chilli powder
  7. Make ready to taste salt
  8. Make ready 2 tsp butter
  9. Take 1 tbsp Fresh Cream
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp ginger
  12. Take 1 tsp maida/All -purpose flour
Instructions to make Creamy Spinach Soup:
  1. Put 1tsp butter in the pan, add cumin, garlic, ginger, green chilli, black pepper and onion and cook the onion till translucent.
  2. Add spinach and cook for 1mnt, then add water and after a boil turn off the gas
  3. Grind the prepared mixture in a mixer and sieve
  4. Put the butter back in the pan, add the flour and fry the flour slightly
  5. Add the prepared spinach and mix, then add fresh cream, white chili powder and salt and cook for 1 to 2mnt.
  6. Your healthy and keto friendly creamy spinach soup is ready
