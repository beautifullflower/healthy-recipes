The ingredients needed to make Pista Kaju Murg Kabab/ kebab with Cheesy Dip:
  1. Take 750 gm Boneless bite size chicken
  2. Get 1-2 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  3. Get 1 medium sized onion paste
  4. Make ready 75 gm pistachio and cashew paste
  5. Make ready 1 tsp Garam masala powder
  6. Make ready 1-2 tbsp Lemon juice
  7. Get 1 tbsp Rose water
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp Kewra water
  9. Prepare to taste Salt
  10. Make ready as needed Green edible color (optional)
  11. Take For grilling on the skewer
  12. Prepare 3 Skewers (big size)
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup bell peppers
  14. Prepare 1 Onion
  15. Make ready 1 Tomato (optional)
  16. Prepare as needed Olive oil
  17. Get For Dip
  18. Make ready 5-6 Garlic cloves
  19. Make ready 100 gm Cream cheese(room temperature)
  20. Take 50 gm Fresh cream (room temperature)
  21. Make ready 1/4 tsp Crushed Black pepper
  22. Take 3 tsp Lemon juice
  23. Make ready to taste Salt
  24. Prepare For Salad
  25. Take 4 Onion slices
  26. Make ready 3 Tomato slices
  27. Get 3 Lemon slices
  28. Take 3 Cucumber slices
  29. Get 1 tsp Seasoning of your choice
Instructions to make Pista Kaju Murg Kabab/ kebab with Cheesy Dip:
  1. In a bowl add the chicken and all the ingredients and mix well and let it marinate for at least an hour or two. Preferably keep it in the fridge if its summer. After that put it in a kadai add enough water to boil and cook the chicken for half an hour.
  2. In the meantime, soak the bamboo skewer in water if you are using one. Once the meat is cooked, let it cool so that you can handle.then put the chicken pieces, bellpeppers and onion in an alternate manner on the skewers, bast it with olive oil with a brush and grill it on an open gas flame for few minutes turning it so that it gets charred and gets the aroma of grilled kebab (as you get when you directly grill the marinated chicken on a charcoal grill). Enjoy with cheesy dip and salad !
  3. For Cheesey dip : In a mixer jar first make a paste of the garlic, then add the cream cheese, fresh cream, lemon, pepper and blend, taste salt, if required add and blend again.your cheesy dip is ready to relish. Bon Appetite!
