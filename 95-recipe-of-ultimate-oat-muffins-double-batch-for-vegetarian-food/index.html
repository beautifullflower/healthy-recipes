Steps to make Oat Muffins (double batch):
  1. Mix dry ingredients together
  2. Mix wet ingredients together
  3. Make a whole in dry ingredients, and pour in wet ingredients
  4. Mix wet and dry ingredients until just combined - about 15 times. Ensure it's still lumpy
  5. Preheat oven to gas mark 6
  6. Put mixture into muffin cases
  7. Put muffin in oven for 15 minutes a tray (don't put both trays in at once, the bottom try won't rise well)
  8. Muffins are done when theygolden brown, and no batter comes out when you put a fork into a muffin
