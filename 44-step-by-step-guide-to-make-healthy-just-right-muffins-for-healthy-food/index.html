The ingredients needed to make Just Right Muffins:
  1. Make ready 1 cup Just Right cereal
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup smooth peanut butter
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup caster sugar
  4. Prepare 2 eggs
  5. Get 1 cup grated carrot
  6. Take 1 cup self-raising flour
  7. Get 1/2 cup milk
  8. Take 1 tspn cinnamon
Instructions to make Just Right Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 180¬∞C/160¬∞C fan-forced. Grease a 12-hole, 1/3 cup-capacity muffin pan.
  2. Combine all dry ingredients in a large bowl including grated carrot.
  3. Add peanut butter, eggs, milk in the centre of the mixture and slowly stir until the mixture is combined.
  4. Spoon mixture into prepared muffin pan.
  5. Place in the oven and cook for 25 minutes or until a skewer inserted in the centre of the muffin comes out clean.
