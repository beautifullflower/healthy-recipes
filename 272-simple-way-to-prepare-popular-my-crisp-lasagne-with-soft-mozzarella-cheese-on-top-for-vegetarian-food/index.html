The ingredients needed to make My Crisp Lasagne with soft Mozzarella Cheese on top. 😀:
  1. Make ready Lasagne…
  2. Prepare 1 tbls Olive oil
  3. Make ready 1/4 of a medium sized onion diced
  4. Prepare 2 Cloves Garlic crushed
  5. Prepare 1 handful small mushrooms sliced thin
  6. Get 250 g Minced Beef
  7. Get 1/2 Tsp pepper
  8. Make ready 1/2 length of Courgette diced
  9. Make ready 1 level tsp mixed Italian herbs
  10. Get 1/2 Green bell pepper diced
  11. Get Pinch Salt (opt)
  12. Prepare 1 Can crushed Tomatoes
  13. Get 1/4 Cup red Wine
  14. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  15. Prepare 1 dried chicken or veg stock crushed
  16. Prepare 1 Cup boiling water
  17. Make ready 1 tbls Tomato Purèe
  18. Prepare slices Mozzarella soft cheese cut in
  19. Take Sauce..
  20. Prepare 1/2 pint milk
  21. Make ready 1 level tbls Butter
  22. Get 1 tbls Flour
Instructions to make My Crisp Lasagne with soft Mozzarella Cheese on top. 😀:
  1. Heat up the pan add the olive oil add the onions fry for 1 minute then add the Garlic and fry for a few secounds add mushrooms.
  2. Next add the minced beef cut and seperate the beef and fry until all coloured. Then add the canned tomatoes
  3. Next the stock add boiling water straight away to mix the stock. Also add the black pepper. Let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the tomato pureè and italian herbs also the diced courgette.
  5. Next the bell pepper and stir in. Then the bay leaf mix all in.
  6. Add the wine let it boil for 1 minute then turn down and simmer for 5 minutes. Meanwhile make the white sauce.
  7. Add the butter to a saucepan and Milk then stir in the flour and keep stirring until it boils then simmer for 1 minute stirring it should thicken a little not too much.add the black pepper.turn heat off and set aside.
  8. The oven dish i am using is a little longer than a lasagne sheet and a llttle wider. Just break off some bits to fit. Line the bottom with 2 ladels of the beef mix make sure its more liquidy. Add the lasagne sheet and bits around to fit.
  9. Next add more of the mix on top of the sheet and again lasagne sheet. 1 more ladel of meat mix and then add the white sauce
  10. Add all the sauce then another layer of lasagne sheet. Then all the meat mix.
  11. On top of the meat mix add the mozzarella slices and sprinkle some dried parsley on top. And add to the preheated oven 180°C for 15-20 minutes. I have no pic of yhe mozzarella on top as i forgot and put it in the oven.
  12. When its done iy looks like this. I like mine with a crisp on top. The mozzarella melts in. This version is really lovely. I leave it to go cold then it can come out in a block. Easy to cut and warm in microwave when your ready to Serve.
