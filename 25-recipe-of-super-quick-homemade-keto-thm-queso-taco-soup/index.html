The ingredients needed to make Keto THM Queso Taco Soup:
  1. Prepare 2 TBS Olive Oil
  2. Make ready 1 large onion
  3. Get 2 Stalks Celery
  4. Prepare 1 Green Bell Pepper (4 bumps on bottom = sweet)
  5. Take 2 TBS Minced Garlic
  6. Make ready 1 Can (15 oz.) Sweet Corn (drained)
  7. Prepare 1 Can (10 oz.) Rotel Tomatoes
  8. Get 1 Can Diced Tomatoes
  9. Get 1 Can (14.5 oz.) Black Beans (I drain and rinse mine)
  10. Get 2 Lbs Ground Meat
  11. Take 2 TBS Taco Seasoning
  12. Take Salt and Pepper to taste
  13. Get 8 oz Queso Cheese
  14. Prepare 1 Cup Heavy Cream
  15. Get 4 Chicken Bouillon Cubes
  16. Take 2 Beef Bouillon Cubes
  17. Make ready 4-6 Cups hot water
Instructions to make Keto THM Queso Taco Soup:
  1. Saute Onion, Bell Pepper, Celery, and Garlic. Add Ground Meat. And seasoning
  2. Dissolve Bouillon in hot water add in. Add canned ingredients. Bring to a low boil.
  3. Cut cheese into cubes add to boiling mixture and add heavy cream.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer to allow flavors to marry. Garnish with shredded cheese and low carb tortilla chips and a dollop of sour cream. Enjoyn
