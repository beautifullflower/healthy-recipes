The ingredients needed to make Banana oat muffins:
  1. Prepare 6 brown bananas
  2. Prepare 6 tablespoons runny organic honey
  3. Take 500 g organic oats
  4. Make ready 250 g self-raising flour
  5. Prepare 4 egg whites
  6. Prepare Sugar free chocolate syrup for decoration
  7. Prepare Banana chips for decoration
Steps to make Banana oat muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees
  2. Mash 6 bananas in a large bowl üçå
  3. Add the runny honey
  4. Add the egg whites and stir well
  5. Pour in the oats and mix until sticky
  6. Add the flour and stir very well
  7. Cook for 20 minutes and allow to cool
  8. Decorate with sugar free chocolate and banana chips
