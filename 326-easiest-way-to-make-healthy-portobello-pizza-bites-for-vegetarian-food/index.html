The ingredients needed to make Portobello Pizza Bites:
  1. Make ready Large portobello mushrooms, stems removed
  2. Get Your favorite tomato sauce
  3. Prepare Shredded mozzarella
  4. Make ready Leftover meatballs (optional)
Instructions to make Portobello Pizza Bites:
  1. Preheat oven to 375F.
  2. Wash or wipe down your mushrooms. Remove the stems, chop them up, and add to your omelette in the morning.
  3. Toss mushroom caps with a few gluggs of olive oil, salt & pepper to taste, spread out on a lightly greased baking sheet lined with foil.
  4. Put a spoonful of tomato sauce in each. Here, you can get creative! I put spaghetti sauce and leftover meatballs.
  5. Bake for 15-20 mins.
  6. Take out. Top with mozzarella cheese. Bake for another 5 mins or until cheese has browned on the top. Enjoy!!
