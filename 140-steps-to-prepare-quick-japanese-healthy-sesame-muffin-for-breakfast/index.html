To get started with this particular recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can cook japanese healthy sesame muffin♪ using 7 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Japanese healthy sesame muffin♪:
  1. Take 150 g Pancake mix
  2. Get 1 egg
  3. Make ready 50 g flour
  4. Take 50 g melted butter
  5. Make ready 200 cc soy milik
  6. Get 50 g brown sugar (or 45g honey if you have)
  7. Make ready 30 g black sesame
Instructions to make Japanese healthy sesame muffin♪:
  1. Mix well with egg, soy milk, sugar.
  2. Add pancake mix, flour, butter, sesame, and mix well.
  3. Pour in your favorite cups. Bake at 180℃ for 25 mins-30 mins. Tips: I recommend you to pour about six-tenths of the cups. I wish I had more cups!!
  4. Done!
