  7. Make ready 250 ml milk
  8. Make ready 150 ml oil

Steps to make My jam filled vanilla muffins:

  4. Add the wet to the dry and mix to combine dont over mix it

  6. Bake for 20 -25 mins until golden and allow to cool slightly…enjoy
