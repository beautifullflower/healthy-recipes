The ingredients needed to make Salted Crackers Mini Pizza Bites:
  1. Prepare 2 packet Salted Crackers
  2. Prepare 1 cup Mozzarella Cheese shredded
  3. Take 2 tbsp Red Chilli flakes
  4. Take 2 tbsp Italian Seasoning or Oregano
  5. Make ready 1 cup Sweet Corn
  6. Prepare 1 cup Onions chopped
  7. Prepare 1 cup Capsicum chopped
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup Mushrooms thinly sliced
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup Pizza sauce or Ketchup
Instructions to make Salted Crackers Mini Pizza Bites:
  1. You can use any brand of Crackers available. I have used a rectangular variety for the best results.
  2. Spread the sauce or ketchup on all the crackers. Arrange them on a baking tray lined with a parchment paper. Top with your favourite veggies followed by cheese.
  3. Bake in a preheated oven on 180° for about 10-12 minutes. That's it. Nothing more. Nothing less. Treat your families to these single bite Cracker Pizzas on any given day. Enjoy!
