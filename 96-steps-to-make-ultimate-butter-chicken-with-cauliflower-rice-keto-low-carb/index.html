The ingredients needed to make Butter Chicken With Cauliflower Rice (Keto/Low Carb):
  1. Get 1 tbs Garlic Powder
  2. Make ready 1 tbs onion powder
  3. Prepare 4 chicken thighs
  4. Get 1 cup water
  5. Take 2 tbs Avacodo Oil
  6. Prepare 1/8 tsp black pepper
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp curry powder
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp turmeric
  9. Get 1/4 tsp cumin
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp ginger
  11. Take 1/2 tsp Chili powder
  12. Take 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  13. Take 100 g salted butter
  14. Get 100 g cream cheese
  15. Prepare 1/2 cup Hienz No Added Sugar Ketchup
  16. Prepare 2 packets stevia
  17. Prepare 800 g Cauliflower rice
Instructions to make Butter Chicken With Cauliflower Rice (Keto/Low Carb):
  1. Dice chicken in small cubes, in frying pan with oil lightly brown chicken.
  2. Add water, onion powder, garlic powder and pepper simmer for 5-10 mins on low- med heat until chicken is cooked.
  3. Add remaining spices. Stir throughly. Recipe is light on spicy heat add more cayenne pepper if you like spicy.
  4. Add butter, cream cheese, sugar free ketchup and stevia. Keep on low heat and stir in until fully blended. Let simmer for 5-10 mins to thicken sauce (stir occasionally)
  5. Cooks Cauliflower rice and serve.
