The ingredients needed to make Keto cauliflower poha:
  1. Get 300 gms Cauliflower rice- one medium head of cauliflower
  2. Get 3 tbsp Peanuts- skinless, around
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp Ghee-
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp Mustard seeds-
  5. Get 1 Dried red chilli-
  6. Prepare 1 large sprig Curry leaves
  7. Get 1 Onion medium size, finely diced
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Ginger- (optional)
  9. Take 1 Green chilli- small
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  11. Prepare to taste Salt

Steps to make Keto cauliflower poha:
  1. Slice the cauliflower into big chunks and grater to mince them to resemble fine rice or couscous. In a large frying pan, dry roast the peanuts to a golden brown. Keep aside.
  2. Into the same frying pan, melt the ghee and once hot throw in the mustard seeds. Once they crackle, add the dried red chilli and curry leaves and saute for a few seconds. Add the onions, ginger, green chilli and turmeric, season with some salt and cook for a few minutes, till the onions become soft.
  3. Tip in the peanuts and give a good mix. Finally, add the minced cauliflower, and mix it all together. Season with salt, add a dash of water, and cover and cook on medium heat for about 5 to 6 minutes.
  4. Keep checking in between and give a mix so that it doesn't stick to the pan. Once the cauliflower is cooked, remove from heat and serve hot.
