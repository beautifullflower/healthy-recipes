Simple Way to Make Popular Pizza for Healthy Food

To get started with this particular recipe, we must prepare a few components. You can have pizza using 9 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you cook it.

The ingredients needed to make Pizza:
  1. Prepare 3 pizza base
  2. Get 1 cup pizza sauce
  3. Get 1 cup sauteed button mushrooms
  4. Take 1/2 cup capsicum (bell pepper), sliced or cubed
  5. Make ready as needed Grated mozzarella cheese or pizza cheese
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon olive oil
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Red chilli flakes
  8. Get 1 tsp oregano
  9. Get 1/2 cup cream cheese
Steps to make Pizza:
  1. Roast top side of base on heated tave until its brown and crispy.
  2. Brush some olive oil on the pizza base. Spread the pizza sauce and cream cheese on the roasted side.
  3. Top shredded mozzarella cheese, sauted mushroom and veggies. - Sprinkle some oregano and red chilli flakes.
  4. Cover it and cook it until the base becomes golden brown and the cheese on top melts.
  5. Wait for a minute or two and serve veggie pizza while still hot.
