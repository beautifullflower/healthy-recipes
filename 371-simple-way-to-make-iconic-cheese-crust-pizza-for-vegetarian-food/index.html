The ingredients needed to make Cheese crust pizza:
  1. Take For Cheese crust
  2. Prepare 1 cup Cheddar cheese
  3. Make ready 1 cup Mozeralla cheese
  4. Prepare 1 Egg
  5. Get Salt pinch of
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp Garlic powder
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp Black pepper
  8. Get 1 tbsp Olive oil
  9. Prepare For topping
  10. Take Chicken cubes(any flavour of your choice, I used tikka cubes)
  11. Make ready Black olives
  12. Take Yellow and red bell peppers
  13. Prepare Mushrooms
  14. Make ready Baby tomatoes
  15. Take Some more cheese
Steps to make Cheese crust pizza:
  1. For the crust dough:In a bo2l add all the ingredients and mix well
  2. In a baking dish place parchment pepper add the dough and spread in a round shape
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes.
  4. Remove from the oven, cool down at room temperature then add the toppings and cheese on top
  5. And again bake for 10 minutes amd serve
