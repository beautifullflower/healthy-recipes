The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Banana Protein Muffins (with Coconut Flour):
  1. Make ready 70 g organic coconut flour (The Groovy Food Company)
  2. Get 3 scoops vegan chocolate protein shake powder (Arbonne)
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. Get 1/4 tsp baking soda
  5. Take 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  6. Get 3 ripe bananas (mashed)
  7. Get 1 cup coconut almond milk (Alpro)
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Get 3 free-range medium sized eggs (beaten)
  10. Make ready 50 g cold pressed virgin coconut oil (+ to grease muffin tin)
Steps to make Chocolate Banana Protein Muffins (with Coconut Flour):
  1. Add all dry ingredients into a bowl (coconut flour, chocolate protein powder, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon powder) ready to be transferred into a food processor. Blend thoroughly.
  2. Beat eggs. Add all wet ingredients (eggs, coconut oil, vanilla extract, coconut & almond milk) into the food processor.
  3. Preheat & bake for 30-35 minutes on 180C. Here's the before and after shot. Enjoy!
