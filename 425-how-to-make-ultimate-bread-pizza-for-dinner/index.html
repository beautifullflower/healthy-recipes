The ingredients needed to make Bread Pizza:
  1. Take 6 slices whole wheat bread
  2. Take 1/2 cup mushrooms chopped
  3. Prepare 1 cup capsicum chopped
  4. Take 1 small tomato chopped
  5. Get 1 onion chopped
  6. Get 1/4 cup olive
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp black pepper
  8. Prepare to taste Salt
  9. Prepare As required Pizza sauce
  10. Make ready As required Pizza cheese
  11. Make ready 1 tsp Oregano
Instructions to make Bread Pizza:
  1. Take a bowl. Add the vegetable but for extra flavour you can use saut√© the vegetables also.
  2. Apply pizza pasta sauce over the bread.
  3. Arrange the toppings- spread vegetable mixture over the pizza sauce, black pepper, salt, grated mozzarella cheese, oregano or you can use italian seasoning also and lastly sprinkle some chilli flakes to make it spicy.
  4. Repeat the same process for other bread slices.
  5. Take a non- stick pan and slightly grease the pan with butter and put the bread pizza on tawa.
  6. Cover with the lid and cook on medium low flame till the cheese melts and bread become crisp. It takes around 2-3 minutes to get roasted. Uncovered the lid and take out the bread Pizza in a serving plate.
  7. Cut into wedges and serve hot.
