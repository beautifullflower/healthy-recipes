The ingredients needed to make Tofu & Egg Cheese Bake:
  1. Get 450 g Firm Tofu *I used Aldi’s ‘Organic Firm Tofu’
  2. Prepare 2-3 Eggs
  3. Get 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  4. Make ready Note: Reduce amount if you use salty toppings such as Olives or Anchovy
  5. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon Pepper
  6. Get 1 tablespoons Olive Oil
  7. Prepare 1 cup Shredded Tasty Cheese OR Pizza Cheese
  8. Make ready <Toppings>
  9. Prepare You can used whatever you like!
  10. Take This time I used 100g ‘Kransky’ Sausage, 1 Jalapeño (‘Oh… it was hot!’), 1/4 cup Semi-dried Tomatoes and 2 Mushrooms
Instructions to make Tofu & Egg Cheese Bake:
  1. Preheat the oven to 200C.
  2. Break down and mash Firm Tofu in a bowl, add Eggs, Salt & Pepper, and make it into coarse paste texture.
  3. Grease inside a baking dish (I used a pie dish) with Olive Oil and spoon the tofu mixture in the dish. Spread evenly and press down using the spoon.
  4. Top with the sliced ingredients of your choice, arrange them evenly on the tofu mixture and cover with Cheese.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden.
