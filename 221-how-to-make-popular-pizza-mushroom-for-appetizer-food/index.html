The ingredients needed to make Pizza mushroom:
  1. Prepare 400 g mushroom (large)
  2. Make ready 1/4 tbsp garlic powder
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp pizza spices
  4. Prepare 1 tsp tomato paste
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp ketshup
  6. Make ready 1 tsp mozerella cheese (grinded)
Steps to make Pizza mushroom:
  1. With a clean piece of cloth wipe the mushrooma
  2. Peel mushrooms' outer layer
  3. Remove mushrooms' roots, dice them finely and add to the pizza sauce
  4. Mix all the above mentioned ingredients well together
  5. Fill mushrooms with sauce, spread some mozerella cheese on top
  6. Enter preheated oven at 200 degrees C, for about 20 minutes or untill reached desired doneness
