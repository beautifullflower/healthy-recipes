The ingredients needed to make Keto with pumpkin:

  2. Get 2 green chillies
  3. Prepare 2 teaspoon oil
  4. Get 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup yellow pumpkin
  6. Get 1/4 cup coconut grated
  7. Get 1/4 cup coconut milk
  8. Get to taste salt
