The ingredients needed to make Herbed Mushroom Mini Pizza Appetizers:
  1. Prepare 2 flatbread pitas (white or wheat) cut into 8 pieces
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp granulated garlic
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup sauteed teriyaki mushrooms
  5. Prepare 3 chopped green onions
  6. Take 1/4 cup shredded mozzerella cheese
  7. Make ready 2 sprigs fresh dill, finely chopped
Steps to make Herbed Mushroom Mini Pizza Appetizers:
  1. Preheat oven to a low broil.
  2. Spread olive oil and granulated garlic over cut up pita bread pieces.
  3. Add chopped green onions over pita bread.
  4. Add sauteed mushrooms over green onions.
  5. Spread shredded mozzarella cheese over top of mushrooms and green onions.
  6. Sprinkle tops of pita bread with chopped fresh dill.
  7. Broil for 8 minutes until cheese is melted and golden brown. Enjoy with marinara sauce or as is.
