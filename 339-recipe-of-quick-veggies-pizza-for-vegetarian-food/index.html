The ingredients needed to make Veggies pizza:
  1. Get 1 Pizza base
  2. Get 1 Onion
  3. Prepare 3-4 Tomatoes
  4. Take 1 Capsicum
  5. Take 1 cup Cheese grated
  6. Take 1 tsp Salt
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Red chilli flakes
  8. Take 1 tsp Mix herbs
  9. Take 1 cup Mushroom
  10. Prepare 1 cup Pizza sauce
Steps to make Veggies pizza:
  1. Take all the veggies and chopped well.
  2. Take a pizza base. Apply chutney on it. Add onion, capsicum, mushroom, tomato on it. Add all spices on it.
  3. Add mozzarella cheese on it. Bake for 15 mins. Pizza is ready to serve. Eat well.
