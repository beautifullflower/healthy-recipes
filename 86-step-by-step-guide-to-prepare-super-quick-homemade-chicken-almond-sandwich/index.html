The ingredients needed to make Chicken Almond sandwich:
  1. Make ready 2 almond bread slice
  2. Prepare Few boneless chicken pieces
  3. Prepare 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  4. Make ready Pinch salt
  5. Take 1 tsp red chilli flakes
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp soya sauce
  7. Take 2 tbsp chilli garlic sauce
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp mayonnaise
Instructions to make Chicken Almond sandwich:
  1. Almond bread ko saikh lain
  2. Boneless chicken mein salt red chilli flakes soya sauce ginger garlic paste daal k paka lain
  3. Bread mein chicken rakhain uper mayonnaise and chili sauce lagain
  4. Uper dosra slice rakh k serve karain
