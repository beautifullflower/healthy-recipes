The ingredients needed to make Omelette Waffle:
  1. Get Cheesy Rosemary Omelette Waffle
  2. Get 1 tbsp fresh rosemary or any herbs
  3. Take 3 tbsp cheddar cheese or any cheese
  4. Get 1 dash salt and pepper
  5. Get Dressing Optional
  6. Take 2 tbsp French salad dressing or any dressing
  7. Take 1 dash fine chili pepper optional
  8. Get 1 waffle machine
  9. Take 4 egg
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp oil or butter
Instructions to make Omelette Waffle:
  1. Apply a layer of oil or butter on the waffle pan then warm up the waffle machine first.
  2. Season eggs then beat together with cheese and rosemary.
  3. Scoop enough eggs onto waffle machine and bake it till lightly brown and cook about 10 to 12 minute depending on the waffle machine that you use (time may be different).
  4. Top omelette waffle with French salad dressing or Heinz chili sauce or spicy butter.
