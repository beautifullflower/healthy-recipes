To get started with this recipe, we must prepare a few components. You can have breakfast pita pizza using 8 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you can achieve that.

The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Pita Pizza:
  1. Make ready Pita
  2. Get Egg
  3. Make ready Mushroom
  4. Make ready Spinach
  5. Take Chicken sausage
  6. Prepare Tomato paste
  7. Prepare Salt and pepper
  8. Prepare Cheese
Instructions to make Breakfast Pita Pizza:
  1. Spread the tomato paste on the pita. Lay your diced vegetables on it
  2. Fry your sausage in a pan and put them on the pita. Put cheese on it.
  3. Bake for 10 min on 200C upper fan on only
  4. Top up egg of your choice. I made a poached egg with it and it’s delish!
