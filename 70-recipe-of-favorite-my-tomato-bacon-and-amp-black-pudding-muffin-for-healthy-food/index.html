The ingredients needed to make My Tomato Bacon & Black Pudding Muffin 🙄:
  1. Get 1 Muffin cut across the middle and Toasted
  2. Prepare 1 slice Beef Tomato
  3. Prepare 1 Slice Bacon
  4. Get 1 slice Black pudding
  5. Make ready 1 tbls Hollandaise Sauce
Steps to make My Tomato Bacon & Black Pudding Muffin 🙄:
  1. Toast the two halves of the Muffin. Then add the Bacon and black pudding under the grill.
  2. Butter the muffins then slice a thick slice of A Beef Tomato add it to the bottom bun. Next the slice of bacon fold in half. Then the black pudding on top.
  3. Add a little salt and pepper then the Hollandaise Sauce then add the top muffin.
