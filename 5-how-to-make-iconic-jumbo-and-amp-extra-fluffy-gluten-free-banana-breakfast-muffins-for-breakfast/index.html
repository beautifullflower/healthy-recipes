The ingredients needed to make Jumbo & Extra Fluffy Gluten-Free Banana Breakfast Muffins:
  1. Prepare 1 1/4 cups buckwheat flour
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup coconut flour
  3. Get 1 tsp ground cinnamon (can be heaping!)
  4. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  5. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  6. Get 1/2 tsp xanthan gum
  7. Take 1/4 tsp sea salt
  8. Take 1/4 cup coconut sugar
  9. Take 1 1/3 cups mashed bananas (3 large ripe bananas)
  10. Take 1/4 cup coconut oil
  11. Take 2 eggs
  12. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla
  13. Get 1 small handful of oats

Steps to make Jumbo & Extra Fluffy Gluten-Free Banana Breakfast Muffins:
  1. Mix the dry ingredients together (buckwheat flour, coconut flour, ground cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, xanthan gum, sea salt, coconut sugar).
  2. In a separate bowl, mash the bananas with a fork until relatively smooth with few lumps. Add in the vanilla and coconut oil. Mix well.
  3. Whisk eggs in another bowl and add whisked eggs to the banana mixture. Mix together well. Then add mixed egg + banana mixture to the dry ingredients. Use a silicone spatula to mix and fold ingredients. The mixture should feel soft and fluffy at this point.
  4. Grease a silicone muffin tray with coconut oil. Fill mixture nearly to the top but leave a tiny bit of room (because these bad boys will expand enormously)! Sprinkle with oats.
  5. Bake at 180¬∞C for 30 minutes. Let cool and serve!
