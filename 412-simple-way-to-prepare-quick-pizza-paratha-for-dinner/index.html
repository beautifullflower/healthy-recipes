The ingredients needed to make Pizza paratha:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Kneaded dough of wheat flour
  2. Take 1 spoon Pizza sauce
  3. Get 1/2 cup mix veg(Chopped onions, mushroom, and veggies which you like)
  4. Make ready as needed Butter
  5. Make ready As needed Origano
  6. Get As needed Grated cheese
  7. Get 4-5 Peprica pieces
  8. Prepare 4-5 Olives pieces
Steps to make Pizza paratha:
  1. From dough make a boll,roll it into chapati.
  2. On it spread butter,pizza sauce and then put chopped veggies, peprica,olives on it's half part.
  3. Then on this half part add cheese and origami. Then both parts of it folded and with the help of fog press it and now cook it on low flame on tawa from both the sides. Now it is ready and serve it with tomato sauce.
