The ingredients needed to make Orange Cranberry & Macadamia Muffins:
  1. Make ready 2 cups Self Raising Flour
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Caster Sugar
  3. Take 2 Eggs
  4. Get 100 g Butter *melted
  5. Make ready 1/2-2/3 cup Milk
  6. Make ready Orange Zest of 1 Orange *finely grated
  7. Make ready 1 cup Cranberries
  8. Make ready Macadamia Nuts 2 to 3 for each muffin
Steps to make Orange Cranberry & Macadamia Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 180C. Line a 12-hole muffin tin with cases.
  2. Combine Flour and Sugar in a large bowl. Add Eggs, Butter, Milk and Orange Zest and mix well. Add Cranberries and fold together until well combined.
  3. Spoon the mixture into cases. Place 2 to 3 Macadamia Nuts for each and push them into the mixture.
  4. Bake for about 20 minutes or until golden and cooked through. Leave in tins for 5 minutes before moving onto wire rack to cool.
