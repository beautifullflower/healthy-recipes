The ingredients needed to make Chicken Fajita Pizza:
  1. Get 3/4 cup Water
  2. Prepare 2 (tablespoon) Sugar
  3. Prepare 1 (tablespoon)Instant Yeast
  4. Take 2 cup Flour
  5. Make ready 1/2 (tablespoon)Salt
  6. Take 1-2 (tablespoon)Water
  7. Make ready 2 (tablespoon)Olive Oil
  8. Take 2-3 (tablespoon)Cooking Oil
  9. Get 300 gm Chicken Strips
  10. Prepare 1 (tablespoon) Garlic powder
  11. Make ready 1 (tablespoon)Salt
  12. Get 2 (tablespoon)Red Chilli powder
  13. Get 1 and 1/2 (tablespoon)Crush Red chilli
  14. Get 1 (tablespoon)Oregano
  15. Make ready 1 and 1/2(tablespoon) Leamon Juice
  16. Make ready 1/2 cup Mushrooms Slice
  17. Get 1 Onion sliced Medium
  18. Make ready 1/2 cup Capsicum julieene
  19. Take 1/4 cup Red Bell paper
  20. Prepare 1/4 cup Pizza Sauce
  21. Get 1/2 cup Mozerella Cheese
  22. Take 1 cup Cheeser Cheese
  23. Prepare 8-9 Black Olives
Instructions to make Chicken Fajita Pizza:
  1. Sub say pehlay water may sugar dalein or mix Karen phir us may yeast dalein or mix Kar Kay 5-10 minute Kay liay rest Kay liay rakh dein
  2. Phir ik bowl may flour,salt dalein or mix karein phir yeast daal dein or gound lein,phir us may water dalein or phir olive oil laga Kay 2_3 hour Kay liay cover Kar Kay rakh dein
  3. Phir frypan may oil dalein or phir chicken dalen or us ko pakayen phir us may garlic powder,salt,red chilli powder,crush red chilli,oregano,Leamon juice,daal Kar mix Karen phir us may Mushrooms,onion,capsicum,red bell paper dal Kar 2 minute Kay liay pakayen or flame off Kar dein
  4. Phir hands pay oil lagayen or dough ko nikal lay or us Kay do hisay Kar lein phir us ko beal lein or pizza pan may dalein or upper pizza sauce lagayen or phir Mozerella cheese or cheeser cheese lagayen or upper olives lagayen or 25-30 minute Kay liay bake Kar lein
  5. Or phir serve karein
