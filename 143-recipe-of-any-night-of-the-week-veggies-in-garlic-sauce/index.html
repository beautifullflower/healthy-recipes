The ingredients needed to make Veggies in garlic sauce:
  1. Prepare 50 gm paneer
  2. Take 1 capsicum
  3. Take 1 onion
  4. Take 1/2 carrot
  5. Take 5-6 garlic cloves
  6. Get 1 tsp black pepper powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp pink salt
  8. Prepare 1 tsp soya sauce
  9. Take 1 tsp stir fry sauce (veeba’s)
  10. Prepare 1 tsp olive oil

  2. Then add sauces & onion to it.Cook it little then add carrot and cook on high flame.
  3. Add salt & black pepper to it then add capsicum and cook again.
  4. Add little water n Paneer. cook for 2mins then serve hot.
