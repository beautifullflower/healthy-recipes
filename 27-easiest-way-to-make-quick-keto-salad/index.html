The ingredients needed to make Keto Salad:
  1. Prepare 1 packet strawberry
  2. Take 1 packet blue berries
  3. Make ready 1 avacardo
  4. Prepare 1 apple,
  5. Prepare 2 kiwi
  6. Get 1 dragon fruit,
  7. Make ready 1/2 red bell pepper
  8. Take 1 /2 yellow bell pepper
  9. Get as required Olive oil,
  10. Prepare 1/2 tbsp salt,
  11. Prepare 1/2 tbsp black pepper
  12. Get 1 cup Processed cheese (4-5 cubes),
  13. Take as required mayonnaise
  14. Make ready as required Coriander leavers or parsley leaves
Instructions to make Keto Salad:
  1. Wash the fruits very nicely.Chop them vertically.
  2. Take a pan.Add 1 tbsp olive oil.Add capsicum.Add salt.After 2 mins add avacardo.
  3. Let it cook for 2 mins.Add the rest of the fruits. Stir well.After 5 mins add 1 cup mayonnaise.
  4. Add black pepper. Cover with a lid. let it cook for 5 -6 mins until creamy. Pour it in a bowl.Shred the processed cheese. Microwave for 2 mins.Garnish with coriander leaves. Serve Hot.
