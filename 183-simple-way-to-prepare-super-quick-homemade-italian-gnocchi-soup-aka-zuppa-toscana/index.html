The ingredients needed to make Italian Gnocchi Soup aka Zuppa Toscana:
  1. Make ready 1 pound ground spicy Italian sausage
  2. Take 1 small onion, diced
  3. Make ready 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Make ready 4 cups bone broth (chicken or beef)
  5. Get 1 red medium pepper, diced
  6. Prepare 1 cup chopped kale (frozen works when it’s not in season)
  7. Make ready 1 batch Keto Garlic Gnocchi
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup heavy cream
  9. Make ready sea salt and freshly cracked black pepper
  10. Make ready optional garnish: Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley & crumbled bacon
Instructions to make Italian Gnocchi Soup aka Zuppa Toscana:
  1. Heat a large stockpot or dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add sausage, onion and garlic. Cook over until the sausage is completely browned, stirring occasionally and breaking up the sausage with a spoon. Drain out as much of the excess grease as possible.
  2. Add in the bone broth and diced red peppers to the pot and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low and add the kale and cook for an additional 5 minutes
  4. Add gnocchi & cream and stir to combine.
  5. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
