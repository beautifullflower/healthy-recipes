The ingredients needed to make Probiotic Yogurt Muffins:
  1. Get 280 g self raising flour
  2. Make ready 2 tsp baking powder
  3. Take 120 g sugar
  4. Make ready 140 g Unsalted butter
  5. Make ready 2 Eggs medium
  6. Make ready 20 g Milk
  7. Take 60 g unsweetened greek yogurt
Steps to make Probiotic Yogurt Muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 190C/375F.
  2. Combine the flour, baking powder and sugar in a large bowl.
  3. Combine the eggs,margarine,yogurt, and milk in another bowl beat untill blended.
  4. Add the egg mix to the flour.Use a rubber spatula or metal spoon to fold the mixture until just combined. Do not overmix the mixture. It will not be smooth. Divide the mixture between the 8 muffin cases.
  5. Bake for 20-25minutes, or until a skewer inserted comes out clean.
