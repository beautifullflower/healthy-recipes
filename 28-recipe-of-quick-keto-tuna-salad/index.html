The ingredients needed to make Keto Tuna Salad:
  1. Prepare 2 tblsp diced Cucumber
  2. Make ready 1 diced Green Bell Pepper
  3. Take 2 Boiled Eggs
  4. Prepare 1 sliced Avocado
  5. Take 2 tblsp chopped Yellow Onion
  6. Prepare 2 pouches Tuna in a can
  7. Take 4 tblsp EVOO
  8. Prepare 2 tblsp Red Wine Vinegar
  9. Get 1 tblsp Salt
Instructions to make Keto Tuna Salad:
  1. Mix all ingredients together and place in fridge for 30 minutes before serving.
