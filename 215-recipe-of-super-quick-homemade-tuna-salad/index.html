The ingredients needed to make Tuna Salad:
  1. Get Vegetables
  2. Take Lettuce
  3. Prepare 3 tomatoes
  4. Get Cucumber
  5. Make ready Boiled Sweetcorn
  6. Make ready Boiled Peas
  7. Get 1/4 onion
  8. Make ready Avocado
  9. Take Pepper (red & green)
  10. Take Tuna
  11. Make ready 1 can Tuna
  12. Prepare Mayonnaise
  13. Prepare Salad Cream (optional)
  14. Make ready French Dressing
  15. Prepare Seasoning
  16. Get Salt
  17. Take Black pepper
  18. Prepare Chilli Flakes
  19. Take Oregano
  20. Get Basil
  21. Prepare Lemon Juice
  22. Take Optional
  23. Get Pomegranate (personal favourite - I didn’t have some)
  24. Take Cheese (Cheddar or Mozzarella)
Instructions to make Tuna Salad:
  1. Chop up desired ingredients and mix together in bowl
  2. Boil sweetcorn / peas / brussel sprouts and mix in the vegetables
  3. Make tuna, with or without mayonnaise (I like to put a lot of mayonnaise). Add some salad cream as well and mix in
  4. Integrate tuna in to the salad and add more dressing / salad cream / mayo to taste. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, chilli flakes, oregano and basil and you’re ready to go :)
