The ingredients needed to make Pan Square Pizza:
  1. Take 1 1/4 cups All-purpose Flour or Maida
  2. Prepare 1/2 tsp Salt
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp Oil
  4. Take 1 tsp Yeast
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp Sugar
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup lukewarm Milk
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp Pizza Sauce
  8. Prepare 1 Onion sliced
  9. Take 1 Capsicum sliced
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup Olives sliced
  11. Get 1/4 cup Mushrooms sliced
  12. Prepare 1 tsp Oregano
  13. Take 1 tsp Red Chilli flakes
  14. Prepare 1 cup Mozzarella Cheese shredded
Instructions to make Pan Square Pizza:
  1. Add sugar and yeast to lukewarm milk. Stir a bit. Cover and wait until it's frothy. Add all the dry ingredients to a bowl.
  2. Add this frothy yeast mix to it along with oil and make a smooth dough. Cover and let it rise for half an hour. If you have much time on hand. Wait for an hour before working on it.
  3. After done, punch the dough once to release all the air bubbles. Roll into a square shaped base. This measurement makes for two small pizza base. Let the base get cooked for about 4-5 minutes. After done, spread the sauce. Top with the veggies, red chilli flakes and oregano at first.
  4. And finally top with the cheese. Keep it covered for just about the time in which it gets melted. That's it. As simple as that. Serve it piping hot immediately.
