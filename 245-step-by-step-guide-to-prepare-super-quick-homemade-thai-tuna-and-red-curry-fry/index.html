The ingredients needed to make Thai Tuna and Red Curry Fry:
  1. Prepare 1 tin oil tuna
  2. Take 1 tablespoon red curry paste
  3. Get 1 tablespoon tapioca powder
  4. Get 8 Citrus hystrix
  5. Take 1 egg
  6. Take 5 green beans
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon sugar
Instructions to make Thai Tuna and Red Curry Fry:
  1. Drain oil then squeeze a little.
  2. Cut green beans and citrus hystrix.
  3. Put tuna, red curry paste, tapioca powder and sugar in food processor and mix. Then add egg and green beans, citrus hystrix.
