The ingredients needed to make Spiced Mango Muffins:
  1. Make ready 150 g dairy free spread
  2. Prepare 150 g caster sugar
  3. Make ready 150 g gluten free self raising flour
  4. Make ready 3 eggs
  5. Take 1 mango, peeled and chopped into small pieces
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  7. Get 1/2 teaspoon mixed spice
Steps to make Spiced Mango Muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 oC - Line an 8 hole muffin tin with paper cases
  2. Beat together the dairy free spread and caster sugar until light and fluffy - Beat in the eggs one at a time
  3. Stir in the flour, mango pieces, cinnamon and mixed spice - Divide between the 8 muffin cases
  4. Bake in the oven for 25 minutes - Allow to cool before enjoying
