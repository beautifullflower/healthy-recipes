The ingredients needed to make Oven tray pizza:
  1. Prepare 200 g white flour
  2. Get 1 tsp salt
  3. Get 1 tsp fast action dried yeast
  4. Take 1 tsp caster sugar
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
  6. Make ready warm water
  7. Get 1/2 jar tomato sauce
  8. Make ready 1/2 pack mozzarella
  9. Get 100 g mushrooms
  10. Get 1 pack bacon bits
  11. Make ready 1/2 onion
Steps to make Oven tray pizza:
  1. Put the flour, salt, yeast, sugar and oil in a bowl.
  2. Mix with a spatula, adding small portions of warm water until the mixture sticks together.
  3. Form a ball of dough and knead for around 5 minutes.
  4. Return to the bowl and cover with a dishcloth, leave to stand for at least an hour until the dough has doubled in size.
  5. Turn the oven to 200 degrees (a bit more if it's not a fan oven).
  6. Roll out the dough thinly and place on baking paper on an oven tray.
  7. Add your toppings - pretty much anything goes, here I'm adding tomato sauce, onion, mozzarella, bacon bits and mushrooms.
  8. Cook for 12 minutes in the middle of the oven. Enjoy!
