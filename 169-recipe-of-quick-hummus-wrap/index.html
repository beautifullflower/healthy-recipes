The ingredients needed to make Hummus wrap:
  1. Take 1 vegan grain free tortilla or wrap
  2. Get 1 cup spinach (washed) (organic)
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes (washed) (organic)
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup hummus
  5. Take 1 tablespoon sliced olives
  6. Make ready 2 tablespoons sriracha mayo
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon very finely chopped jalapeños

These loaded veggie hummus wraps are a great way to add tons of veggies to your eating plan. Wrap the tortilla into a burrito shape and fold in the sides. Cut it in the middle and serve. This vegan hummus wrap is quick, simple and, best of all, healthy!
