The ingredients needed to make Maxican pizza omelette:
  1. Get 3 Egg
  2. Make ready 1/4 (tablespoon)salt
  3. Get 1/2 (tablespoon)black pepper
  4. Make ready 1/2 (tablespoon) vinegar
  5. Make ready 1/2 (tablespoon)soya sauce
  6. Take 1/2 (tablespoon)chilli sauce
  7. Get 4 (tablespoon)milk
  8. Get 1/2 cup Green bell paper(chopped)
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup tomato(chopped)
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup onion (chopped)
  11. Take 1 (tablespoon) oil
  12. Take 1 cup leftover chicken
  13. Get 1/2 cup cheese
  14. Make ready 1/4 cup olives
  15. Make ready 1/4 cup Mushrooms (optional)
  16. Take 9-10 slice Capsicum
  17. Take 1/2 (tablespoon)oregano
Instructions to make Maxican pizza omelette:
  1. Sub say pehlay eggs ko beat Kar lein phir us may salt,black pepper,vinegar,soya sauce,chilli sauce,milk,green bell paper,tomato,onion dall Kar mix karen
  2. Phir ik saucepan may oil dalen or mixture ko dal dein phir mixture Kay upper chicken,cheese,olives, Mushrooms,Capsicum or oregano dal dein
  3. Or phir 15-20 minute Kay liay dum laga dein
  4. Or phir serve Karen
