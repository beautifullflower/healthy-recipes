The ingredients needed to make Christmas Muffins:
  1. Take For the muffins:
  2. Make ready 150 g dairy free spread
  3. Make ready 150 g caster sugar
  4. Prepare 3 eggs
  5. Prepare 150 g gluten free self raising flour
  6. Make ready 100 g Christmas mincemeat
  7. Make ready For the topping:
  8. Get 1 tbsp brown sugar
  9. Get 1 tbsp flaked almonds
  10. Make ready 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp icing sugar
Instructions to make Christmas Muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 oC - Line a muffin tin with 8 paper cases
  2. Beat together the caster sugar and dairy free spread until light and fluffy - Whisk in the eggs one at a time
  3. Stir in the gluten free self raising flour and Christmas mincemeat - Divide between the 8 muffin cases
  4. Top a sprinkle of brown sugar, flaked almonds and ground cinnamon - Bake in the oven for 20 minutes
  5. Allow to cool and serve dusted with icing sugar
