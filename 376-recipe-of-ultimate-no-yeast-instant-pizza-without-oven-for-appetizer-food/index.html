The ingredients needed to make No Yeast Instant Pizza  without oven:
  1. Make ready 1 cup whole wheat flour
  2. Take 1/2 cup curd
  3. Take 1 tsp oilve oil
  4. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  5. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  6. Prepare to taste Salt
  7. Prepare For toppings
  8. Make ready 1 zucchini sliced
  9. Take 1 medium sliced capsicums
  10. Take 1/4 cup Mushroom(tined)
  11. Prepare 2 tsp sweet corn
  12. Take 1 cup grated Mozzarella chesse
  13. Prepare 1 tsp Chilli flakes
  14. Get 2 tsp pizza sauce
  15. Take 1 tbsp butter
  16. Prepare 1 tbsp Oregano seasoning
  17. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
  18. Get 3 cherry tomatoes
  19. Prepare 3-4 green olives
  20. Prepare 3-4 black olives
  21. Make ready to taste salt
Instructions to make No Yeast Instant Pizza  without oven:
  1. In a large mixing bowl take all dry ingredients flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and olive oil. Mix well making sure everything is combined well.
  2. Add curd and knead smooth dough. The dough should be like roti dough.
  3. Cover the dough with wet cloth and let it about 15 to 20 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into 3 parts and roll it out thin. Use a fork to prick the pizza base.
  5. Take a kadhai add some salt at the bottom. Place a stand and place a plate on top of the stand. Cover this with a lid and pre heat about 10 minutes.
  6. Now put the pizza base on greased plate and cover with lid. Cook at low to medium flame for 10 minutes.The pizza base shall be cooked.
  7. Spread some butter and now spread pizza sauce on top of the pizza base, Add same mozzarella cheese. veggies of your choice & finally with grated cheese.
  8. Now add veggies of your choice like zucchini, capsicums, corn, mushroom, cherry tomatoes, olive and add some salt,and olive oil,finally with grated cheese.Add some olives, oregano, and chili flakes top of the mozzarella chesse.
  9. Cook this on a tawa. Cook on low flame and cover it so that the cheese melts. Once the cheese melts, remove the lid and let it cook until the base turns as crispy as you like it to be.
  10. Garnish with basil leaves. Cut it into pieces & serve immediately. Enjoy this pizza
