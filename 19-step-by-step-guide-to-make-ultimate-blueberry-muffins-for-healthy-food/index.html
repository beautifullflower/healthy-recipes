The ingredients needed to make Blueberry Muffins:
  1. Get 2 1/4 cups self-raising flour
  2. Make ready 90 g butter, chopped
  3. Make ready 3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  4. Prepare 125 g fresh blueberries
  5. Take 1 cup milk
  6. Prepare 2 eggs, lightly beaten
Steps to make Blueberry Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C/160Â°C fan-forced. Grease a 12-hole, 1/3 cup-capacity muffin pan
  2. Sift flour into a bowl. Using fingertips, rub butter into flour until mixture resembles fine breadcrumbs. Stir in sugar
  3. Make a well in centre of flour mixture. Add blueberries, milk and egg. Gently stir until just combined
  4. Spoon mixture into prepared holes. Bake for 25 minutes or until a skewer inserted in centre of muffin comes out clean
  5. Stand in pan for 5 minutes. Turn out onto a wire rack to cool. Serve.
