The ingredients needed to make Broccoli almond soup:
  1. Prepare 1 broccoli stem and florets both
  2. Make ready 1 onion sliced
  3. Take 5-6 cloves garlic
  4. Take 15 almonds blanched(soak in water overnight and remove skin)
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon butter
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon garlic crushed
  7. Take 1 teaspoon whole wheat flour
  8. Take 1 cup milk
  9. Take 3 cups water
  10. Get as per taste Salt
  11. Prepare 10-15 leaves spinach
  12. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder

Instructions to make Broccoli almond soup:
  1. Arrange the ingredients as shown in pic.
  2. Now in a vessel heat water and then add onion, garlic and broccoli stem. Cover the vessel and let it boil for 5 minutes.
  3. Then add in the broccoli florets in water. Then add in roughly chopped spinach, and almond and let it boil for around 4 minutes.
  4. Now let it cool.
  5. To make white sauce in a pan add in butter, garlic, wheat flour and let it cook till it becomes slight golden. Then add in milk.
  6. After adding milk stir the mixture continuously so that lumps do not form. Cook the sauce till it becomes slight thick. Then add in salt and black pepper powder.
  7. Now take the boiled broccoli in a mixer grinder and grind it. Now broccoli puree is ready.
  8. Then turn on the flame of white sauce and add in the broccoli puree. Mix it well. Add the broccoli stalk in white sauce. Mix it well using spatula.
  9. Now add in salt, black pepper powder. Cook it till it becomes slight thick.
  10. Your broccoli soup is ready to serve. Take a serving bowl and add in some soup and garnish the soup with some almonds and garlic bread.
