The ingredients needed to make Palak and beet idli pizza:
  1. Make ready 1 cup suji / semolina
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup curd
  3. Get 2 tbsp palak puree / spiach puree
  4. Get 2 tbsp beet puree
  5. Get 2 tsp salt
  6. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp pizza sauce
  8. Get 2 cubes process cheese
  9. Take 6-7 cherry tomatoes
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp chopped mushrooms
  11. Take 1 tbsp green capsicum
  12. Take 1 tbsp yellow capsicum
  13. Prepare 1 green chilli
  14. Make ready 3 alopinoes
  15. Take 2 tbsp Crumbled paneer
  16. Take 2 tbsp oregano
  17. Make ready 2 tbsp flakes
  18. Make ready 1 tsp pepper powder
  19. Get as per taste Salt
Steps to make Palak and beet idli pizza:
  1. Take suji and add curd in it mix them
  2. Add salt and water as required for idli battar
  3. Divide in two parts add beet puree in one part and palak puree in second part
  4. Then mix both puree finely in both battar
  5. Grease idli moulds and pour both battar in moulds
  6. And cook idlies in steamer for 20 min. Then cool them and de mould them
  7. Now take all chopped vegetables in a plate
  8. Cut idlies in horizontal
  9. Arrange them in baking tray and apply pizza sauce upon them
  10. And add grated cheese upon sauce
  11. And add vegetables as desire
  12. Top with paneer and sprinkle flakes,oregano,salt and pepper and bake in preheated oven at 180 degree till cheese melt
  13. And serve with sauce
