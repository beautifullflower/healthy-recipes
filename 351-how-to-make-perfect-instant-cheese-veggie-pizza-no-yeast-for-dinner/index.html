The ingredients needed to make Instant Cheese Veggie Pizza (No yeast):
  1. Get 2 Cup refined flour/wheat flour
  2. Take 1 tbsp butter for dough
  3. Get 1 Cup grated cheese
  4. Get 2 bell pepper juvenile
  5. Get 1/2 cup boiled corn kernels
  6. Take 1/2 cup chopped Mushrooms
  7. Take 4 tbsp pizza sauce (homemade)
  8. Make ready as per taste Salt
  9. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
Steps to make Instant Cheese Veggie Pizza (No yeast):
  1. Take 1 cup of hot water add butter to it and mix well. In a mixing bowl take flour add baking powder and salt and knead a soft dough using butter water mixture we made just before. Pre heat the oven on 200C.
  2. Lightly flour dust work area and Now make two equal portions out of it. Roll in the shaped thin 12 inch roti and put it on a non-stick pizza plate. Pierce it with fork.
  3. Cook the vegetables on high flame in a pan, add salt and mix well. Spoon the pizza sauce evenly over the pizza base.
  4. Now add the cooked veggies and generous layer of mozzarella cheese on top of it. And bake the pizza for 15-17 mins or until golden brown. It’s very easy quick and simple but very very tasty.
