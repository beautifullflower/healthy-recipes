The ingredients needed to make My, Oh What an Omelette ğŸ˜:
  1. Get 6 Small eggs or 5 large
  2. Get 1 tbls olive oil
  3. Get 1 cup mushrooms sliced
  4. Take 6 Slices Bacon chopped up
  5. Take 1 tbls chopped onion
  6. Prepare 1/2 of a large Ripe Avacado chopped
  7. Get 3 tbls milk
  8. Get 1/2 Sweet Pepper chopped
  9. Prepare Sliced soft Mozzarella cheese Sliced
  10. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
  11. Make ready 1 tsp Dried Italian herbs
  12. Get 1 large Wrap
Instructions to make My, Oh What an Omelette ğŸ˜:
  1. In a non stick frying pan add the oil and heat then add the bacon and mushrooms. And cook until they are cooked.
  2. In a bowl add all the eggs and whisk for 1 min add the salt pepper and italian herbs
  3. Then add a little milk
  4. When the mushroom and bacon are cooked add the onion avacado.
  5. Then add the egg mix
  6. Then add the sweet peppers
  7. Make sure by giving a little shake that the bottom is not stuck to the pan. Then add the pan under the grill for 3 minutes
  8. Take out and add the mozzarella cheese
  9. Add it back under the grill to melt it
  10. Add the wrap to a serving plate and slide the omelette onto it so it fits
  11. Cut into 4 and serve add a little of your favourite sauce on the side
  12. And enjoy !
