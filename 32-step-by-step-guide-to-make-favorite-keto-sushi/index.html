Step-by-Step Guide to Make Favorite Keto Sushi

To begin with this particular recipe, we have to prepare a few ingredients. You can cook keto sushi using 5 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Keto Sushi:
  1. Get 5 sheets Nori
  2. Get .25 lbs smoked salmon lox
  3. Get 2 oz cream cheese
  4. Get .75 avocado
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Sesame seeds
Steps to make Keto Sushi:
  1. Dampen 2/3 of nori with basting brush
  2. Spread 1/5 of cream cheese thinly along dry portion of nori
  3. Spread salmon and thinly sliced avocado over cream cheese
  4. Roll up sushi
  5. Add small amount of water to moisten nori if needed
  6. Slice roll into 9 pieces
  7. Repeat 4 more times.
  8. Eat. Makes 45 pieces. 16 calories each.
