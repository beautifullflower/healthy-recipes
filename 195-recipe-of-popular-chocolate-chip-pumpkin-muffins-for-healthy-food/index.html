The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip pumpkin muffins:
  1. Take 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Get 1 cup whole wheat flour
  3. Get 3/4 tsp baking soda
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  5. Take 1 cup pumpkin puree
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup Coconut oil, melted
  8. Take 1/4 cup plain yoghurt
  9. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  10. Prepare 2 eggs
  11. Prepare 3/4 cup brown sugar
  12. Take 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  13. Take 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  14. Make ready 1/2 cup chocolate chips
Instructions to make Chocolate chip pumpkin muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 175Â°F fan forced or 350 Fahrenheit.
  2. Line a 12 muffin tray with liners. Set aside
  3. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients.
  4. Add chocolate chips. Reserving some for top of muffins.
  5. In another mixing bowl. Mix together the wet ingredients. Add the brown sugar. Mix well
  6. Stir the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Mix until combined. DO NOT OVERMIX.
  7. Scoop batter I not prepared muffins pan. Put chocolate chips on top of each muffin.
  8. Bake for 18 - 22 minutes or until cake tested comes out clean. L
  9. Let cool in pan then transfer to wire rack. Enjoy warm or cold.
