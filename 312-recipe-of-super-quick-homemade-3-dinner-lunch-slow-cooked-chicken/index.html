The ingredients needed to make 3. (Dinner/Lunch) Slow cooked chicken:
  1. Get Calories 350 / Macros: Protein 35g / Carbs 42g / Fats 6g
  2. Take chicken Chicken breast fillet
  3. Make ready Real Stock Salt reduced Chicken
  4. Make ready Olive oil
  5. Prepare Salt, pepper & Garlic powder
  6. Take Raw Sweetpotato
  7. Take Serve with a side salad/veggies
Instructions to make 3. (Dinner/Lunch) Slow cooked chicken:
  1. Place chicken breasts in a slow cooker and add in chicken stock, salt, pepper, garlic & other spices of your choosing. Let it slow cook on medium to high for 4-5 hours or lower heat for 7-8 hours.
  2. Alternatively you can also cook chicken in the oven or air fryer if you don't wish to use the slow cooker.
  3. Cut up sweet potato into preferred eating pieces and put into oven or air fryer on 180 degrees for 20-30 minutes or until golden brown. Spray a little olive oil on them before placing them into the oven.
