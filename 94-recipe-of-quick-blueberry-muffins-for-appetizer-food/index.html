The ingredients needed to make Blueberry Muffins:
  1. Get 250 Grams Butter
  2. Make ready 500 Grams Plain Flour
  3. Take 500 Grams Castor Sugar
  4. Prepare 250 mls Milk
  5. Make ready 4 Eggs
  6. Prepare 2 Teaspoons Baking Powder
  7. Prepare 500 Grams Blueberries
  8. Take 1 Teaspoons Salt
Steps to make Blueberry Muffins:
  1. Pre- heat oven to 180 degrees, gas mark 4. Grease or line 2 x 12 muffin tins.
  2. Sieve together Salt, Baking Powder and Flour and put to one side.
  3. Cream the butter and sugar together until fluffy. Add the lightly beaten eggs and Milk and beat well. Add flour mix until just combined. Stir in blueberries.
  4. Fill Muffins until about 2/3 full. Bake for 20-30 minutes until golden brown. Enjoy!
