The ingredients needed to make Simple Tuna Sandwich #sandwich:
  1. Take 1 tin tuna in spring water
  2. Make ready Handful fresh coriander
  3. Make ready 1/2 of a onion slices
  4. Make ready to taste Salt
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp red crushed chilli
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp Mayonnaise or burger sauce
Instructions to make Simple Tuna Sandwich #sandwich:
  1. Drain the tuna from tin and then pour in a mixing bowl. Add all the ingredients and mix well.
  2. Use either brown bread or white bread to toast. Spread the mixture of tuna and toast. Enjoy 😉
