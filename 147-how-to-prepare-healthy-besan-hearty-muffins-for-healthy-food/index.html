The ingredients needed to make Besan hearty Muffins:
  1. Take 250 g besan
  2. Make ready 150 g curd
  3. Take 2 table spoon Mint chutney
  4. Get 2 table spoon Red tomato sauce
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon kasuri methi
  6. Get 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  7. Take pinch Ajwain.a
  8. Take Salt as per taste
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking powder
  10. Take 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  11. Prepare 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
Steps to make Besan hearty Muffins:
  1. Make a batter of besan mixed with curd and water
  2. Add all the spices mentioned..
  3. Keep it for sometimes
  4. Add baking powder.stir the batter thoroughly.
  5. Grease the heart shaped silicon moulds…
  6. Add the batter into it.
  7. Put them in the preheated OTG for 15 minutes..
  8. Take out..demould after cooling
  9. On the ventral hollow side of the hearts muffins..fill chutney and serve..
  10. Enjoy this healthy treat of besan
