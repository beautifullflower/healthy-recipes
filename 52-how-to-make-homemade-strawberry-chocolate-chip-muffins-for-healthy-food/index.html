The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Chocolate Chip Muffins:
  1. Take 150 g dairy free spread
  2. Get 150 g caster sugar
  3. Take 3 eggs
  4. Take 150 g self raising gluten free flour
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon xanthan gum
  6. Make ready 10 g strawberries
  7. Take 100 g dairy free chocolate chips
Steps to make Strawberry Chocolate Chip Muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 oC - Beat together the caster sugar and the dairy free spread until light and fluffy - Add the three eggs individually, beating well between each addition - Add in the flour and xanthan gum - Mix until well combined
  2. Stir in the strawberries and chocolate chips - Split the mixture between 6 muffin moulds lined with paper cases
  3. Bake in the centre of the oven for 20 minutes - Remove from the oven and allow to cool
