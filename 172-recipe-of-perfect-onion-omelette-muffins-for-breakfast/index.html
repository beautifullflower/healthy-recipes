The ingredients needed to make Onion omelette muffins:
  1. Prepare 2 eggs
  2. Get 1 large onion, peeled and thinly sliced
  3. Make ready to taste salt, pepper
Steps to make Onion omelette muffins:
  1. Fry sliced onion in a pan until golden brown. Pour into muffin cups (no need to fill cups up, just half way).
  2. Crack an egg top of each cup, sprinkle salt and pepper. Put in oven with 200C and cook for 15-120 minutes.
