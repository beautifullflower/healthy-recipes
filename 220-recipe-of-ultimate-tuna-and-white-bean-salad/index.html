The ingredients needed to make Tuna and White Bean Salad:
  1. Prepare 400 g Cannellini Beans
  2. Take 300 g Albacore Tuna
  3. Prepare 10 Anchovies
  4. Take 1 Red Onion
  5. Make ready 450 g Jar Roasted Red & Yellow Peppers
  6. Make ready 250 g Cherry Tomatoes
  7. Prepare 100 g Black Olives
  8. Prepare 50 g Parsley
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp Garlic Puree
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  11. Get 1 Juice of Lemon
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp Balsamic Vinegar
  13. Prepare #MyCookbook
