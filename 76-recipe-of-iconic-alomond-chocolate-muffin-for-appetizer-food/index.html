The ingredients needed to make Alomond chocolate muffin:
  1. Make ready 200 grams plan flour
  2. Take 125 grams granulated sugar
  3. Prepare 20 grams chocolate powder
  4. Prepare 160 grams flaked almonds (can be replaced with choco chip)
  5. Prepare 125 gram butter
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking powder
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon baking soda
  8. Prepare 2 eegs
  9. Take 50 ml milk
Steps to make Alomond chocolate muffin:
  1. Preheat the oven to 200¬∞c, then prepare muffin molds and muffin paper. - Mix together flour, chocolate powder, baking powder, baking soda, then sift and set aside. - Melt the butter together with milk.
  2. Beat eggs and sugar with a wisk together until the sugar is disloved. - Add mixture of flour and other into a beaten egg and sugar. Add butter and milk, stir until evenly distributed, then add the almonds and stir again evenly.
  3. Pour the mixture into a mold that has been covered with muffin paper about 4 tablespoons per portion. - Oven with a temperature of 200¬∞c. About 20-25 minutes according to each oven.
