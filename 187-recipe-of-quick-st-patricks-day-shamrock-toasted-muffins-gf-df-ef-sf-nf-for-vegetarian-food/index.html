The ingredients needed to make St Patricks Day Shamrock Toasted Muffins, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Make ready 4 English muffins - see my gluten-free vegan recipe
  2. Get 225 g Violife brand cream cheese or see my free-from recipe
  3. Make ready 24 slices cucumber
  4. Make ready 75 g shredded Violife cheddar style cheese (optional)
  5. Prepare 8 thin slices of green bell pepper
  6. Prepare to taste salt
Steps to make St Patricks Day Shamrock Toasted Muffins, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Split the muffins in half and toast them on each side
  2. Mix the cream cheese and shredded cheese together (if using) and spread on each muffin
  3. Place 3 pieces of cucumber on top of each muffin and use the sliced peoper to make the shamrock stalk
  4. Sprinkle a little salt on top and serve
