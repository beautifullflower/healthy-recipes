The ingredients needed to make Tuna and black caviar nigiri sushi:
  1. Take 1 block Fresh Tuna
  2. Take 30 g Black Caviar
  3. Take Chives
  4. Make ready Soya Sauce
  5. Make ready Wasabi
  6. Prepare Black pepper
  7. Take 500 g Sushi Rice
  8. Prepare Sushi Vinegar
  9. Prepare 500 ml Rice vinegar
  10. Prepare 150 g White Sugar
  11. Make ready 100 g Table Salt
  12. Take 1 stick Japanese Kombu

Instructions to make Tuna and black caviar nigiri sushi:

  2. Mix the sushi rice with the sushi vinegar- 500g of rice need 100ml of sushi vinegar.Let it cool down and shape a little rice rectangular shapes.
