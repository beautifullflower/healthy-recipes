The ingredients needed to make Mushroom and onion Pizza:
  1. Take 150 gm All purpose flour-
  2. Prepare 1 tsp Dry yeast powder
  3. Take 2 tsp Clearified Butter
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup onion sliced
  5. Take 1/2 cup mushroom cut in rounds
  6. Make ready 1 cup cheese grated
  7. Make ready to taste mix herbs and salt
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp pizza sauce
Steps to make Mushroom and onion Pizza:
  1. Take 2 cup Lukewarm water add sugar and yeast to it and mix well so that sugar can dissolve properly and let it rest for proffing for 15-20 minutes.
  2. After rest make pizza dough, spread pizza sauce, and topping with veggies.
  3. Sprinkle mix herbs,Add cheese and butter on top. - Bake pizza for 10-12 minutes in a deep vessel till it's cheese melt and it become crisp.
