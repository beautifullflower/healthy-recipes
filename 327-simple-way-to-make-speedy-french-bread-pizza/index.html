The ingredients needed to make French Bread Pizza:
  1. Take gluten Free half baguettes
  2. Prepare chopped tomatoes
  3. Make ready tomatoe puree
  4. Make ready garlic clove minced
  5. Prepare oregano
  6. Take basil
  7. Prepare sugar
  8. Take salt and pepper for seasoning
  9. Prepare dairy free cheese grated
Steps to make French Bread Pizza:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 oC - Cut the baguettes open lengthways and place on a baking sheet - Bake in the oven for 5 minutes - Make the pizza sauce by blending together the chopped tomatoes, tomatoes purée, garlic, oregano, basil and sugar - Season to taste
  2. Spread on the part baked bread - Top with the dairy free cheese - Return to the oven for 5 minutes or until the cheese is melted and golden
