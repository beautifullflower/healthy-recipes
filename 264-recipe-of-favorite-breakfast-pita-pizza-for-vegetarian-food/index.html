To begin with this particular recipe, we must prepare a few ingredients. You can cook breakfast pita pizza using 8 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Pita Pizza:
  1. Prepare Pita
  2. Make ready Egg
  3. Take Mushroom
  4. Get Spinach
  5. Take Chicken sausage
  6. Take Tomato paste
  7. Make ready Salt and pepper
  8. Make ready Cheese
Steps to make Breakfast Pita Pizza:
  1. Spread the tomato paste on the pita. Lay your diced vegetables on it
  2. Fry your sausage in a pan and put them on the pita. Put cheese on it.
  3. Bake for 10 min on 200C upper fan on only
  4. Top up egg of your choice. I made a poached egg with it and it’s delish!
