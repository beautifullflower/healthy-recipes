The ingredients needed to make My Tuna Cheesy salad jacket Potato 😍:
  1. Make ready 1 small tin tuna drained
  2. Make ready 1 inch piece cucumber diced small
  3. Make ready 2 tbls red onion diced
  4. Make ready 1 tbls chopped sweet pepper diced
  5. Make ready 1/4 Tsp cracked black pepper
  6. Take 2 tbls mild Grated chedder cheese
  7. Prepare 2 tbls Philadelphia soft cheese
  8. Take 2 tbls Mayonaise or Sour Cream
Steps to make My Tuna Cheesy salad jacket Potato 😍:
  1. Add the potato to a microwave dish with a fork push holes in the potato a few times all around. Add to the microwave for 4 minutes then turn over pop back for a further 4-5 minutes.
  2. Add the strained tuna in a bowl for mixing. Add diced cucumber. Then mix in philadelphia soft cheese. Mix in.
  3. Mix in the mayo or sour cream and then add the Sweet bell pepper
  4. Next add the red onion also add the cracked black pepper and Salt. Then add the grated cheese mix all in.
  5. Next heat up in the microwave for 3 minutes and serve hot.
  6. Cut the potato across the length but not all the way then cut across the width add butter and sprinkle salt next add the tuna filling. Add cracked black pepper on top.
