The ingredients needed to make Egg muffins:
  1. Get 1/2 pound bulk pork sausage
  2. Prepare 12 large eggs
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup chopped onion
  4. Get 1/4 cup chopped bell pepper
  5. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. Take 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  7. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  8. Get 3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps to make Egg muffins:
  1. Brown the sausage and drain.
  2. Beat the eggs. Add onion, peppers, salt, pepper and garlic powder.
  3. Stir in sausage and cheese. Spoon by 1/3 cupfuls into greased muffin tins.
  4. Bake at 350¬∞F for 20 to 25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  5. Two muffins make a nice serving size.
