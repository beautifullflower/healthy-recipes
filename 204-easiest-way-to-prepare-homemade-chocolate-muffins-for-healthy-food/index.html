The ingredients needed to make Chocolate muffins!:
  1. Make ready "dry" ingredients
  2. Make ready 250 grams flour
  3. Get 175 grams sugar
  4. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp cocoa
  6. Prepare 150 grams chocolate chips
  7. Get "wet" ingredients
  8. Get 250 ml milk
  9. Get 90 ml vegetable oil
  10. Take 1 egg
  11. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
Steps to make Chocolate muffins!:
  1. In two bowls mix "dry" and "wet" ingredients separately.
  2. Then, add "wet" group to the "dry". Mix it well.
  3. Pour the mixture to the muffin molds. *if you like crunchy chocolate, you can put chocolate chips on top. :) Bake it in preheated oven on 200ºC for 20min.
