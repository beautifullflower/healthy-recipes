Steps to make Tuna stir fry:
  1. Cut the tuna into cubes
  2. Cut the veg into ribbons
  3. Heat rapeseed oil in a wok and add the tuna & onions
  4. Steam the carrots and beans for 8 minutes and the broccoli for 5 minutes
  5. Once the tuna is sealed add all the veg and stir fry for a few minutes
  6. Add oyster sauce to coat the ingredients. Add more if you want more of a sauce
  7. Serve with rice or noodles
