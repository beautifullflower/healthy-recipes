The ingredients needed to make Butter-raisins donuts muffins:
  1. Make ready 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Take 1/2 cup cold milk
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup butter
  5. Prepare 3/4 Tsp nutmeg powder
  6. Make ready 1 Tsp baking powder
  7. Take 2 Tbsp raisins and candied orange peel
  8. Get Ingredients for dipping
  9. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  10. Get 1 Tsp cinnamon
  11. Get 150 g butter
Steps to make Butter-raisins donuts muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 190 C. Melt the butter. In a bowl with melted butter, add sugar and nutmeg, mix together.
  3. Then, add milk and baking powder
  5. And at the end mix with flour until combine
  7. Grease inside the muffins pan. Fill muffins molds with the mixture.
  8. Fill muffins molds with the mixture. Place some raisins and candied orange peel in the center of muffin mixture and push inside.
  9. Bake muffins in the oven for 20 minutes. Once they are baked, allow to cool. Now for dipping mix sugar and cinnamon in a bowl.
  10. Melt the butter in separate bowl. Now, dip each muffin into the melted butter and then dip in the sugar/cinnamon mixture and serve them 
