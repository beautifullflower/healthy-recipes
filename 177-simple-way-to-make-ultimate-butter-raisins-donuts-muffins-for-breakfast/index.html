The ingredients needed to make Butter-raisins donuts muffins:
  1. Make ready 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Take 1/2 cup cold milk
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup butter
  5. Prepare 3/4 Tsp nutmeg powder
  6. Make ready 1 Tsp baking powder
  7. Take 2 Tbsp raisins and candied orange peel
  8. Get Ingredients for dipping
  9. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  10. Get 1 Tsp cinnamon
  11. Get 150 g butter
Steps to make Butter-raisins donuts muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 190 C. Melt the butter. In a bowl with melted butter, add sugar and nutmeg, mix together.
