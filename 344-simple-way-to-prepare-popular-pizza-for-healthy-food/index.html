The ingredients needed to make Pizza:
  1. Prepare 1 cup maida
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup curd
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 tbsp baking soda
  5. Get as required Oil
  6. Make ready As needed Water to knead the dough
  7. Get as per taste Salt
  8. Prepare Toppings
  9. Prepare 1 Capsicum
  10. Get 1 Tomatoes
  11. Take 1 Onions
  12. Prepare as per need Mushroom
  13. Take as required Corn kernels
  14. Prepare as needed Olives
  15. Take As needed Paneer
  16. Take As required Cheese
  17. Get as required Mayonnaise
  18. Take As required Schezwan sauce
  19. Get as per taste Tomato ketchup
  20. Prepare as per taste Oregano
  21. Take as per taste Red chilli flakes
  22. Prepare as per taste Mixed herbs
Instructions to make Pizza:
  1. Knead the dough using all the ingredients mentioned above. The dough should be runny. Apply some oil to the dough and cover with a wet cloth and rest for 30 mins
  2. Now after 30 mins take the dough and knead it for again.Now pinch off a small ball sized dough and roll it into a thick chapati.
  3. Spread some schezwan sauce, tomato sauce and mayonnaise on the pizza base.
  4. Add lots of cheese over the base and now place all the veggies like capsicum, tomatoes, onions, olives, mushroom, corn kernels etc over the base. Insert paneer cubes.
  5. Bake the Pizza at the temperature of 475 F or 245 C in oven
