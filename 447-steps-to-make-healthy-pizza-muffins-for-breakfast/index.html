The ingredients needed to make Pizza muffins:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cup refined flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp sugar
  4. Take 1 tsp yeast olive oil
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 cup warm water
  6. Get 1/2 tbsp Dissolve yeast in warm water mixed with sugar
  7. Make ready For pizza stuffing
  8. Prepare 1 chopped onions
  9. Make ready 1/2 capsicum
  10. Get 5-6 mushrooms chopped
  11. Get 2 tbsp pizza sauce
  12. Make ready 5-6 black olives
  13. Get 1/4 cup corn kernels
  14. Prepare as needed Mozzarella cheese
  15. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
Steps to make Pizza muffins:
  1. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm water with sugar. Keep it covered for 5-10 minutes
  2. Once frothed up make a dough with maida and apply oil and keep aside for an hour to double.
  3. After an hour or so punch the air out of the dough.
  4. Take little portion of dough and fill with pizza toppings
  5. Take a pan, add olive oils and sauté onions, capsicum chopped, olives, mushrooms and corn kernels
  6. Add pizza sauce and stir evenly. Fill in the small balls of dough with cheese and make small stuffed balls.
  7. Place each dough ball in muffin tray for second rise so that it takes the shape of muffins nicely
  8. Bake at 180 degrees for 15-20 mins
  9. Take out when done
  10. Apply butter and enjoy
