The ingredients needed to make Keto Omelette Sandwich 🥪 Recipe: LCHF Recipe 💁🏻‍♀️:
  1. Prepare 3-4 tbsp Butter (Salted)
  2. Prepare 1/4 Cup Onion: Finely Chopped
  3. Prepare 1/8 Cup Scallions: Finely Chopped
  4. Prepare 1/2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  5. Get as per taste Salt
  6. Take 2 Eggs
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Black Peppercorns: Freshly Cracked
  8. Get 1 tbsp Carrot: Finely Chopped
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp Capsicum: Finely Chopped
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp Cabbage: Finely Chopped
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp Fresh Milk: Boiled & RT
  12. Prepare 2-3 Cheese Slices
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp Tomato-Chilli Ketchup
  14. Get 1 tsp Red Chilli Flakes
Instructions to make Keto Omelette Sandwich 🥪 Recipe: LCHF Recipe 💁🏻‍♀️:
  1. In a frying pan: Add in the Butter (keep in reserve 1.5 tbsps, for later use)- Add in the Ginger-Garlic Paste, sauté for a minute- then, add in all the chopped veggies, aforementioned, sauté until translucent & reduces a bit
  2. Add in the salt and keep sautéing over the medium flame- When done, add in a tsp of milk & one cheese slice- tearing into bits…mix everything well together until nicely combined
  3. Turn off the flame & allow it to cool down a bit before mixing the same with the egg batter- else, the egg would be cooked if it’s hot enough…
  4. Now, whisk the eggs well enough with some salt, peppers & red chilli flakes- Mix the milk & whisk well again until frothy…
  5. Now, mix into it the cooled down veggies mixture & give it all a really good mix until everything is nicely combined & well incorporated
  6. In the same frying pan: Add in the rest of the reserved butter…heat up over the medium flame, now slowly pour in the entire mixture into it & spread all along the pan- let it cook for about 4-5 mins time over the slow flame
  7. Once done with one side- flip it onto the other & again cook it for another 3-4 mins time…Time to add in the rest of the cheese slices onto one side of the omelette & then, fold it carefully- Flame should be low-medium all along now
  8. Once folded properly: Flip onto its other side, carefully & gently & then, Divide the same into 2 halves….Dish out…ENJOY 😋 being the HEALTH CONSCIOUS 💁🏻‍♀️
