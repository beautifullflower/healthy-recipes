The ingredients needed to make Choc chip muffin with marsbar centre:
  1. Take 2 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  3. Prepare 3 eggs
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 packages cream cheese
  5. Make ready 1 3/4 tbsp vanilla extract
  6. Take 1 packages chocolate chips
  7. Prepare 4 mini mars bar
  8. Take 1 1/2 cup milk
  9. Make ready 1 1/2 cup butter melted
  10. Get 1 3/4 cup sugar
Steps to make Choc chip muffin with marsbar centre:
  1. Mix eggs, sugar, cream cheese, vanilla.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder into egg mix. Add the milk to get a nice smooth mix, add melted butter. Mix well.
  3. Add choc chips mix well
  4. Then put half measure in tray cut marsbar choc in 3 small pieces adding 1 quarter, then top up with mix.
  5. Put in oven for 20 mins or until lightly browned. Gas mark 5 about 250Â°F.
