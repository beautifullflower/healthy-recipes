The ingredients needed to make taisen's easy to go english muffin sandwich:
  1. Prepare spilt english muffin
  2. Make ready sliced kosher pickles
  3. Prepare to 3 slices of roma tomato
  4. Make ready to 4 slices of chicken breast lunch meat
  5. Get or 2 slices of provolone cheese
  6. Get mayonnaise to taste
  7. Make ready mustard or spicy mustard to taste
  8. Get salt and pepper to taste if desired
Instructions to make taisen's easy to go english muffin sandwich:
  1. slice the pickles length wise then in half
  2. slice up tomato
  3. slice the muffin in half. toast it
  4. put the slices on a plate. spread mayo and mustard on both sides
  5. put cheese on one side. put the meat on the other half
  6. top on half with pickle slices and the other with tomato
  7. if desired add a few sprinkles of salt and pepper
  8. put it together and eat! you can also warm it if you like after your done putting it together. enjoy
