  5. Get For the filling

  7. Make ready rice vinegar

Instructions to make Shrimps gyoza 🥟:
  1. Prepare your dough by mixing all the ingredients to a smooth ball and let rest about 30 min while you prepare the filling. Chop your shrimps to very small pieces, place in a bowl and add the remaining ingredients. Prepare your skins of about 9 cm and fill with a heaped tsp each.
  2. Close your dumplings and cook first in a pan until nicely browned, ten add some water and steam with lid on for you minutes. You should be able to cook them all in one go in a 24 cm pan. If smaller, repeat the process until you finish all the dumplings.
