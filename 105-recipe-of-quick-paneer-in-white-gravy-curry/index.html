The ingredients needed to make Paneer In White Gravy Curry:
  1. Get 1 cup cubed paneer
  2. Get 1/2 tbsp broken cashew nuts
  3. Prepare 1 tsp black pepper
  4. Get 1/2 tsp garam masala
  5. Take 1 onion chopped and paste
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp milk
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp fresh cream
  8. Take Few chopped coriander leaves for garnishing
  9. Get 1 tsp oil or ghee
  10. Take as per taste Salt
Steps to make Paneer In White Gravy Curry:
  1. Grind onion, garlic, and cashew nuts into a paste.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add the paste fry for 3 minutes. Then add milk, garam masala, black pepper, fresh cream salt, and a little water. Stir it continously for 3 minutes. Then add paneer cubes. Add water if it is needed. Cook in a slow flame till the curry is gravy. Once it is done.
  3. Transfer to a serving plate. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve with chapati and pulao.
