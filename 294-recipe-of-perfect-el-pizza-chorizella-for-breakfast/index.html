The ingredients needed to make El Pizza Chorizella:
  1. Make ready 2 cups flour
  2. Take 1/2 tsp Yeast active dry yeast
  3. Get 6 tbsp Tomato and Herb mix (It can be pasta bake or tomato puree mixed with chopped tomatoes with mixed herbs [e.g oregano, thyme, basil])
  4. Prepare 125 grams Mozzarella
  5. Get handful Spinach
  6. Get 3/4 sliced Chesnut Mushroom
  7. Make ready thin sliced Cheddar cheese; for stuffed crust
  8. Get 3 tbsp sweet corn
  9. Get 10-20 slices Chorizo
  10. Make ready 1/2 handful Curly Kale
  11. Prepare pinch Salt
Instructions to make El Pizza Chorizella:
  1. Mix the flour and the yeas add the salt and mix while adding water (if it starts getting too sticky add more flour)
  2. Knead the dough until it's not sticky
  3. Pre heat the oven to 180 degrees celsius
  4. Leave for 30 minutes for the dough to leaven
  5. Then take out a tray or pyrex and make your desired pizza base shape
  6. Add tomato mix sauce on the base
  7. Place half of the slices of mozzarella
  8. Sprinkle the ingredients
  9. Place the other half of mozzarella
  10. For the stuffed crust: place the thin strips of cheddar cheese following the crust line and carefully roll the crust over the cheese strips
