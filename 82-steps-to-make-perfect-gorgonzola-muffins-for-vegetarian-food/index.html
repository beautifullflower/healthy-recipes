The ingredients needed to make Gorgonzola muffins:
  1. Take 75 g plain flour
  2. Prepare 150 g Gorgonzola cheese
  3. Take 1 egg
  4. Get 1 tablespoon veg oil
  5. Take 50 ml milk
  6. Take 7 g dried instant yeast
  7. Take 30 g chopped nuts
  8. Get Pinch salt
Steps to make Gorgonzola muffins:
  1. Put egg and oil in a bowl. Add milk and mix. Add chopped Gorgonzola and mix
  2. Add rest of ingredients and mix until everything blended well. Leave to rest for 20 min while you now heat the oven to 200. Lightly grease muffin trays. Pour mixture into each evenly. Bake for 20 minutes. Check they are cooked with a cocktail stick
  3. Cool on a baking tray and enjoy for breakfast ðŸ˜„
