The ingredients needed to make Keto Diet: Peppered grilled chicken:
  1. Prepare 1/2 kg chicken
  2. Prepare 5 fresh pepper
  3. Get 1 bulb onion
  4. Make ready 1 knorr cube
  5. Make ready to taste salt
  6. Make ready 1 tsp crushed garlic and ginger
  7. Make ready olive oil cooking spray
Instructions to make Keto Diet: Peppered grilled chicken:
  1. Wash chicken and drain; preheat oven 260degree
  2. Blend pepper, onions, garlic and ginger and set aside; add 2tbsp of pepper paste and toss into the chicken leave it to marinate as long as you wish and send it to the oven
  3. Heat up a small pot and spray your cooking spray into the pot, add the blended paste and fry till the paste is cooked through
  4. Once the chicken is grilled complete, toss in the a tablespoon of the fried paste into the grilled chicken and mix together enjoy with a bottle of lemon chilled water
