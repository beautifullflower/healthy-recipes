The ingredients needed to make Glacé cherry muffins:
  1. Make ready 200 g softened butter
  2. Get 200 g caster sugar
  3. Prepare 4 medium eggs
  4. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Make ready 200 g self raising flour
  6. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp milk
  8. Take 200 g glacé cherries

Instructions to make Glacé cherry muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 220°c fan.
  2. Put the butter and sugar into a large mixing bowl. Using an electric whisk, mix until pale and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs to the bowl one at a time, whisking each time. Then add the vanilla extract and whisk again.
  4. Sift the flour and baking powder into the bowl then fold into the mixture using a spoon. The mixture will be thick.
  5. Add the milk and again fold through the mixture. This will loosen the mixture, just the right amount.
  6. Chop the glacé cherries into quarters, add to the mixture and fold them in well.
  7. Line a 12-hole muffin tin with paper muffin cases. Divide the mixture evenly between the cases.
  8. Put into the oven for 5 minutes then immediately turn the temperature down to 180°c for 15 mins. Keep an eye on them as every oven is different. Remove from oven and enjoy.
