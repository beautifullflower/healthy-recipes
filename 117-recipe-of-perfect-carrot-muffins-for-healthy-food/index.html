The ingredients needed to make Carrot muffins:
  1. Take 1 cup all purpose flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup yogurt
  3. Take 1/4 cup oil or butter
  4. Get 3/4 cup sugar
  5. Prepare 1 cup grated carrot
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  8. Get 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp cinnamon powder
  10. Take 2 tbsp milk
  11. Make ready Dry fruit for garnish

Instructions to make Carrot muffins:
  1. In a mixing bowl, Add yogurt and sugar mix well after add baking powder and baking soda mix well 2 min and keep for 5 min.
  2. Add oil in yogurt mixture, mix gently after that add grated carrot.
  3. Now mix flour with cinnamon powder mix well now adding the milk and mix it.
  4. Lastly add lemon juice and mix well.
  5. Pour the mixture in 12 medium muffins cup and garnish with dry fruit.
  6. Put some dry fruit on the mixture.
  7. Baked in preheated oven at 180 degree for 25 to 30 min. - Take tooth pick test and cool it.
