The ingredients needed to make My Slow Cooked Pulled Chicken.#Mainmeal#Sandwich#Lunch:
  1. Get 2 Chicken breasts cut into strips. Better to use chicken thighs
  2. Prepare 1 Cup Dr Peppa Drink
  3. Make ready 2 Garlic Cloves crushed
  4. Prepare 1 tsp BBQ Seasoning
  5. Get 1/4 tsp black pepper
Instructions to make My Slow Cooked Pulled Chicken.#Mainmeal#Sandwich#Lunch:
  1. Ingredients
  2. Slice the chicken and lay in the slow cooker pot
  3. Next sprinkle over the BBQ Seasoning
  4. Next add the garlic. Then the Dr Peppa
  5. Add the pepper and add the lid and set on high for 4 hours
  6. When its soft and cooked drain off the excess fluid just leave a tiny amount. And add to a bowl.
  7. With Two forks pull apart the chicken into shreds. Next add your BBQ Sauce. I used a smokey Sauce. And mix in using the two forks.
  8. Add to a serving dish and warm before serving. On a sandwich, french stick or in a wrap or on the side with french fries or salad.
