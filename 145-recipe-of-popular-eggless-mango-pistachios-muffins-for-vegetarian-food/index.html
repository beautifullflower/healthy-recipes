The ingredients needed to make Eggless Mango Pistachios Muffins:
  1. Get 1 1/2 cup plain flour
  2. Get 1 tsp baking Powder
  3. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  4. Get 1/8 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  6. Get 1 cup mango puree
  7. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  8. Take 1/3 cup oil/ butter
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp yogurt
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp milk
  11. Get Pistachios for garnish
Steps to make Eggless Mango Pistachios Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven at 180 c for 10 min.
  2. In a bowl, add all dry ingredients and mix well keep aside.
  3. In a another bowl mix all wet ingredients firstly mango puree and sugar mix well using a blender then add oil milk and yogurt.
  4. Add dry mixture in the wet mixture until become a smooth mixture.
  5. Now batter for muffins is ready.
  6. Pour the mixture into the muffins cups (medium size 12 cup) put some pistachios for garnish.
  7. Put them in preheat oven bake 15 to 20 min.
