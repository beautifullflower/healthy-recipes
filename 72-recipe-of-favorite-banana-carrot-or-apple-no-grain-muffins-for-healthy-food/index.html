The ingredients needed to make Banana/carrot or apple no grain muffins:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 bananas mashed
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup coconut oil melted
  3. Get 1/4 tsp vanilla powder
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp cream of tartar
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup almond flour
  7. Prepare 1 egg
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  9. Get 1/4 tsp ginger
  10. Take 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  11. Take 3 tbsps coconut flour/ cassava flour
  12. Take 1 tbsps water
Steps to make Banana/carrot or apple no grain muffins:
  1. Combine dry ingredients
  2. Combine wet ingredients
  3. Put wet and dry together, pour into muffin cases. Bake 25-30 mins at 180c
  4. Try adding 1 grated carrot and 1/8 cup of chopped walnuts and 1/2 banana; or 3 tbsps of apple purée, or 2 grated apples and 1/8 cup of chopped up pecans and 1/2 banana ; or 1/2 banana and a large handful of blueberries
