The ingredients needed to make Antipasto Salad (Keto):
  1. Make ready 2 small zucchini, halved and thin sliced
  2. Take 2 small yellow squash, halved and thin sliced
  3. Prepare 8 ounce button mushrooms, quartered
  4. Prepare 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  5. Prepare 1/2 red onion, halved and thin sliced
  6. Get 1 lb asparagus, cut into bites size pieces
  7. Take 1 cup Spanish olives, drained
  8. Take 1 can medium black olives, drained
  9. Get 1 can artichokes, drained and quartered
  10. Make ready 8 oz fresh mozzarella small balls, drained
  11. Prepare 1 pkg hard salami, thin sliced
  12. Prepare 1/2 pkg sliced pepperoni
  13. Prepare 1 small bottle zesty Italian dressing
  14. Get 1 cup shredded Parmesan
  15. Get 3 Tbsp Italian seasoning
  16. Get to taste Salt & black pepper
  17. Get 1 bunch fresh basil thin sliced
Instructions to make Antipasto Salad (Keto):
  1. Prepare vegetables and put in large bowl.
  2. Drain canned vegetables and add to bowl.
  3. Add all other ingredients and stir to mix.
  4. Refrigerate until ready to serve. Can be made the night before. Enjoy
