The ingredients needed to make Chocolate fudge muffins:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 cups caster sugar
  2. Make ready 2 eggs
  3. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla essence
  4. Get 12 fl oz milk
  5. Prepare 1 cup melted butter
  6. Get 2 cups self raising flour (or all purpose flour with 2 teaspoons of baking powder)
  7. Take 1 cup dark cocoa powder
  8. Make ready 150 grams (minimum) dark or milk chocolate chunks
Instructions to make Chocolate fudge muffins:
  1. Beat together: sugar, eggs, milk and vanilla essence - add melted butter and beat well
  2. Place chocolate chunks into a bowl with a tablespoon of the flour and stir together (this step helps the chunks to evenly distribute whilst cooking and not sink to the bottom)
  3. Sift flour and cocoa and mix in well. Add the chunks in flour and stir in.
  4. Evenly distribute the mixture between 12 muffin cases, fill to the top
  5. Bake in the top half of a preheated oven - 190ºC (ideally gas mark 4½) - for 16 minutes
  6. Leave in tray for a few minutes to harden slightly
