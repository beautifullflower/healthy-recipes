The ingredients needed to make Keto Cauliflower “Mock Potato” Salad:
  1. Prepare 4 cups mashed cauliflower
  2. Take 1/2 cup chopped onion
  3. Take 2 stalks diced celery
  4. Take 3 large boiled eggs diced
  5. Get 1/2 cup diced dill pickles
  6. Get 1/2 cup sliced green olives
  7. Prepare 2/3 cup real mayonnaise
  8. Get 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  9. Prepare 1/3 cup yellow mustard
  10. Make ready Salt
  11. Make ready Pepper
  12. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
Instructions to make Keto Cauliflower “Mock Potato” Salad:
  1. Steam or boil 1 to 1 1/2 heads of fresh cauliflower. Cool down. Rice it with food processor. Place in microwaveable dish and cook on high in microwave 8-10 minutes until soft. Cool
  2. In a large mixing bowl add diced eggs, onions, celery, pickles, and olives. Stir to mix thoroughly.
  3. Add cauliflower to vegetables and egg mixture. Add mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, and all spices to the bowl. Stir until mixed thoroughly. Chill at least 2 hours prior to serving. Enjoy.
