Steps to make Easy canned tuna pasta bake:
  1. Bring a water to cook pasta to boil while you prepare tuna.
  2. Using 2 canned tuna each 180 grams drain one of them as it will be too much oil. Now in skillet, saute garlic for few seconds.
  3. Add tuna, black pepper, chopped spring onions and parsley, suate for few minutes until spring onions soft.
  4. Add pasta to boiling water and cook it according to package about 10 minutes. Drain.
  5. In oven proof dish, mix pasta with tuna mixture and milk. Add mozzarella on top.
