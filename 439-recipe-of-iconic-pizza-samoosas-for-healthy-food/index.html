The ingredients needed to make Pizza Samoosas:
  1. Get 1 kg chicken
  2. Get 3 tsp green chilli paste
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp crushed garlic
  4. Make ready 1 tsp chicken spice
  5. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
  6. Prepare 1 tsp dried Italian herbs
  7. Prepare 1 each green, red and yellow peppers finely cubed
  8. Take 1 punnet mushrooms chopped
  9. Make ready 1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese, grated
Steps to make Pizza Samoosas:
  1. Heat a little ghee in a non-stick pan, add in chicken, chilli, garlic, seasoning and dried herbs. - Cook until moisture has dried. Remove to a bowl and set aside.
  2. In the same pan saute mushrooms and peppers until cooked through, season with salt and pepper. Add to the chicken and allow the mixture to cool. - Once the mixture is cool add in the cheese and stir through.  - Your mixture is now ready for filling.
  3. Fill into samoosas pastry folding over into triangles and stick the end with a paste of flour and water. Deep fry in shallow oil until evenly browned. Serve with chutney of your choice.
