The ingredients needed to make Refreshing tuna, avocado and artichokes salad:
  1. Take Salad
  2. Make ready 1 tinned tuna chunk
  3. Take 1 large avocado
  4. Make ready 1 jar artichokes
  5. Get 100 gr cherry tomatoes
  6. Get 1 pack rocket
  7. Get Dressing
  8. Make ready 3 tbsp soya sauce
  9. Get 1 tsp grated ginger
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
Steps to make Refreshing tuna, avocado and artichokes salad:
  1. Wash and dry the rocket
  2. Wash and cut the cherry tomatoes in half
  3. Peel and cut chunky pieces of avocado
  4. Mix rocket, tomatoes, avocado and artichokes
  5. Add on top the drained tuna
  6. Mix all the dressing ingredients and add it to the salad…
  7. Enjoy! Buen provecho! 😋
