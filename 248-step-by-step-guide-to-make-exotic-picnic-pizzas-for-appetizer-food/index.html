The ingredients needed to make Picnic pizzas:
  1. Make ready 5 pitta breads
  2. Make ready 250 gr grated cheese
  3. Prepare 500 gr fresh mushrooms washed and chopped
  4. Make ready 400 gr chopped tomatoes
  5. Prepare 2 tbs fresh parsley finely chopped
  6. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
  7. Get 1 green pepper, thinly sliced
  8. Prepare Olive oil
  9. Make ready Salt
  10. Make ready Pepper

Instructions to make Picnic pizzas:
  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan, add the onion and fry until softened.
  2. Add the mushrooms and the tomatoes and put salt and pepper.
  3. For the next 15 minutes stir occasionally.
  4. Then add the parsley and cook over a low heat until soft.
  5. Leave to cool at room temperature.
  6. Preheat the oven to 200C.
  7. Smooth the mushroom sauce over the pitta breads.
  8. Scatter with the green pepper and the cheese.
  9. Bake for 15 minutes until golden.
