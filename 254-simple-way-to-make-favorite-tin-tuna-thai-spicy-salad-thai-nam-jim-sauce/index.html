The ingredients needed to make Tin Tuna Thai spicy salad (Thai Nam Jim sauce):
  1. Get 1 tin tuna (drain all water but keep 4 tbsp)
  2. Get 250 sweetheart cabbage (thinly sliced)
  3. Prepare 1 carrot (grated)
  4. Take 2 thin slices baby shallots
  5. Prepare 2 spring onions (Thin slices)
  6. Prepare Nam jim Sauce
  7. Take 2-3 Thai red chilli
  8. Make ready 1-2 lime
  9. Make ready 1 tsp brown sugar
  10. Prepare 1 clove garlic (grated)
  11. Get 2 tbsp fish sauce
  12. Make ready 4 tbsp water from tin of tuna
Instructions to make Tin Tuna Thai spicy salad (Thai Nam Jim sauce):
  1. Mix salad mix- cabbage, shallots, carrot together in a big salad bowl
  2. Drain water of the tin of tuna but keep some juice to make the Nam Jim sauce
  3. Mix Nam Jim sauce together in a bowl
  4. Place salad mix on the plate then add tuna on
  5. Pour Nam Jim sauce all over the salad and enjoy this healthy and delicious tuna salad
