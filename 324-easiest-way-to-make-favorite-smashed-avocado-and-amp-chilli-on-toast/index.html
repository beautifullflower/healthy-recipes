The ingredients needed to make Smashed Avocado & Chilli on Toast 🥑🌶:
  1. Make ready 1 ripe avocado
  2. Get 1/2 red chilli, finely chopped
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp lime juice
  4. Take Pinch salt and pepper
  5. Make ready Smoked paprika
  6. Take 2 small slices seeded toast
Instructions to make Smashed Avocado & Chilli on Toast 🥑🌶:
  1. Simply mash the avocado with the lime and chilli. Add the salt and pepper to taste and spread on your toast. Sprinkle with smoked paprika to finish.
