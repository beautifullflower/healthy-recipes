The ingredients needed to make Pita pizza:
  1. Take 4 pcs Pita bread
  2. Make ready 1 cup Boneless chicken strips/cubes
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp Salt
  4. Take 1/4 tsp Crushed red chillies
  5. Get 1 tbsp Vinegar
  6. Make ready 1 tsp Oil
  7. Make ready Sauce:
  8. Take 2 tbsp Oil
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Garlic
  10. Take 3 tbsp Tomato paste
  11. Get 1/4 tsp Salt n crushed red chillies
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp Oregano
  13. Get 4 tbsp Ketchup
  14. Get 1 tbsp Sugar
  15. Make ready Veggies:
  16. Make ready 1 Onions cubes
  17. Prepare 1 Capsicum cubes
  18. Prepare 4 Mushrooms sliced
  19. Take 1/2 cup Sweet corns
  20. Make ready Black olives slices 1/4 cup
  21. Make ready Cheddar cheese one packet grated
  22. Prepare Oregano to sprinkle
Steps to make Pita pizza:
  1. For pizza first cook chicken by adding in salt, vinegar n crushed red chillies. Cook with 1 tbsp oil till tender n dry. Cool n use.
  2. For sauce put oil fry garlic till golden. Now add in tomato paste, salt, sugar, crushed red chillies and oregano along with ketchup n water 1/4 cup n cook till saucy.
  3. For assembling put pita on a tray n apply sauce. Then put 1 tbsp cheese n top with chicken strips.
  4. Further add mushrooms n sweet corns n finally top with cheese n oregano.
  5. Next put veggies (onions and capsicum)slightly sauteed in 1 tsp oil for few seconds.Add in black olives.
  6. Bake at 240 degrees hardly for 2 mins till golden from base n cheese from top melts.
  7. Cut into 4 slices each n serve hot !!
