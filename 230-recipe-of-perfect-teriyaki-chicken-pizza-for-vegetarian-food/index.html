The ingredients needed to make Teriyaki Chicken Pizza:
  1. Take 1 Pizza Base *I used 28cm base
  2. Prepare 2-3 tablespoons Japanese Mayonnaise
  3. Take 2 Shiitake Mushrooms OR Button Mushrooms *thinly sliced
  4. Make ready 2 tablespoons finely chopped Onion
  5. Take 100 g Mozzarella Cheese OR Pizza Mix *shredded or torn into small pieces
  6. Prepare 1/4 sheet Toasted Nori *cut into fine strips
  7. Get Teriyaki Chicken
  8. Get 350-400 g Chicken Thigh Fillets
  9. Prepare 1 pinch Salt
  10. Prepare 1 teaspoon Oil
  11. Take 1 tablespoon Mirin
  12. Get 1 tablespoon Sugar
  13. Get 2 tablespoons Soy Sauce
  14. Make ready 1 tablespoon Rice Vinegar *optional
Instructions to make Teriyaki Chicken Pizza:
  1. Combine Mirin, Sugar, Soy Sauce and Rice Vinegar in a small bowl. Lightly season Chicken with Salt.
  2. Heat Oil in a frying pan, and cook Chicken until golden. Remove excess Oil using paper towel if you want to do so, then pour the sauce over and keep cooking until the sauce thickens. Transfer to a plate and cut into small pieces.
  3. Preheat the oven to 220°C.
  4. Thinly spread Japanese Mayonnaise over the Pizza Base. Mix Mushrooms slices and Chicken pieces with the sauce, and scatter over the Pizza Base evenly. Add finely chopped Onion and Mozzarella (OR Pizza Mix).
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until nicely browned. Sprinkle with shredded Toasted Nori and enjoy.
