The ingredients needed to make Keto Bacon Spinach Salad:
  1. Prepare 50 g Spinach
  2. Get Cooked Bacon
  3. Get 1 T butter
  4. Get 1 T MCT or Coconut Oil
  5. Take Dash Pink Salt
  6. Take Dash Chili Flakes
  7. Prepare Any left over meat (optional)
Instructions to make Keto Bacon Spinach Salad:
  1. Wash the spinach. Lightly steam in the Microwave a few seconds.
  2. Add your protein. Add the butter while it's still hot.
  3. Add your oil, salt and chili. Mix well.
  4. Enjoy.
