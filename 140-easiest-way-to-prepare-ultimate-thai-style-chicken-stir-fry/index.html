The ingredients needed to make Thai-Style Chicken Stir-Fry:
  1. Make ready 1 lb chicken thighs, skin and bones removed
  2. Prepare 2 T fish sauce
  3. Take 1 T coconut aminos
  4. Make ready 2 small bell peppers, red, orange or yellow, sliced
  5. Get 2 medium spring onions, sliced
  6. Make ready 1 T fresh ginger, grated
  7. Make ready 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  8. Get 1 small hot chile pepper, seeded and minced
  9. Make ready 1/4 c coconut oil
  10. Take 2 c bean sprouts
  11. Get 1/4 c toasted nut butter
  12. Prepare 1 T lime juice
  13. Make ready salt and pepper
  14. Take 2 T fresh cilantro
Instructions to make Thai-Style Chicken Stir-Fry:
  1. Slice chicken into med. pieces and add fish sauce and coconut aminos. Marinate at least 1 hr.
  2. In large pan, heat half of coconut oil. Add chicken and cook until browned. Remove from pan and set aside.
  3. Grease pan with remaining oil. Add ginger, garlic and chile pepper. Cook 2-3 min., stirring constantly.
  4. Add bell pepper and onion and season with salt and pepper, cook 5 min. Add bean sprouts and cook 1 min.
  5. Add chicken, toasted nut butter, lime juice and combine well. Cook until chicken is heated through. Garnish with cilantro and season with more salt if needed.
