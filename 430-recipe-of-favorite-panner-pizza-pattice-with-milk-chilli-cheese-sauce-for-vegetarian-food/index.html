The ingredients needed to make Panner Pizza Pattice With Milk Chilli Cheese sauce:
  1. Prepare 1 Cup paneer
  2. Take 1/2 cup Cheese
  3. Take 1/2 cup mixed veggies chopped(i have used Corns,Capsicum,Mushroom,Onion)
  4. Prepare 1 chopped green chilli
  5. Get 1 tbsp coriander leaves
  6. Take For Spices-
  7. Make ready 1 tsp chilli flakes
  8. Take as per taste salt
  9. Make ready 2 tsp pizza sauce
  10. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  11. Prepare 1/4 cup Maida
  12. Take 1 Pizza Base(cut into 2 round pieces)
  13. Prepare some onions, Tomato and Capcicum Small round cut
  14. Prepare For Slurry-
  15. Prepare 1/4 cup maida and cornflour(mix with water)
  16. Get 1 cup bread crumbs
  17. Make ready 1 tsp chilli flakes
  18. Get as per taste salt
  19. Make ready as required Oil for Frying
  20. Get For Serving milk sauce,grated paneer and few corns
Instructions to make Panner Pizza Pattice With Milk Chilli Cheese sauce:
  1. For Pattice Stuffing-Take a big bowl and add into the paneer, cheese, chopped Veggies, Spices and maida and mix it well.
  2. Now take a Pizza Base And use the cookie cutter and cut round small pieces and fill stuffing-Take one small round pizza base and cut two small rounds put the stuffing then add onion ring, tomato and capsicum, apply sauce on the second base and cover it nicely. Carefully dip into the maida slurry and now coat the breadcrumbs and rest For 5 Mints in the freezer.
  3. Heat oil in a kadhai and fry until golden and crisp. - For Milk Chilli Cheese Sauce–Take a pan add milk, cheese, cornflour and stir or mix it now add one tsp hot chilli sauce and one tsp small capsicum pieces keep stirring for pouring sauce. The sauce is ready.
  4. Now plating– Garnish with crumbled paneer, few corns and milk sauce.
