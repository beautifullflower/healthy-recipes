The ingredients needed to make Matcha & White Chocolate Muffins:
  1. Make ready 2 cups Self-Raising Flour
  2. Take 1/2 cup Caster Sugar
  3. Get 1/4 teaspoon Salt
  4. Prepare 1-2 tablespoons Matcha Powder *Today I added 2 tablespoons
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup Vegetable Oil
  6. Get 1 cup Milk
  7. Get 1 Egg *lightly whisked
  8. Make ready 100 g White Chocolate *coarsely chopped
Instructions to make Matcha & White Chocolate Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 180℃. Line a 12-hole muffin pan with cases.
  2. Place all ingredients in a bowl and mix to combine. Spoon the mixture into each case. Bake for 20 minutes or until cooked through. Cool on a wire rack.
