The ingredients needed to make Puff Pizza:
  1. Prepare Puff pastry
  2. Take Hot sauce or tomato sauce
  3. Get Thyme
  4. Get For the fillings:
  5. Take Half cup mix veggies and sweet corn
  6. Make ready 1 cup mushrooms
  7. Get 1 soya sausage ready made and sliced
  8. Take 1 1/2 cup cheese
  9. Prepare Black pepper
Steps to make Puff Pizza:
  1. Roll out the pastry and cut it into the shape of your pan/tray. Base it with the sauce and thyme.
  2. Add sauce and black pepper to your mix veggies,sweetcorn,mushrooms and sausage. Top up with cheese and place it on your braai stand. Once pastry has puffed and cheese has melted you are ready to enjoy!
