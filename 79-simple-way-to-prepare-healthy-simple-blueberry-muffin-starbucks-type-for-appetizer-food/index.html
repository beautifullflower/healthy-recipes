The ingredients needed to make Simple blueberry muffin (Starbucks type):
  1. Prepare 55 g Unsalted butter
  2. Take 75 Caster sugar
  3. Make ready 1 Egg
  4. Prepare 75 g Sour cream or plain yoghourt
  5. Make ready 100 g Blueberry
  6. Prepare 85 g Plain flour
  7. Prepare 0.5 teaspoon Baking soda
  8. Prepare 0.5 teaspoon Vanilla
Steps to make Simple blueberry muffin (Starbucks type):
  1. Mix the softened butter with sugar and keep mixing until it turns pale. Add in one egg and mix well.
  2. Add in yoghourt and vanilla and mix well.
  3. Add in the sieved flour ingredients and mix well with wet ingredients.
  4. Cover blueberries with one tablespoon flour.
  5. Combine blueberries with batter. Then equally divide the batter into 4. Bake in 180℃ oven for 20-25 minutes. Enjoy.
