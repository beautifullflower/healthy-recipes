The ingredients needed to make Pizza:
  1. Make ready Dough
  2. Get 3 cup flour
  3. Take 1 1/2 tsp active dry yeast
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  5. Take 1 tbsp sugar
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
  7. Take Sauce
  8. Take 1 cup warm water
  9. Get 1 can tomato
  10. Take 3 tbsp tomato paste
  11. Make ready pinch salt & pepper
  12. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
  13. Get 1 herbs of your choice
  14. Take Topping
  15. Make ready 1 cheese
  16. Prepare 1 olives
  17. Take 1 cooked chicken breast
  18. Take 1 mushrooms
  19. Make ready 1 sweet corn
Instructions to make Pizza:
  1. Mix all the ingredients for the dough in a bowl of a stand mixer.
  2. Knead until smooth about 10 minutes. Let it rise in a oiled bowl for about 1 hour.
  3. For the sauce blend the tomato can whit the salt and pepper, tomato paste and oil. Then sieve the mixture and simmer it for 20 minutes. In the end add the herbs.
  4. Divide the dough in 3 pieces and roll it out in one of your pans.
  5. Add the toppings and bake in for 15 minutes at 200°C.
  6. Let it cool for 2 minutes and serve.
