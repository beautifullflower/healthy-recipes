The ingredients needed to make One-minute mug muffin:
  1. Make ready 4 dessert spoons almond flour
  2. Take 1/4 teaspoon baking powder (optional)
  3. Get 1 tablespoon olive oil
  4. Prepare 1 egg
  5. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
Instructions to make One-minute mug muffin:
  1. Spoon almond flour into medium sized coffee mug (with baking powder if using, for more rise and faster cooking but I prefer taste without)
  2. Add olive oil, egg, salt and pepper. Mix well with a fork.
  3. If batter is very thick, you can thin with a little milk or cream.
  4. Cook at full power in microwave oven for one minute. If not fully set on top, cook for a further 30 secs.
  5. Remove from mug, slice and butter while still hot.
  6. Eat with accompaniment of choice.
