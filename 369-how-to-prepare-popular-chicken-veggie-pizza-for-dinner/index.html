The ingredients needed to make Chicken Veggie Pizza:
  1. Get 500 g flour
  2. Make ready 1 yeast sachet(11 g)
  3. Make ready 20 ml warm water
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup oil
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  7. Prepare Half cup cubed carrots
  8. Get Half cup cubed onions
  9. Prepare Half cup cubed capsicum
  10. Make ready Half cup slice mushrooms
  11. Make ready 1 cup mozralla cheese
  12. Take Half cup cheddar cheese
  13. Get Half cup pizza sauce
  14. Get Half cup sliced black olives
  15. Get 1 cup boiled chicken cubes boiled in Tikka masala
Steps to make Chicken Veggie Pizza:
  1. Mix yeast in warm water along with brown sugar and put covered in a hot and humid place for 15 minutes
  2. After 15 minutes the yeast will be fully rinsen
  3. In a large bowl add flour, yeast mixture, oil salt and knead. Add warm water to make the dough.
  4. Cover the dough with a cling foil and place it on a hot and humid place for 2 hours or until it fully risen
  5. Preheat the oven to 220C
  6. Grease two medium sized pizza pan
  7. Take out the dough and knead it again, make two ball, as half kg yield 2 medium size pizzas
  8. Make two medium size pizza and place them in both the greased pans
  9. Spread pizza sauce, the cheddar equally on both of them
  10. Then spread chicken cubes, along with vegetables
  11. In the last shred mozralla, place black olives, and sprinkle dried parsley and red cilli flakes
  12. Bake on 1st shelf for 20 minutes until the dough turns light brown and the top of cheese melts
  13. Delicious chicken cheese pizza is ready
