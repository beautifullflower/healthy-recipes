The ingredients needed to make Carrot breakfast muffins:
  1. Make ready Wet ingredients
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Take 100 g dates syrup (or maple syrup or honey)
  4. Get 100 g margarine or butter melted
  5. Make ready 190 g grated carrots
  6. Get 20 g brown sugar
  7. Take Dry ingredients
  8. Prepare 120 g oat flour (make it by blend oats in high speed blender)
  9. Make ready 80 g oats
  10. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  11. Take 1 tsp cinnamon ground
  12. Prepare 0,5 tsp nutmeg
  13. Make ready 2 tsp baking powder
  14. Prepare 3/4 tsp baking soda
  15. Make ready 0,5 tsp salt
  16. Prepare 50 g raisin
  17. Get 50 g nuts of choices

Steps to make Carrot breakfast muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180deg C
  2. Wet ingredients: whisk the eggs for about 15 seconds and add the remaining wet ingredients. Stir until well combine.
  3. Dry ingredients: combine all the dry ingredients.
  4. Combine both wet and dry ingredients, mix until everything well incorporated. Don't over mixed it.
  5. Pour it to the muffin pan and bake for 25 minutes. Do the toothpick test.
