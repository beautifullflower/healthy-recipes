The ingredients needed to make Mandys mini quiche pies:
  1. Prepare Pastry:
  2. Make ready 2 shortcrust pastry (store bought)
  3. Make ready 1 egg beaten to glaze pastry
  4. Take spray oil for muffin tin
  5. Get Filling:
  6. Get 5 large eggs
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup heavy cream
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp fresh chives chopped
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp fresh chopped thyme
  10. Get 4 slice cooked bacon chopped
  11. Take 1 cup grated cheese
  12. Get 1 medium onion, chopped
  13. Get 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  14. Make ready salt and pepper
Steps to make Mandys mini quiche pies:
  1. Preheat oven to 180‚ÑÉ/350‚Ñâ. Unroll pastry set aside, in a large mixing bowl add eggs, cream, chives, cheese, beat together until it's light and airy.
  2. In a saute pan gently saute onions in oil until translucent remove and drain on kitchen paper allow to cool slightly, add to egg mixture, next add chopped cooked bacon, season and set aside.
  3. Cut the pastry into rounds that fit in a lightly sprayed (cooking spray) muffin tin, then cut a smaller round for the top of the pie.
  4. With pastry in the tin fill up about 2/3rds with egg mixture, lightly brush edges of the pastry with beaten egg press the smaller pastry round to seal in the mixture, brush top with egg. (Put a small cut into the top of the pastry to allow steam to escape)
  5. In a preheated oven at 180‚ÑÉ/350‚Ñâ. Place tray on the middle shelf of the oven and bake for 20-25 minutes until golden and the filling has set.
  6. After cooking leave in tin for about 2-3 minutes then carefully remove and serve with a side salad and enjoy.
