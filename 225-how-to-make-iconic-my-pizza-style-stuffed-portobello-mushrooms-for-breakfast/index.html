The ingredients needed to make My Pizza style stuffed portobello mushrooms:
  1. Take 6 Portabello mushrooms
  2. Prepare 6 tsp Mascarpone italian soft Cheese or soft mozarella
  3. Make ready 18 slices cooked pepperoni slices in each mushroom
  4. Make ready 1 tsp tomato puree in each mushroom
  5. Take 1 sprinkle of Dried Basil on each one
Instructions to make My Pizza style stuffed portobello mushrooms:
  1. Lay out the mushrooms middle stem removed. These ones i bourght are not that big.
  2. Add the tomato puree to each one then 3 slices of cooked pepperoni.
  3. Next add the cheese with a tsp drop a blob on top in the middle between the pepperoni
  4. Sprinkle the basil
  5. Wrap in twos in tin foil but loose on top.
  6. Add them to a tray and bake in a preheated oven for 20 minutes on high.
  7. Take out and if the bottom of the mushroom is soggy just add them to a dry frying pan just to dry strengthen the bottom.for 3 minutes the mushrooms leak out some water thats what will mak them soggy.
  8. You can put a thin slice of tomato on at the end and grill it for a bit.
