The ingredients needed to make Valentine’s Day treats for 2:
  1. Get For the breakfast:
  2. Make ready oatmeal (dry)
  3. Take raisins
  4. Prepare milk
  5. Get Heart shaped cookie cutter
  6. Take For the lunch:
  7. Prepare Italian white or whole wheat bread
  8. Get oatmeal (dry)
  9. Get peanut butter or almond butter
  10. Prepare cream cheese
  11. Make ready vanilla
  12. Make ready For the dinner:
  13. Take large boiled chicken breasts
  14. Prepare peppercorns
  15. Take red pepper flakes
  16. Get olive oil
  17. Get For the drink:
  18. Get oranges
  19. Take raspberries
  20. Prepare limes
  21. Take a lemon
  22. Take red food coloring
Steps to make Valentine’s Day treats for 2:
  1. For the breakfast:
  2. In a heart shaped cookie cutter, mix raisins and oatmeal
  3. Slide something underneath the cookie cutter for this part. Add the milk.
  4. Serve cold!
  5. For the lunch:
  6. In a bowl mix peanut butter and cream cheese with an electric mixer.
  7. Spread on 2 pieces of bread.
  8. On the other 2 pieces of bread, add the oatmeal. Drizzle vanilla over the oatmeal and put the sandwich together!
  9. For the dinner:
  10. In a fry pan, heat olive oil.
  11. In a bowl, mix together, chicken, peppercorns, and red pepper flakes.
  12. Add the seasoned chicken breasts to the pan and cook until hot.
  13. For the drink:
  14. Juice the 12 oranges and put the juice in a blender.
  15. Add the raspberries and blend until well mixed and foamy.
  16. Juice the limes and the lemon. Add to the blender.
  17. Add food coloring and blend for 2 minutes.
  18. Enjoy!
