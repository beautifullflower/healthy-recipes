The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin Muffins:
  1. Prepare 4 eggs
  2. Get 2 cups sugar
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cups cooking oil
  4. Make ready 1 can (14 oz) pumpkin
  5. Get 3 cups flour
  6. Take 1 tbsp cinnamon
  7. Get 2 tsp baking soda
  8. Get 2 tsp baking powder
  9. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  10. Get 2 cups raisins
Instructions to make Pumpkin Muffins:
  1. In a large bowl beat eggs slightly.
  2. Add sugar, oil, pumpkin and mix thoroughly.
  3. Add flour, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
  4. Mix well then add raisins.
  5. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full.
  6. Bake at 400 for 15-17 minutes.
