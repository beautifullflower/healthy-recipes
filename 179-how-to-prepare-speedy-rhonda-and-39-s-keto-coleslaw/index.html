The ingredients needed to make Rhonda's Keto Coleslaw:
  1. Make ready First mix together
  2. Make ready 14 oz. Bag Coleslaw Mix
  3. Make ready 1 Bag/Bunch Green Onion's (sliced)
  4. Get 1/2 Tbls. Dried Parsley
  5. Take Second Mix Together Dressing Ingredients
  6. Make ready 1 Cup Homemade Mayonnaise
  7. Get 2 Heaping Tbls. Sour Cream
  8. Make ready 1 Dropper Full Liquid Sevia
  9. Prepare 2 tsp. Applecider Vinegar
  10. Take 2 tsp. Lemon Juice (fresh or Concentrate)
  11. Get 1 tsp. Dry Mustard
  12. Prepare 1/4 tsp. Celery Seed
  13. Prepare 3/4 tsp. Garlic Powder
  14. Get 1/2 tsp. Punk Himalayan Salt
  15. Get 1/4 tsp Freshly Ground Black Pepper
Steps to make Rhonda's Keto Coleslaw:
  1. Mix the salad mixture with the dressing mixture until well combined.
  2. Add additional salt and pepper to taste
  3. Enjoy!
