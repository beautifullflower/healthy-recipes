The ingredients needed to make Scrumptious easy to make pizza toast.:
  1. Take 2 slice Of white bread (or any of your choosing)
  2. Take 1 tbsp Of tomato ketchup (or any sauce of your choice)
  3. Make ready 1/2 Of 1 small cooking onion or red.
  4. Take 1 medium White mushroom
  5. Make ready 1/3 tsp chili powder (mild)
  6. Prepare 8 medium slices of mature cheddar cheese (or any cheese you like)
  7. Make ready 1 slice Of dry cured ham
Steps to make Scrumptious easy to make pizza toast.:
  1. Take the 2 slices of bread & toast them till golden brown. Spread tomato ketchup evenly over both slices then sprinkle over (mild) chilli powder.
  2. Finely chop half an onion & mushroom. Add & evenly spread onion first then mushroom over toasts.
  3. Take 1 slice of dry cured ham & cut into small square pieces. Spread half of the cut ham on both toasts. Cut 8 medium slices of mature cheddar cheese & place 4 slices on each toast then add remaining ham to both.
  4. Pre heat oven to gas mark 5 (190°c). Place both toasts onto a baking tray & cook for 15 minutes till cheese is golden brown.
