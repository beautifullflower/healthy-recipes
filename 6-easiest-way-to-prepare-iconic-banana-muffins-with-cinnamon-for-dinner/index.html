The ingredients needed to make Banana muffins with cinnamon:
  1. Get 3 large ripe bananas, mushed
  2. Prepare 170 g soured cream (15%)
  3. Take 90 g butter, melted
  4. Take 1 egg
  5. Get 60 g caster sugar
  6. Prepare 1 tsp
  7. Get 200 g all purpose flour
  8. Take 60 g wholemeal flour
  9. Get 1/2 tsp bicarbonate soda
  10. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  11. Take Pinch salt
  12. Take Icing sugar for the top

Steps to make Banana muffins with cinnamon:
  1. Heat the oven to 180C
  2. Sift together the flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda, cinnamon and caster sugar with a pinch of salt.
  3. In a separate bowl mix the melted butter, egg, mashed bananas and soured cream
  4. Make a well in the centre of the dry ingredients and pour the wet ingredients in. Roughly mix together with a fork
  5. Spoon the mixture into the muffin cases
  6. Bake until golden brown
  7. Put the icing sugar on the top
