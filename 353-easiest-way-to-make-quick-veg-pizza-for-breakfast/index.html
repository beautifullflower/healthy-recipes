Easiest Way to Make Quick Veg pizza for Breakfast

Hello everybody, it is me, Dave, welcome to our recipe page. Today, I’m gonna show you how to prepare a special dish, veg pizza. One of my favorites. For mine, I am going to make it a little bit unique. This is gonna smell and look delicious.

Veg pizza is one of the most popular of recent trending foods in the world. It’s simple, it is fast, it tastes delicious. It is enjoyed by millions every day. They are nice and they look fantastic. Veg pizza is something that I have loved my entire life.

To begin with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few components. You can cook veg pizza using 16 ingredients and 2 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Veg pizza:
  1. Prepare FOR PIZZA DOUGH:
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup warm water
  3. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
  4. Make ready 1 tsp dry yeast
  5. Make ready 2 cups maida / plain flour / all-purpose flour
  6. Take to taste salt
  7. Make ready 3 tbsp olive oil
  8. Take as required, water to knead
  9. Get FOR TOPPINGS:
  10. Get 3 tbsp pizza sauce
  11. Take 5 mushrooms, thinly sliced
  12. Make ready 3 tbsp onion, roughly chopped
  13. Make ready ¬º cup capsicum, roughly chopped
  14. Get 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, grated
  15. Get ¬Ω tsp oregano /italian seasoning
  16. Take as required Mix herbs
Steps to make Veg pizza:
  1. Firstly, in a large mixing bowl take warm water, sugar and dry yeast and give a quick mix. - allow it to rest for 5 minutes. - now add maida, salt and olive oil. - knead the dough adding water as required. - tuck the dough and form to a ball. place in a large mixing bowl. - cover rest and rise in a warm place for about 2 hours. - punch the dough with fist to release down the air.
  2. VEG PIZZA BAKING RECIPE: - place the dough on dusted pizza plate or tray. - flatten the dough by stretching with both hands. - further, leaving a cm or more and create a dent. - then prick with centre of dough with the help of fork. - furthermore, spread pizza sauce generously leaving the sides slightly. - also top with topping of choice. - then spread grated cheese and sprinkle italian seasonings. - further, bake at 500 degrees fahrenheit for about 12 - 15 minutes. - finally, slice and serve pizza hot.
