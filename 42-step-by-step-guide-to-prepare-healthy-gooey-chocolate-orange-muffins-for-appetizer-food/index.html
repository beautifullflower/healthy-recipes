The ingredients needed to make Gooey Chocolate Orange Muffins:
  1. Take For the Muffins
  2. Get 1/2 TBSP orange zest
  3. Take 2 TBSP freshly squeezed orange juice
  4. Make ready 50 g good quality cocoa powder
  5. Get 100 g dark muscavado sugar
  6. Make ready 250 ml boiling water
  7. Take 125 g unsalted butter, at room temperature
  8. Get 150 g caster sugar
  9. Get 2 large eggs
  10. Make ready 225 g plain flour
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking powder
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  13. Get 16 mini Terry‚Äôs chocolate orange segments, chopped into chips
  14. Prepare For the orange & cardamom buttercream
  15. Make ready 200 g unsalted butter, softened
  16. Get 350 g icing sugar
  17. Get Zest & juice from half large orange
  18. Prepare 6 cardamom pods, seeds crushed and ground
  19. Make ready To decorate
  20. Take 16 mini Terry‚Äôs Chocolate Orange segments
Instructions to make Gooey Chocolate Orange Muffins:
  1. Preheat your oven to 180 degrees fan. Place the cocoa powder, 100g dark muscavado sugar and orange zest and juice in a large bowl. Pour in the boiling water and whisk until well mixed. Set aside to cool.
  2. Beat the butter and caster sugar together in a large mixing bowl, ideally using a free standing mixer or electric whisk. Beat in each egg one at a time, mixing each egg for around 2 mins each.
  3. Add the flour, baking powder and bicarb and fold in very gently so as not to knock the air out.
  4. Slowly incorporate the cocoa/orange mixture into the batter until well incorporated.
  5. Pour this beautifully chocolatey mixture into the muffin cases. Place the terry‚Äôs chocolate orange pieces into each case, dividing it up equally.
  6. Place into the oven and bake for 15-18 minutes. Or until a skewer comes out clean. Remove from the oven and allow to cool on a rack.
  7. Next, make the buttercream. Beat together the butter and icing sugar until smooth. Add the crushed cardamom pods seeds, orange juice and orange zest and beat well.
  8. Once the cakes are completely cool, pipe the buttercream on top, and place a whole mini Terry‚Äôs segment on each one to decorate.
