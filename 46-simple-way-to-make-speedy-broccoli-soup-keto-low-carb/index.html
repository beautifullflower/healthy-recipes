The ingredients needed to make Broccoli Soup (Keto/Low Carb):
  1. Take 30 g Salted Butter
  2. Get 4 tbsp whipping cream
  3. Prepare 100 g cream cheese
  4. Make ready 60 g marble cheese
  5. Take 2 cups water
  6. Prepare 1 chicken bullion cube
  7. Take 2.5 cups steamed broccoli (1 stock)
  8. Take 1/3 cup almond flour
  9. Make ready 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  10. Make ready 1/8 tsp black pepper
Instructions to make Broccoli Soup (Keto/Low Carb):
  1. Steam broccoli, put to side.
  2. Combine butter, whipping cream, cream cheese, marble cheese, once melted add remaining ingredients including broccoli.
  3. Use blender to blend. add salt as required to taste.
