The ingredients needed to make Sausage pizza:
  1. Make ready 280 g bread flour
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp sugar
  3. Take 190 ml milk
  4. Make ready 20 g butter
  5. Prepare pinch salt
  6. Prepare 1 tsp active dry yeast
  7. Prepare pizza sauce
  8. Take For topping
  9. Take sausage
  10. Make ready tomato
  11. Get pepper
  12. Prepare mushroom
  13. Get mozzarella cheese
Steps to make Sausage pizza:
  1. Prepare pizza dough by mixing bread flour, sugar, salt, yeast. Then add milk and butter. Mix the dough follow bread maker instruction.
  2. Roll the dough in rectangular shape. Apply pizza sauce and all topping. Put mozzarella cheese on top.
  3. Bake at 170C for 20 min.
