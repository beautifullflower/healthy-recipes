To begin with this recipe, we must prepare a few ingredients. You can cook ham & egg muffins using 3 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Ham & Egg Muffins:
  1. Make ready 4 slices ham, parma ham or veggie ham
  2. Get 4 small eggs
  3. Make ready Handful grated cheese
Steps to make Ham & Egg Muffins:
  1. Line a muffin tin with a slice of ham. Press the ham down to create a well and try to lift the edges of the ham up to create a deep well.
  2. Crack an egg into the ham cup, then sprinkle with cheese.
  3. Bake at 190 C for 12-13 minutes.
