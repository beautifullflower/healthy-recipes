The ingredients needed to make Cornmeal Muffins:
  1. Make ready 1 cup flour
  2. Take 1 cup cornmeal
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar
  4. Get 4 tsp baking powder
  5. Get 1 tbsp salt
  6. Take 1 egg
  7. Take 1 cup milk
  8. Take 2 tbsp melted butter
Instructions to make Cornmeal Muffins:
  1. sift together cornmeal, sugar, baking powder and salt
  2. Add beaten egg and milk, mix well and add melted butter
  3. put into greased muffin pans and bake in 204c oven for 20-25 minutes
