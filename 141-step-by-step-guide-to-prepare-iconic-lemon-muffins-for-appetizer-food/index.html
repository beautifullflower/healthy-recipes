The ingredients needed to make Lemon muffins:
  1. Get 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  2. Take 1/3 cup milk
  3. Get 1/3 cup oil
  4. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup yogurt
  6. Take 1 tbsp lemon zest
  7. Get 1 tsp lemon essence
  8. Get 1/4 tsp yellow colour (option) Mix all in a bowl
  9. Prepare 1 cup maida or plain flour
  10. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  11. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  12. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
Instructions to make Lemon muffins:
  1. Preheat oven at 175 for 10 min
  2. Mix all in another bowl
  3. Mix both part in a bowl n fill in 10 muffins cup till 1/3 part.
  4. Bake 175 c for 15 to 20 min.
  5. When it completely cool down you can Icing with butter cream.
