The ingredients needed to make Basic Plain Muffin:
  1. Get 2 cups Self-Raising Flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup Caster Sugar
  3. Get 1/4 teaspoon Salt
  4. Get 1 Egg *whisked
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup Vegetable Oil *e.g.Canola Oil
  6. Prepare 1 cup Milk
Instructions to make Basic Plain Muffin:
  1. Preheat oven to 180℃. Line a 12-hole muffin pan with cases.
  2. Place all ingredients in a bowl and mix to combine. Spoon the mixture into each case. Bake for 20 minutes or until cooked through.
