The ingredients needed to make Pizza Bites:
  1. Take for dough
  2. Get 2 cup flour (maida)
  3. Prepare 1 egg
  4. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  5. Take 2 tbsp oil
  6. Prepare for yeast proofing
  7. Take 3/4 cup lukewarm water
  8. Take 2 tsp yeast
  9. Get 1 tsp sugar
  10. Prepare for filling
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  12. Make ready 1/4 cup olives
  13. Take 1/4 cup mushrooms
  14. Take 1/4 cup pepperoni
  15. Make ready 1/4 cup capsicum
  16. Make ready 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
Steps to make Pizza Bites:
  1. In a bowl take lukewarm water and dissolve sugar in it. Add yeast and let it sit for 5 to 10 mins. This is called yeast proofing.
  2. In a separate large bowl sift together flour and salt. Make a well. Add in oil and 1 beaten egg. Knead the dough. Add yeast water in it. Knead further until smooth. Cover it and keep aside for 1 hr for rising.
  3. After the dough has risen, sprinkle some flour onto the surface. Take out dough from bowl, punch and knead it into a round shape. Cut the dough into half. Keep 1 half in ziplock bag in fridge for later use.
  4. Roll out the dough. Spread pizza sauce on it.
  5. Add fillings of vegetables and protein, and cheese. Roll the dough lengthwise to form a log.
  6. With a knife, cut sections from the log, and place into greased baking tray.
  7. Bake in preheated oven at 180°C, for 30 to 35 minutes. Serve with tea.
