To begin with this recipe, we must first prepare a few components. You can have portobello mushroom pizzas using 6 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you can achieve that.

The ingredients needed to make Portobello Mushroom Pizzas:
  1. Get Portobello Mushrooms
  2. Make ready Mozzarella Cheese
  3. Get Tomato Sauce
  4. Prepare Cracked Pepper
  5. Get Salt
  6. Prepare Italian Seasoning
Steps to make Portobello Mushroom Pizzas:
  1. Take out Stems from Mushrooms then clean with wet paper towel DO NOT WASH IN SINK it will make mushroom absorb water and be mushy
  2. Next step is to lightly drizzle olive oil on underside of mushroom. Then pinch of Salt, Pepper, and Italian Seasoning.
  3. Next turn on grill to about 300 to 350 degrees then put mushrooms on bottom rack top side down so seasoning doesn't fall into grill. Wait until Mushrooms are partially cooked then turn them over to cook seasons side until edges look dark brown and slightly wilted that's how you know they are cooked.
  4. Next put Seasoned side of mushrooms with Tomato/Basil Sauce homemade or store bought on top about 2 tablespoons worth. Then finely shred cheese and spread on top to cover sauce because who doesn't like cheese.
  5. Garnish with your favorite pizza topping such as Pepperoni, Olives, Jalape√±o etc. then put back into grill to melt and brown delicious Mozzarella Cheese. Then be ready to be amazed at delicious Portobello Mushroom Pizza. Sprinkle with crushed peppers.
