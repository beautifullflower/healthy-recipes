The ingredients needed to make Keto Chicken Korma:
  1. Get 500 gm chicken (boneless or with bone as you like)
  2. Get 1/2 cup curd
  3. Get 2 onion
  4. Make ready 1 tsp red chilli powder
  5. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala powder
  6. Take 1 tsp coriander masala
  7. Make ready 1 tsp ginger, garlic paste
  8. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  9. Take 1 star anise
  10. Get 4-6 black pepper
  11. Get 2 green cardamom
  12. Get 1 small stick cinnamon
  13. Get 1 tbsp chopped coriander
  14. Take 3 tbsp ghee
  15. Prepare to taste Salt
Steps to make Keto Chicken Korma:
  1. Heat a pan add ghee and add sliced onion and fry it till golden in colour and keep aside
  2. Now take a jar put curd and add fried onion and blend it and keep aside
  3. Now take the same pan with ghee in it and heat it and add all the whole spices and stir it - now add chicken and stir it and add ginger garlic paste and stir it again
  4. Now add fried onion - curd paste that we made and stir it up well
  5. Now add all masala powder, salt and cover it and cook on low to medium flame for 4-5 minutes or when chicken is done
  6. Now sprinkle chopped coriander and give it a final stir
  7. Now Chicken korma is ready
  8. Serve it
