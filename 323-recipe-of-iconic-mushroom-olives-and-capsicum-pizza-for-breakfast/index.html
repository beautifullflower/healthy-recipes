The ingredients needed to make Mushroom, Olives and Capsicum Pizza:
  1. Take 1 pizza base store bought
  2. Take 1 pizza sauce sachet
  3. Prepare 1 capsicum red cut in circles
  4. Take 12 - 15 black olives chopped
  5. Take 7 button mushrooms diced
  6. Take 1.5 - 2 cups cheese grated
  7. Take 180 C Preheat the oven to
Instructions to make Mushroom, Olives and Capsicum Pizza:
  1. Cover the base with the pizza sauce, and top it with the veggies: mushrooms, capsicum and the olives
  2. Finally cover it with a generous amount of grated cheese like mozzarella or cheddar
  3. Put it in the oven and allow the veggies to cook through and the cheese melt
