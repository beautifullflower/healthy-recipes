The ingredients needed to make Fajita Pizza:
  1. Make ready For Dough
  2. Take 2 cups self rising flour
  3. Take 1 cup water
  4. Get 1 tsp yeast
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp sugar
  6. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  7. Make ready 1 tsp olive oil
  8. Get For Tomato Sauce
  9. Get 4 tomatoes
  10. Take 1 tbsp oil
  11. Get 1 onion
  12. Prepare 1 garlic clove
  13. Take 1 cup water
  14. Get 1 tsp salt
  15. Get 1 tsp black pepper
  16. Prepare 1 tsp mixed herbs
  17. Prepare Topping
  18. Make ready 1 cup frozen chicken chunks
  19. Get 2 cups mixed cheese mozzarella and cheddar
  20. Take 1 cup olives
  21. Make ready 1 cup onion diced
  22. Get 1 cup green capsicum diced
  23. Get 1 cup mushrooms sliced
  24. Take 1 tsp mixed herbs
Steps to make Fajita Pizza:
  1. Make dough. Add sugar and yeast in water. Leave for 2 min. Mix salt in flour. Then add water to flour and knead to form dough.
  2. Make into a ball. Add 1 tsp olive oil and pat onto dough with hands. Leave to rise for 1 hr in a warm place.
  3. Make tomato sauce. Wash and halve the tomatoes. In a pot, add oil, diced onions, and 1 clove chopped garlic. Fry for 1 min.
  4. Add tomatoes to pan. Add salt, pepper, and water. Let it cook on medium heat for 10 mins until tomatoes are soft.
  5. Mash with back of wooden spoon. Sauce should be thick and chunky. Add herbs and mix. Turn off heat.
  6. Wash and chop veggies for topping. Defrost chicken chunks.
  7. Take out dough and roll it out onto a flat surface to form a large circle. Spread sauce over it. Spread all veggies and cheese.
  8. Bake in preheated oven at 180°C for 30 mins. When crust becomes golden, take it out.
  9. Rest for 5 mins. Serve with garlic butter. Enjoy!
