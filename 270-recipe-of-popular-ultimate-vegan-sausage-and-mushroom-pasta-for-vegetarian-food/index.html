The ingredients needed to make Ultimate vegan sausage and mushroom pasta:
  1. Prepare 400 g shiitake mushrooms
  2. Take 4 tofurky sausages
  3. Get 3 cloves garlic
  4. Prepare 1 red onion
  5. Get 1.5 can tomato sauce
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  7. Make ready 100 g black and green marinated olives
  8. Prepare 1 tsp red chilly flakes
  9. Get 1 tsp black pepper
  10. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  11. Prepare olive oil
  12. Prepare 140 g macaroni pasta
  13. Take 100 g shredded vegan mozzarella
Instructions to make Ultimate vegan sausage and mushroom pasta:
  1. Cook the pasta in some salt water and a little olive oil like Gordon Ramsey.
  2. Saute the onions, garlic, sausages (sliced) and mushrooms in some olive oil. Add the other ingridients and cook for about 10 min.
  3. Plate the pasta and the sauce and cover with grated cheese. Put the dish in the microwave for 2 minutes to melt the cheese.
