The ingredients needed to make Cheese and Mushroom Savoury Pancake:
  1. Make ready 250 ml milk
  2. Prepare 50 g butter
  3. Make ready 150 g self raising flour
  4. Prepare 1 egg
  5. Make ready 150 g parmesan
  6. Get 150 g mozzarella
  7. Make ready 220 g mushrooms
  8. Make ready 3 spring onions
  9. Get 1 small handful of basil
  10. Get salt and pepper to season
Steps to make Cheese and Mushroom Savoury Pancake:
  1. Chop the basil and spring onions, grate the parmesan and slice the mozzarella.
  2. Slice the mushrooms and put in a microwaveable bowl. Cook on high for 2-4 minutes, or until most (but not all) of the water that leaks out of the mushrooms has evaporated. This concentrates the flavour.
  3. Add the basil, spring onion and cheese to the hot mushrooms and stir until the cheese melts, you may need to microwave a little more until it is nice and runny.
  4. Add the egg flour butter and milk to a bowl and whisk until smooth. Then pour into a lightly oiled and heated pan. Cook until brown on both sides.
  5. When cooked, fill your pancake with the hot cheesy mushroom mixture. Service with a salad of your choice. Enjoy!
