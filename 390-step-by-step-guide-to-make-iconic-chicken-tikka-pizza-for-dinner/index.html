The ingredients needed to make Chicken Tikka Pizza:
  1. Make ready Pizza Dough
  2. Take 2 cup flour
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp dry milk
  4. Prepare 1 tsp caster sugar
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp instant yeast
  7. Make ready 3 tbsp oil
  8. Make ready Lukewarm water as required for binding
  9. Get Pizza sauce
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp Garlic paste
  11. Make ready 1/2 cup tomato paste
  12. Take 2 tbsp oil
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  14. Prepare 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  15. Prepare 1/2 tsp oregano
  16. Get 2 tbsp ketchup
  17. Get Chicken Tikka
  18. Make ready 2 breast pieces chicken boneless (cut into small pieces)
  19. Take 3 tbsp Tikka Masala
  20. Make ready 1/2 tsp Garlic paste
  21. Prepare 1/2 tsp Ginger paste
  22. Prepare 1 tsp lemon juice
  23. Prepare Topping
  24. Get 1 capsicum Julien cut
  25. Prepare 1 tomato Julien cut
  26. Get 1 oinon slices
  27. Take 4 tbsp mushrooms (slices)
  28. Make ready 4 tbsp black olives
  29. Get 1 cup cheddar cheese
  30. Make ready 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  31. Get 1/2 tsp oregano (for sprinkle)
  32. Take Pinch chilli flakes (for sprinkle)
Steps to make Chicken Tikka Pizza:
  1. For Dough : take a bowl add flour, salt, yeast,milk powder, caster sugar and oil mix well.
  2. Keep adding lukewarm water as required to keep it soft.
  3. Cover this dough and leave for 2 hours to rise.
  4. Grease the pizza pan and sprinkle the semolina.
  5. Roll out on to a surface and line a pizza pan with it and prick it with fork.
  6. Place this pan for 6 to 7 minutes in a preheated oven.
  7. Prepare the pizza sauce.
  8. Oil in a pan add Garlic paste and fry for 2 minutes.
  9. Add all ingredients and cook for 3 minutes and sauce is ready.
  10. Use this sauce when it's cold.
  11. Marinate the chicken with Tikka Masala, ginger garlic paste and lemon juice.
  12. Leave for 15 minutes and fry until chicken is soft.
  13. Spread the pizza sauce on dough and sprinkle cheddar cheese.
  14. Now spread the chicken Tikka pieces on it. Sprinkle onion, capsicum, tomato, mushroom, black olives and cheddar cheese and mozzarella cheese.
  15. Sprinkle the oregano and chilli flakes.
  16. Place this pan in preheated oven at 180 c for 25 minutes.
