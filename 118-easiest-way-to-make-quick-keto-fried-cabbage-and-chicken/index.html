To get started with this particular recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can have keto fried cabbage and chicken using 6 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Keto Fried cabbage and chicken:
  1. Make ready 1/2 small sized cabbage cut
  2. Make ready 1 small pepper
  3. Make ready 2 pieces already cooked chicken(shredded)
  4. Take Unsalted butter
  5. Make ready Pinch salt
  6. Take 1/2 tsp tumeric
Instructions to make Keto Fried cabbage and chicken:
  1. Place your pan on hot flame, add butter to melt.
  2. Add your sliced pepper and onions, shredded chicken and fry for a few minutes
  3. For your spices you add your salt and tumeric..(because this is a keto meal I did not use seasoning cubes but others can)
  4. After stiring the mixture, add your cut cabbage and allow to steam for a while….and your fried cabbage and chicken is ready.
