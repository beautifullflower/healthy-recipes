The ingredients needed to make Oven Baked Chicken Loaded Bread Pizzas:
  1. Prepare 16 Bread slices
  2. Get 1 cup Pizza Sauce
  3. Make ready 1 cup Corn kernels
  4. Get 1 cup sliced Mushrooms
  5. Make ready 1 cup sliced Olives
  6. Make ready 1 cup chopped Onions
  7. Make ready 1 cup chopped Capsicum
  8. Take 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
  9. Prepare FOR THE CHICKEN -
  10. Take 400 gm Boneless Chicken cubes
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  12. Prepare 1 tsp Cumin powder roasted
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp Tomato Ketchup
  14. Make ready 1 tsp Salt
  15. Prepare 1 tsp Tikka Masala
  16. Make ready 2-3 cups Mozzarella Cheese shredded
Steps to make Oven Baked Chicken Loaded Bread Pizzas:
  1. Saute Chicken in Olive Oil by using the rest of the ingredients mentioned. Keep aside.
  2. Preheat your oven on 180° at first. Now start with the rest of the preparations. Spread Sauce on all the Bread slices. Top with all the colourful vegetables.
  3. Last but not the least, add sauteed Chicken that's ready. Finally top it up with a generous amount of Cheese. Sprinkle some Italian seasoning on each. Pop in the oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes.
  4. What I do is to first bake them all with almost all the toppings except the Cheese. After baking for 12-15 minutes, remove the tray. Top with the shredded Mozzarella Cheese and sprinkle some Italian seasoning and Red Chilli flakes if desired.
  5. Again pop it up in the oven just until the Cheese melts. That's it. Absolutely lipsmacking and amazingly delicious Dinner is at your disposal. Enjoy!
