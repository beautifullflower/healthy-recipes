The ingredients needed to make Butter Roasted Radishes:
  1. Prepare 1 pound radishes, trimmed and halved
  2. Take 6 T butter
  3. Get 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  4. Take 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  5. Take 1/2 tsp salt
Steps to make Butter Roasted Radishes:
  1. Melt butter in large skillet. Add radishes and seasonings.
  2. Stir and "smash" with back of spatula to cook/fry.
