The ingredients needed to make Thin crust wheat flour pizza base and yummy pizza:
  1. Get 1 cup Wheat flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  3. Take 1/4 tsp Baking soda
  4. Get 2 tsp Oil
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup crud
  6. Prepare to taste Salt
  7. Prepare For topping
  8. Get as needed pizza sauce
  9. Take 1/2 tsp Chilli flakes
  10. Get pinch Oregano
  11. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  12. Get 1 cup mix Veggies (potato,carrot,beans,peas,mushroom,capsicum)
  13. Take as needed Mozzarella cheese
  14. Make ready 1/4 cup Corn
Steps to make Thin crust wheat flour pizza base and yummy pizza:
  1. Pizza base on a pan.
  2. Take 2 cups of wheat flour in a bowl, add 1/2 tsp of salt, 1/2 tsp baking powder, 1/4 tsp baking soda, oil 2 tsp and knead the dough with crud.
  3. After kneading the soft dough, mash it for 5-6 min. Then cover it with wet cloth and rest it 10 min.
  4. After 10 min, Dust the dry flour from a dough lump and flatten it thickly into a round shape. Then prick it using a fork.
  5. Preheat a pan on low flame and spread some on plate and lift a pizza base and place it over the plate.
  6. Cover and cook it for 10-15 minutes low to medium flame. Now your pizza base is ready.
  7. Now take the pizza base. Spread some pizza sauce on it, top up with all veggies of your choice and top up with granted cheese, add black pepper, oregano and chilli flakes.
  8. Cook it low flame for 5-7 minutes. Turn off the flame now your yummy pizza is ready to serve.
