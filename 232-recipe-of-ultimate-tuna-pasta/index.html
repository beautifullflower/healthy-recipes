The ingredients needed to make Tuna pasta:
  1. Make ready 1/2 onion
  2. Prepare 3 garlic cloves
  3. Prepare (Herbs of your choice)
  4. Prepare Salt and pepper
  5. Prepare 1 tablespoon Olive oil
  6. Get Parsley
  7. Take Parmesan cheese
  8. Prepare Pasta of your choice
  9. Take 1 Tin tuna
  10. Get 1 tin chopped tomatoes

Instructions to make Tuna pasta:
  1. Dice your onion and grate the garlic then heat your pan with olive oil
  2. Fry up your onion and garlic next add all your herbs and spices
  3. Add your tuna and fry for about 1-2 minutes then add your chopped tomatoes and simmer for about 3 minutes
  4. Grate some Parmesan cheese and chop up your parsley
  5. Drain the pasta and add to your sauce and mix well, serve with the Parmesan and parsley ☺️☺️
