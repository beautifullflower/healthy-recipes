The ingredients needed to make Low carb marinara sauce:
  1. Prepare 6 tomato medium
  2. Make ready 4 pod garlic
  3. Make ready 1 tsp Mixed herbs
  4. Get 1 tsp Tyson olive oil
  5. Take as per taste Chilli flakes
  6. Prepare 1 /2 tsp Basil-
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  8. Prepare per taste Salt

Instructions to make Low carb marinara sauce:
  1. Blanch tomatoes and remove the skin
  2. Crush or blend completely as preferences of cunchy or smooth sauce
  3. Now add oil in a pan at medium flame and add garlic fry for 10 sec
  4. Now add tomato and black pepper, oregano, Basil, chilli flakes and salt and little water
  5. Simmer for 2-3 min it's ready enjoy or make pizza, pasta etc whatever you like
