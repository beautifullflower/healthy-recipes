The ingredients needed to make Cheese and olive muffins:
  1. Make ready 150 g Plain flour
  2. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Make ready 75 g grated cheese
  4. Get 1 large egg
  5. Make ready 10 chopped olives
  6. Make ready 100 ml milk
  7. Make ready 50 g butter or oil
  8. Make ready Chopped fresh basil
  9. Get Salt & pepper
  10. Prepare Tbsp tomato purée
Instructions to make Cheese and olive muffins:
  1. Heat the oven to 190 degrees. Add 6 muffin cases to the muffin tin.
  2. Sieve the flour. Add the baking powder & stir well.
  3. Add the grated cheese and olives. Add salt & pepper and basil. Mix.
  4. Whisk the egg, add the milk and melted butter. Mix.
  5. Add the wet ingredients to the dry. Mix as little as needed.
  6. Add the tomato purée and mix until just swirled throughout.
  7. Add to the muffin cases. Bake for 20-25 minutes
