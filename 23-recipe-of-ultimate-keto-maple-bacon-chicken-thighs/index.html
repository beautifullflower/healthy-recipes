The ingredients needed to make Keto Maple Bacon Chicken Thighs:
  1. Prepare 270 g chicken thighs
  2. Make ready 6 bacon slices
  3. Take 1 Tbs Maple syrup (SF)
  4. Take 1 tsp mustard
  5. Get to taste Salt & pepper
Instructions to make Keto Maple Bacon Chicken Thighs:
  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees.
  2. Taking one at a time, tightly wrap each chicken thigh with two slices of bacon.
  3. Transfer the bacon wrapped chicken to an oven proof dish and season with salt and pepper. Mix the mustard and maple flavored syrup together until well combined
  4. Drizzle the maple-mustard mixture over the chicken, ensuring each thigh is evenly coated.
  5. Transfer to the oven to bake for 25-30 minutes or until the meat is completely cooked through and the juices run clear.
