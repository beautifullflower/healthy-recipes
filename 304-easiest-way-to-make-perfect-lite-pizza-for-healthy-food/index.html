The ingredients needed to make Lite Pizza:
  1. Make ready Crust
  2. Make ready 2 cup whole wheat flour
  3. Get 3/4 cup cold water
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup olive oil
  5. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  6. Take 2 tsp baking powder
  7. Get toppings
  8. Get 1 cup skim milk mozzarella (or the fresh homemade mozzarella you can find on my profile!)
  9. Take 1/4 cup lite Parmesan
  10. Prepare 1/2 medium onion
  11. Make ready 1 cup mushrooms
  12. Get 1/2 cup lean ham, turkey, chicken or turkey sausage
  13. Take 1/2 cup peppers
  14. Get 1/2 sliced tomato
  15. Make ready 1/2 cup pizza sauce
Steps to make Lite Pizza:
  1. Preheat oven 400° Mix all crust ingredients into a soft dough. Press into a pizza pan.
  2. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add sauce and all toppings
  4. Cook for 15 mins or until cheese is bubbly and a tiny bit browned.
