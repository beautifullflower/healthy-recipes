The ingredients needed to make Seafood chowder:
  1. Take 500 g Marinara Seafood Mix
  2. Prepare 1 medium carrot
  3. Take 1 stick celery
  4. Prepare 750 ml chicken stock
  5. Make ready 1 leek
  6. Make ready 1/2 brown onion
  7. Prepare 200 ml double cream
  8. Take 100 g cheddar cheese
  9. Make ready 50 g Grated Parmesan
  10. Get Chives to serve

Steps to make Seafood chowder:
  1. Chop carrot, celery, leek and onion roughly. Add to chicken stock, bring to the boil and simmer for 10 mins until veg is soft
  2. Blend the cooked stock and vegetables together. Do it in batches as hot liquid expands. Next add 200ml of double cream and salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Add marinara mix or other fish to the creamy soup, and simmer for around 5-7 mins.
  4. (Optional) Add 100g of cheddar and around 50g of Parmesan.
  5. Serve with some chopped chives.
