The ingredients needed to make Bread and butter muffins:
  1. Get 350 g brown stale bread cut into breadcrumbs
  2. Prepare 900 ml milk
  3. Prepare 1 cup granulated sugar
  4. Prepare 100 g butter/ vegetable spread
  5. Take sticks Cinnamon
  6. Prepare Lemon zest
  7. Make ready 200 g raisins
  8. Make ready 125 ml dark rum
  9. Prepare tin Butter and breadcrumbs to coat muffin
Steps to make Bread and butter muffins:
  1. Soak raisins in rum for about 20 minutes
  2. Cut bread in small pieces. Warm the milk with cinnamon and bring to the boil. Soak bread with flavoured milk for 30 minutes
