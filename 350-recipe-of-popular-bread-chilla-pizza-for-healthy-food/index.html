The ingredients needed to make Bread chilla pizza:
  1. Prepare 7-8 Bread slices
  2. Make ready 1 cup besan/gram flour
  3. Make ready 1 chopped small onion
  4. Prepare 2 tsp coriander leaves
  5. Make ready to taste Salt
  6. Take 1 tsp black pepper powder
  7. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  8. Take For pizza
  9. Make ready 1 cup sliced onion ,capsicum,tomato
  10. Take 1/4 cup sliced saute mushrooms
  11. Prepare handful boiled sweet corns
  12. Prepare 2 tbsp pizza sauce
  13. Prepare as needed Mozerella cheese
  14. Get 1 tsp Oregano seasoning
  15. Get 1 tsp Chilli flakes
Steps to make Bread chilla pizza:
  1. Firstly mix the besan with chopped onion and coriander leaves and make a batter for chilla with the water.Now add salt,black pepper powder and mix it well.Now add baking powder at last and rest it for 10 minutes.
  2. Now take 4 bread slices and cut them in a round shape as shown in pic.
  3. Now heat the tawa,add little oil and pour the ladle full of besan batter on it to make a chilla at low flame.After 2 minutes arrange bread slices on it and cover the joint parts with batter only.Then flip the side.
  4. Now spread some pizza sauce,then cheese,then arrange all the veggies and again spread mozarella cheese and sprinkle oregana and chilli flakes.And cover it and cook it till cheese melts at low flame only.Our delicious bread chilla pizza is ready.Enjoy
