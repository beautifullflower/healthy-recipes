The ingredients needed to make Fry Pan Pizza #ramdankitiyari:
  1. Take 1 cup (250 ml) warm water
  2. Prepare 1 teaspoon (5 ml) instant yeast
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon (5 ml) sugar
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon (5 ml) salt
  5. Get 5 TBS pizza sauce
  6. Take 1/3 cup pizza cheese
  7. Take Pinch oregano
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp oil
  9. Take 2 cups (300 g) all-purpose flour
  10. Take 1/2 cup mix vegi (onion. capsicum. Olive. Mushrooms)
Instructions to make Fry Pan Pizza #ramdankitiyari:
  1. For Dough : make a well in the flour and salt and pour the liquids to the center. Work with your fingers gradually adding dry ingredients to the liquids. What is important is to get a soft dough and to let it rise in a warm place.
  2. Make a round medium size dough circle. mark with fork over it.
  3. Spread pizza sauce den add a layer of fine cut vegis.
  4. On top add pizza cheese.
  5. At last sprinkle some oregano.
  6. Grease fry pan and shift pizza over it. Cover tightly den heat over low medium.
  7. Like around 10-15 mint your pizza is perfectly cooked. serve hot
