The ingredients needed to make Healthy Tofu Sandwich:
  1. Take as required French rolls / sandwich rolls / buns / sliced bread brown
  2. Prepare 1 inch thick firm tofu slices 2-3
  3. Get 1 zucchini cut lengthwise
  4. Make ready 1 tomato sliced
  5. Get Few pieces of iceberg lettuce
  6. Take 1 tablespoon olive oil / butter
  7. Get 1/4 cup Coriander chutney
  8. Get 1/4 tsp salt & pepper powder mix
  9. Take as required Sandwich masala for sprinkle
Instructions to make Healthy Tofu Sandwich:
  1. Prepare sandwich ingredients; Slice tofu into ¼” thickness and pat dry. Wash and remove ends of zucchini, then cut lengthwise into approx. ⅛” thick slices. Wash and slice tomatoes. Set aside.
  2. Generously grease a flat griddle and bring to medium heat. Add tofu and zucchini slices and lightly sprinkle with salt & pepper powder. Grill on both sides until they are light brown in colour. Remove from griddle and wipe and clean the griddle
  3. Slice rolls lengthwise through the center and lightly spread with olive oil/butter on the inside portion. Place on medium-heat griddle to toast. Turn off the heat.
  4. Assemble the sandwich: Turn over the roll and lightly spread Coriander Chutney on the bottom half of the roll. - Layer with lettuce followed by tofu slices. Spread tofu with more chutney. Layer with zucchini, sprinkle sandwich masala followed by tomatoes.
  5. Cover with the top half of the roll with chutney followed by sandwich masala. - Enjoy with green chutney/tomato ketchup - Note:- you can use choice of your bread; white, multigrain & - brown
