The ingredients needed to make Mac Daddy Salad:
  1. Take 1 pound ground beef
  2. Take 1 small onion, minced
  3. Prepare 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Make ready 1 tsp sea salt
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper
  6. Take 1 large head romaine lettuce, shredded
  7. Get 3/4 c shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  8. Make ready 1/3 c sliced dill pickles
  9. Prepare 1/2 c Thousand Island dressing
  10. Make ready 2 T toasted sesame seeds
Instructions to make Mac Daddy Salad:
  1. In large skillet over medium heat, combine beef, onion, garlic, salt and pepper. Cook until beef is browned through. Set aside and let cool.
  2. In large mixing bowl, combine lettuce, beef mixture, cheese and pickles. Toss to combine.
  3. Drizzle dressing on top and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
