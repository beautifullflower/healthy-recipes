The ingredients needed to make Hidden Surprise Tuna Deli Salad 🥗:
  1. Make ready 1 tin tuna, drained
  2. Make ready 1/2 small eating apple, peeled and finely diced
  3. Prepare 1/4 white onion, finely diced
  4. Make ready 1/2 stalk celery, finely diced
  5. Make ready 1 small gherkin/pickle, finely diced
  6. Make ready Pinch cayenne pepper
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp mayo
  8. Prepare 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
  9. Prepare Salt and pepper
  10. Get 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds and pine nuts to garnish
Steps to make Hidden Surprise Tuna Deli Salad 🥗:
  1. Add all of your ingredients except for the garnish to a bowl and mix well.
  2. Season to taste with salt and pepper then sprinkle with the seeds and pine nuts.
