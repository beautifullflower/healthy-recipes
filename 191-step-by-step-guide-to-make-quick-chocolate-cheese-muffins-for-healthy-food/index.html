The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Cheese Muffins:
  1. Make ready [FILLING]
  2. Take 200 g Cream cheese
  3. Take 40 g Sugar
  4. Prepare 1 Egg yolk
  5. Prepare 80 g Chocolate chips
  6. Get [WET]
  7. Get 3 Eggs
  8. Get 50 ml Milk
  9. Prepare 70 ml Olive oil (or any vegetable oil)
  10. Make ready 120 g Sugar
  11. Get [DRY]
  12. Take 150 g Plain flour
  13. Get 30 g Cocoa powder
  14. Take 1 tsp Baking powder
Steps to make Chocolate Cheese Muffins:
  1. [For Filling] This is ingredients for filling.
  2. Put cream cheese in a bowl and mix it with spoon until smooth. Add egg yolk and sugar and mix well.
  3. Add chocolate chips and mix well.
  4. [For Muffin] This is ingredients for muffin.
  5. Put all [WET] ingredients in a separate bowl and mix well.
  6. Shift all [DRY] ingredients into [5] and mix until there is no sign of flour.
  7. Start heating oven at 180℃.
  8. Put muffin mixture into muffin tin. Use ice cream scooper if you have, it's easy and you can put mixture evenly. (A full scoop for a muffin)
  9. Drop filling. (Bit less than muffin mixture, 80% of ice cream scooper for a muffin.)
  10. Bake them in 180℃ oven for 20 minutes. Enjoy lovely combination of chocolate and cream cheese!
