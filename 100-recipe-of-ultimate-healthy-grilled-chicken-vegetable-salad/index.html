The ingredients needed to make Healthy Grilled Chicken Vegetable Salad 🥗:
  1. Make ready 1 potato (boiled) cut lengthwise
  2. Prepare 2 zucchini (cut lengthwise)
  3. Make ready 1 carrot (slightly boiled) cut lengthwise
  4. Prepare 1 tomato (cut lengthwise)
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup cabbage chopped
  6. Take Broccoli as required
  7. Take 2 cup chicken pieces (already cooked)
  8. Get 2 tbsp mudroom (tin pack)
  9. Get 2 tbsp olive oil
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp white pepper powder
  12. Prepare 1/4 tsp Garlic powder
  13. Prepare 1/4 tsp lemon juice
  14. Prepare 1/4 tsp mixed Herb (optional)
Instructions to make Healthy Grilled Chicken Vegetable Salad 🥗:
  1. Arrange all the vegetables and roasted chicken. - https://cookpad.com/pk/recipes/14553829-roasted-chicken-with-vege?invite_token=LNcydnG9ayYuwzT5kmiXYSzp&amp;shared_at=1613656294
  2. Take a grill pan, add oil and vegetables and stir fry.
  3. Add all dry spices and mix well.
  4. Add chicken pieces and lemon juice and stir fry.
  5. Healthy Grilled Chicken Vegetable Salad is ready to serve.
