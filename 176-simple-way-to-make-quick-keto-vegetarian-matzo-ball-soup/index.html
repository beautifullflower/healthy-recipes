The ingredients needed to make Keto - Vegetarian Matzo Ball Soup:
  1. Make ready 5 large eggs
  2. Take 3 1/2 c. almond flour
  3. Make ready 1/2 c. ground flax seeds
  4. Make ready 1/4 c. seltzer water
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp. kosher salt
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp. olive oil divided
  7. Get 6 c. better than bullion vegetable broth
  8. Prepare 1 large carrot peeled and chopped
  9. Take 1 large celery stalk chopped
  10. Make ready 2 tbsp. flat leaf parsley roughly chopped
  11. Take 2 tbsp. dill weed roughly chopped
  12. Take 1 small onion diced
Steps to make Keto - Vegetarian Matzo Ball Soup:
  1. Whisk the eggs in a large bowl.
  2. Add the oil, water, salt, almond flour, and flax seed. Mix until even.
  3. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for two hours.
  4. Boil water in a large pot.
  5. Take the mixture from the refrigerator and using wet hands, roll the mixture into small balls, no larger than a ping pong ball (they will expand during cooking).
  6. Drop the balls into the boiling water one by one as you make them. Lower the flame and cook for 30 minutes.
  7. Drain the water and set aside.
  8. In large stock pot, add vegetable broth and remaining ingredients and let simmer on low heat for 2.5 hours
  9. Add matzo balls to warm as the final step & serve
