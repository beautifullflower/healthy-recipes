The ingredients needed to make Banana muffins:
  1. Take 2 cup self raising flour
  2. Prepare 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  3. Take 1/2 cup brown sugar
  4. Prepare 2 eggs
  5. Take 1/4 cup milk
  6. Take 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  7. Prepare 3 mashed ripe bananas
  8. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Prepare 1 tsp almond extract
Instructions to make Banana muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 200C
  2. Blend together flour, sugar and bicarbonate of soda.
  3. Add beaten eggs, milk and oil and the extracts. U can add sultanas, walnuts or mixed fruit
  4. Bake for 20-25minutes or until a toothpick inserted at the middle of muffin comes out clean
  5. Enjoy!
