The ingredients needed to make My Chicken Cucumber Lettuce Wraps:
  1. Get 1 (12.5 oz) can chunk chicken breast
  2. Make ready 1 head lettuce
  3. Get 6 green onions
  4. Make ready 1/3 cup mayo
  5. Take 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  6. Take 1 cucumber (scrape seeds out)
  7. Take 1 tsp Franks Red Hot Sauce
  8. Take 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  9. Take 1/4 tsp celery seed
  10. Take 1/4 tsp Pink Himalayan salt (optional)
  11. Get 1/4 cup mayo if you want to add more at the end
Instructions to make My Chicken Cucumber Lettuce Wraps:
  1. Wash lettuce leaves. About 5 to 6 leaves.
  2. Drain chicken juice from can.
  3. In a medium bowl add chicken, onions and mayo
  4. Add Frank's Red Hot Sauce and all the spices.
  5. Cut cucumber in small chunks
  6. Mix all ingredients together. Put chicken mixture in wraps and roll them up.
