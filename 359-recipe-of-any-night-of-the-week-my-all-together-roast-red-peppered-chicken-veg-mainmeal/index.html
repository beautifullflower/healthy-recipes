The ingredients needed to make My All together Roast. Red Peppered Chicken + Veg. #Mainmeal:
  1. Prepare 1 Small Fresh Chicken
  2. Take 2 large Carrots cut in half along the length then half accross
  3. Take 6 Florets fresh Broccoli
  4. Get 2 Large Sweet Potatoes cut in half. Washed and leave skin on
  5. Take 1 Lemon
  6. Take Coating Chicken….
  7. Prepare 2 tsp Dried Red Peppers
  8. Make ready 2 tsp Oil
  9. Take Gravy………..
  10. Make ready The juices left after chicken is cooked
  11. Make ready 1 chicken stock cube
  12. Take 1 tsp Corn flour mixed in 3 tbls cold water
Instructions to make My All together Roast. Red Peppered Chicken + Veg. #Mainmeal:
  1. Mix the oil and red pepper in a bowl and mix add a little salt
  2. Coat all over the Top of the chicken and sides. Then push the whole lemon inside the chicken.
  3. Grease an oven dish with a lid or cover with foil then lay the chicken in the middle. Add the veg all around
  5. Cover the Top and add to a preheated oven 180 ¬∞C for 45 minutes.
  6. Take out the oven and remove the veg and set aside. Baste The Chicken with the juices and add back in the oven for 20 minutes without the lid and turn heat down to 160¬∞C.
  7. When done rest the chicken then carve off what you need, add the veg to a microwave and warm up and serve.
  8. Gravy…………. If you want it collect the juices in a pan. This gravy is so nice with the flavours of lemon and red pepper in the juices.
  9. Add the water and stir in the chicken stock cube.
  10. Add to the heat and stir until boiling. Add the cornflour to the cold water and mix in.
  11. Pour the cornflour in the gravy stirring it will start thickening. Add another 1/2 tsp cornflour to little cold water and add that for thickness.
