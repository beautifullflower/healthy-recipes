The ingredients needed to make Chocolate marshmallow muffins:
  1. Take 250 g plain flour
  2. Get 150 g sugar (brown sugar for a richer flavour)
  3. Prepare 4 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 50 g cocoa powder
  5. Make ready 75 g dark or white chocolate drops or chopped chocolate pieces
  6. Get 50 g mini marshmallows
  7. Take 175 ml milk
  8. Prepare 100 ml oil
  9. Prepare 2 medium eggs
  10. Get 1/2 tsp vanilla essence
Steps to make Chocolate marshmallow muffins:
  1. Mix together dry ingredients. Create a well in the middle and pour in wet ingredients. Fold in until well combined.
  2. Stir in mini marshmallows. Fill paper cases almost full.
  3. Put into a preheated oven at 180C for 20 minutes or until springy to the touch and a toothpick inserted into the centre comes out clean.
  4. Cool on a wire rack. Enjoy.
