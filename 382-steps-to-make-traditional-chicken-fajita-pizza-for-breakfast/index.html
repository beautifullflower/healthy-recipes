The ingredients needed to make Chicken Fajita Pizza:
  1. Prepare 1 cup leftover chicken chunks
  2. Take 200 gm pizza dough
  3. Get 1 cup Prepared pizza sauce
  4. Take 1 capsicum julienne cut
  5. Take 1 onion julienne cut
  6. Make ready 1 tomato julienne cut
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup olives
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup mushroom
  9. Make ready 200 gm or more cheese
Instructions to make Chicken Fajita Pizza:
  1. You can prepare the dough and sauce from the links above.
  2. Set the dough on the lined pizza pan with hands. Use a drop of oil if needed.
  3. Now top with pizza sauce, vegetables, chicken chunks and cheese.
  4. Bake in a pre heated oven for about 15-20 mins.
  5. Serve immediately with drink and enjoy !!
