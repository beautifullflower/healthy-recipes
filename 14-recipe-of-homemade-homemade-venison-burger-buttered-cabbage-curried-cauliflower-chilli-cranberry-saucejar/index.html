The ingredients needed to make Homemade Venison Burger

Buttered Cabbage Curried Cauliflower Chilli Cranberry Sauce(Jar):

  1. Take 500 gm lean Venison mince
  2. Get 1 tsp Garlic powder
  3. Get 1 tsp dried thyme
  4. Make ready 1 tsp onion powder
  5. Get S&P
  6. Get 1 slice Mozzarella cheese
  7. Get 2 slices cheddar cheese
  8. Prepare 1 cauliflower
  9. Prepare 1 cumin
  10. Make ready 1 tsp thyme
  11. Prepare 1 tsp turmeric
  12. Prepare 1 tsp coriander seeds
  13. Get 1 tsp curry powder of choice (madras)
  14. Take Lemon Olive oil
  15. Prepare 1 cabbage
  16. Prepare Butter
  17. Make ready 1 tsp Cranberry Chilli jam
Instructions to make Homemade Venison Burger

Buttered Cabbage Curried Cauliflower Chilli Cranberry Sauce(Jar):

  1. Mix all ingredients from first section to make the venison pate. Form the burger shape
  2. Prep Cauliflower & mix with curry ingredients. Roast for 30mins
  3. Prep cabbage & sautÃ© for 10/15 mins till tender
  4. Pan fry burgers 10 mins on each side. Add cheese & cover. Cook till melted
  5. Serve with Cranberry jam
