The ingredients needed to make Chicken bacon ranch & buffalo chicken pizza 🍕 🍗 🥓 🍄 🌶:
  1. Get 1 can pizza crust (cut in half)
  2. Get For the buffalo chicken:
  3. Make ready 1 cup cooked shredded chicken
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
  5. Get 2 ounces mozzarella cheese
  6. Make ready 2 ounces Colby jack cheese
  7. Take 1/3 cup diced purple onion
  8. Make ready Italian herbs
  9. Get For the chicken bacon ranch:
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  11. Take 4 ounces mozzarella
  12. Prepare 1/3 cup diced purple onion
  13. Make ready 1/3 cup diced mushrooms
  14. Make ready 2 bacon strips cooked and crumbled
  15. Make ready 1 cup cooked shredded chicken
  16. Make ready Italian herbs
Steps to make Chicken bacon ranch & buffalo chicken pizza 🍕 🍗 🥓 🍄 🌶:
  1. Preheat oven to 425. Open the pizza dough and cut it in half.
  2. Prepare each pizza starting with sauce then toppings then cheese and Italian herbs. Drizzle the buffalo one with more sauce. Bake 14-15 min. Toss under broiler for 2 minutes to make the cheese speckled and bubbly.
