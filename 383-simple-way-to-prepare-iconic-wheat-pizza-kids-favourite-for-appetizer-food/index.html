The ingredients needed to make Wheat pizza kids favourite:
  1. Make ready For pizza dough–
  2. Get 1/2 cup warm water
  3. Take 1 Tsp sugar
  4. Make ready 1 Tsp dry yeast
  5. Take 2 cup whole wheat flour/ aata
  6. Get to taste Salt
  7. Take 3 tbsp oil
  8. Prepare As required Water
  9. Prepare For toppings:
  10. Make ready 3 tbsp pizza sauce
  11. Get 1/2 cup mushrooms, thin sliced
  12. Take 1/2 cup onions roughly chopped
  13. Make ready 1/4 cup capsicum, roughly chopped
  14. Make ready 6-7 jalapeno(optional)
  15. Make ready 1/4 cup sweet corn
  16. Take 1/4 black olives (optional)
  17. Take 1/2 cup mozerella cheese grated
  18. Take 1/2 tsp oregano/ Italian seasoning/ mixed herbs
Steps to make Wheat pizza kids favourite:
  1. Firstly, in a large mixing bowl take warm water, sugar and dry yeast and give a quick mix.
  2. Allow it to rest for 5 minutes, - now add maida, salt and olive oil. - knead the dough adding water as required.
  3. Tuck the dough and form to a ball. place in a large mixing bowl. Cover it with cling rap and let it rest and rise in a warm place for about 2 hours.
  4. Punch the dough with fist to release down the air.
  5. Now place the dough on dusted pizza plate or tray,flatten dough by stretching with both hands.
  6. Further, leaving a cm or more and create a dent, then prick with centre of dough with the help of fork, dough will look like this, you can get it easily from the market.
  7. Furthermore, spread pizza sauce generously leaving the sides slightly.
  8. Also top with topping of choice.
  9. Then spread grated cheese and sprinkle italian seasonings.
  10. Further, bake at 220¬∞c for about 12 - 15 minutes.
  11. Finally, slice and serve pizza hot.
