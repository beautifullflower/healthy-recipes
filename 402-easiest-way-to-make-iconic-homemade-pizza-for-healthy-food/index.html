The ingredients needed to make Homemade pizza:
  1. Get Dough
  2. Take 2 cups flour
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cup warm water
  4. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 2 tsp sugar
  6. Get 1 tsp yeast
  7. Take 2 tbs cooking oil
  8. Prepare Topping
  9. Make ready Mushrooms
  10. Get Robot peppers
  11. Get Mixed herbs
  12. Make ready Grated cheese
  13. Get Chicken breast
  14. Get Tomatoes
Instructions to make Homemade pizza:
  1. Mix your warm water with yeast,salt and sugar leave for five minutes before adding the flour. After leave dough to rise fo two hours.
  2. Punch your dough and shape and the prick holes with a fork. Smear tomato paste on the dough and add ur toppings
  3. Bake for 20 minutes at 200°
