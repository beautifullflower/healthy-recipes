The ingredients needed to make Triple Chocolate Muffin:
  1. Take 125 gram sugar
  2. Get 1 egg
  3. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Get 125 ml milk
  5. Make ready 50 gram melted butter
  6. Make ready 25 ml vegetable oil
  7. Prepare 100 gram dark chocolate, chopped roughly
  8. Make ready Choco chips for garnish
  9. Take Dry ingredient
  10. Take 120 gram plain flour
  11. Prepare 25 gram Cocoa powder
  12. Take 25 gram milk powder
  13. Take 1 tbsp corn flour/maizena
  14. Get 1 tsp baking powder
Steps to make Triple Chocolate Muffin:
  1. Sift all dry ingredients. Set aside.
  2. In large bowl, mix sugar and egg. Wisk until sugar dissolved. Add salt, butter, and oil. Mix well.
  3. Add dry ingredients, mix and then add dark chocolate into mixture. Don't over mix. Just until all ingredients combined.
  4. Pour the batter to muffin mould. Just 2/3 part from mould, sprinkle with chocochip and bake in preheated oven (180°C) for 25 - 30 minutes.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
