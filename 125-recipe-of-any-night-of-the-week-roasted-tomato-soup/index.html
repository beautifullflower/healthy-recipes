The ingredients needed to make Roasted Tomato Soup:
  1. Get 8-10 tomatoes medium size
  2. Get 6-8 garlic cloves
  3. Get 1 onion
  4. Get 1 tsp olive oil
  5. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Get 1/4 tsp Black salt
  7. Get 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  8. Get 1 tsp chilli flakes
  9. Take 1 tsp parsley
  10. Make ready to taste sugar
  11. Make ready 1 tsp peanuts
  12. Make ready 1 tsp roasted crushed peanuts
  13. Take 2 tsp cream
  14. Get 1 cup water
Steps to make Roasted Tomato Soup:
  1. Peel the skin of all tomatoes cut all into 4 to 6 pieces.
  2. Peel the garlic and crush.
  3. Chop the onion.
  4. Now take a nonstick pan and oil after hot add garlic saute it, now add onion saute it.
  5. Now add chopped tomatoes pieces in it roast for 5 to 7 minutes.now add water cover with lid and cook for 15 minutes on medium flame.
  6. Now cool it and add raw peanut in it make paste in mixer grinder.
  7. Now take a pan add lil oil पूरे this paste add spices and roasted peanuts lil water cook for 2 minutes.
  8. Add 1 tbs cream in it.
  9. Serve hot by garnish with cream it's healthy and yummy soup.
