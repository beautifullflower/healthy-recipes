  5. Prepare 2 mushrooms
  6. Get 2-3 tablespoon pizza sauce
  7. Get to taste Salt
  8. Make ready As needed Mozzarella Cheese
  9. Take 1-2 tsp dried oregano
  10. Get 2 tbsp vegetable oil
