Instructions to make Pizza on stick:
  1. In a pan heat oil and add chicken, bell pepper, garlic powder, chilli sauce, salt and oregano. Cook it well
  2. Cut red bell pepper, green capsicum and onion into cubes.
  3. Take skewers and thread it with chicken, bell pepper, capsicum, mushroom and onion.
  4. Roll dough and cut strips of it.
  5. Spread pizza sauce (any brand you can buy, I have used Dr.Oetker Funfoods pasta and pizza sauce) on strips and wrap it around the skewers. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on it. Top it with olives (optional).
  6. Bake at 180 degrees for 10-12 mins.
  7. Serve hot with ketchup.
