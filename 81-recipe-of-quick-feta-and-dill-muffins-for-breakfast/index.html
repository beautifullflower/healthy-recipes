The ingredients needed to make Feta and Dill muffins:
  1. Make ready 50 g whole-wheat flour
  2. Make ready 175 g plain white flour
  3. Take 2.5 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  5. Take 275 ml whole milk
  6. Get 1 egg
  7. Make ready 100 g cottage cheese
  8. Prepare 75 g unsalted butter, melted
  9. Take 50 g feta cheese, crumbled
  10. Make ready 0.5 tsp whole grain mustard
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  12. Prepare 1 tsp ground black pepper
  13. Make ready 8 sundried tomatoes, chopped
  14. Make ready 25 g pumpkin seeds
Instructions to make Feta and Dill muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees Celcius. Line a 12 muffin tin with cases
  2. Whisk together the flours, bp and salt in a bowl. Add the milk, egg, cottage cheese, melted butter, feta cheese, mustard, dill, and black pepper. Mix all together and spoon into the muffin cases. Top each muffin with a little sundried tomato and pumpkin seeds
  3. Bake in the oven for 20-25 minutes till browned, or until a skewer inserted comes out clean
