The ingredients needed to make Pizza bread a.k.a. Zapiekanka:
  1. Get Bread, rolls or baguette
  2. Take Base
  3. Prepare 300 g mushrooms
  4. Get 350 g tomatoes
  5. Make ready Toppings
  6. Prepare 250 g Mozzarella
  7. Make ready Ham or salami
  8. Get Ketchup
  9. Make ready Spices
  10. Take Salt
  11. Make ready Pepper
  12. Prepare Garlic
  13. Prepare Oregano
  14. Take Basil
  15. Make ready Tools
  16. Make ready 2 frying pans
  17. Take Oven
Instructions to make Pizza bread a.k.a. Zapiekanka:
  1. Prepare ingredients.
  2. Chop tomatoes to very small pieces. Then add salt, pepper, basil and garlic. Mix everything well.
  3. Chop mushrooms to form you like. Add salt, pepper and garlic.
  4. Heat two frying pans and add butter. Then put tomatoes to one and mushrooms to another.
  5. Fry tomatoes until they will lose a water and sauce will get thick. After that you can blend it if you want but you don’t have to.
  6. Also fry mushrooms until the will turn dark and they will lose water.
  7. Heat the oven to 180°C with fan turned on.
  8. Cut baguette in half and put tomatoes base and mushrooms on it
  9. Finish with the toppings you like. I used grated mozzarella and salami.
  10. Put everything to oven for 7-10 min.
  11. Sprinkle with oregano. We serve this with ketchup. Enjoy!
