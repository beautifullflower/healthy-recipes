The ingredients needed to make Miso & Mushroom Pizza:
  1. Make ready 1 Pizza Base *I used 28cm base
  2. Make ready 100 g Mushrooms *thinly sliced
  3. Make ready *Note: Today I used common Button Mushrooms and Shiitake Mushrooms
  4. Get 100 g Mozzarella Cheese OR Pizza Mix *shredded or torn into small pieces
  5. Take 1/2 Spring Onion *finely chopped
  6. Get Miso Sauce
  7. Get 1 & 1/3 tablespoons (20ml) Miso *dark colour Miso recommended
  8. Make ready 1 tablespoon Mirin
  9. Get 1 pinch Ground Chilli *optional
Steps to make Miso & Mushroom Pizza:
  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C.
  2. Combine Miso and Mirin in a small bowl. Thinly spread the Miso sauce over the Pizza Base. Scatter thinly sliced Mushrooms over the Pizza Base evenly. Add Mozzarella (OR Pizza Mix).
  3. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until nicely browned. Sprinkle with finely chopped Spring Onion and enjoy.
