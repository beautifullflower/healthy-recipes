The ingredients needed to make Banana and pecan muffins:
  1. Get 150 g Banana
  2. Make ready 70 g Unsalted butter
  3. Make ready 40 g Caster sugar
  4. Make ready 35 g Honey
  5. Take 1 teaspoon Vanilla paste
  6. Take 120 g Plain flour
  7. Take 30 g Corn flour
  8. Prepare 6 g Baking powder
  9. Make ready 50 g Pecan
  10. Take Salt 1 tiny pinch
Steps to make Banana and pecan muffins:
  1. Mix softened butter with caster sugar.
  2. Add in honey and mix it well with butter.
  3. Add in the vanilla paste.
  4. Add in one egg.
  5. Mashed the banana and then add it into the butter bowl.
  6. Sieve all the flours and mix them gently with the mashed banana and butter.
  7. Add in the pecan nuts. (Keep some nuts to sprinkle on the top later)
  8. Divide the muffin batter into the baking tray.
  9. Bake in 170°C oven for 25-28 minutes. Let them stay in the tray for 5 minutes after being removed from the oven. Then take those muffins out and put them on the cooling rack to let them cool down. Enjoy.
