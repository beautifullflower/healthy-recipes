The ingredients needed to make Veg Tawa Pizza:
  1. Prepare For Pizza base
  2. Make ready 1 cup Maida
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup Ata
  4. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  5. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Take 1 Tbsp oil
  7. Take 3 Tbsp yogurt
  8. Get Salt
  9. Make ready Water to knead the dough
  10. Prepare For Toppings
  11. Take 3-4 mushrooms sliced
  12. Prepare Green capsicum cubed
  13. Get Yellow capsicum cubed
  14. Prepare Red capsicum cubed
  15. Get 2-3 green olives sliced
  16. Take 1-2 black olives sliced
  17. Take 1-2 garlic cloves sliced fine
  18. Get 1 medium size onion sliced fine
  19. Take 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  20. Prepare 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  21. Prepare leaves Few basil
  22. Prepare 1 Tbsp olive oil
  23. Make ready Mozzarella cheese grated
  24. Take For Tomato sauce
  25. Prepare 2-3 garlic cloves sliced
  26. Get 1 large tomato purée
  27. Take 1 medium size onion sliced fine
  28. Prepare 1 Tbsp olive oil
  29. Make ready 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  30. Get 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  31. Take 1 Tbsp vinegar
  32. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  33. Prepare Salt
Steps to make Veg Tawa Pizza:
  1. For pizza dough - in a bowl add Maida, Ata. In a small bowl mix baking soda, baking powder and oil and add to the bowl of Ata & Maida, add salt, whisked yogurt and mix well.
  2. Make a soft dough with adequate water and give it to rest for 1 hour
  3. For Toppings - in a wok heat olive oil and add sliced onion, cubed mushrooms, cubed capsicums, olives and sauté on low flame for 1-2 mins. Add chilli flakes, oregano, basil leaves and stir. Set aside
  4. For tomato base - in the same wok heat olive oil, add sliced garlic and onions, stir until translucent. Add tomato purée, vinegar, chilli flakes, oregano, sugar and salt. Stir
  5. On low flame let the tomato base dry a little. Set aside.
  6. To make the pizza - on the kitchen top drizzle Maida flour a little and by pressing ones fingers make a base. Pizza base cannot be rolled with a rolling pin. Prick with a fork all through the surface
  7. Place the pan on heat and on low flame place the dough base. Cover and let it cook for 2-3 mins
  8. Flip the side and apply the tomato base, all vegetables for toppings, basil leaves, olives, mozzarella cheese grated, dried oregano, few chilli flakes, more grated mozzarella and on low flame covered let it cook until the cheese melts.
  9. Set aside, drizzle olive oil and plate sliced. Enjoy
