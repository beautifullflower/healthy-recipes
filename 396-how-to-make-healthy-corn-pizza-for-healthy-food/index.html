To get started with this particular recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can cook corn pizza using 12 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you cook it.

The ingredients needed to make Corn pizza:
  1. Get 1 Pizza base
  2. Take 3 tbsp Pizza sauce
  3. Take 2 tbsp Schezwan sauce
  4. Get 1 big onion thin sliced
  5. Get To taste Salt
  6. Get 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  7. Get 1/2 tsp oregano
  8. Get 1 capsicum
  9. Get 1/2 cup mushroom
  10. Prepare 1 big tomato
  11. Take 1/2 cup corn
  12. Take as needed Mozzarella cheese
Steps to make Corn pizza:
  1. First will take pizza base and spread over Pizza sauce or schezwan sauce.(I mixed both and spread)….I made Pizza sauce at home.
  2. Now arrange the veggies of your choice.. I have used capsicum, onions, tomato, corn and mushroom.
  3. Add the grated mozzarella cheese on top of the veggies and leave it in the microwave pan around 30-45 min.(depend which company microwave we are using).
