The ingredients needed to make Cheese Burst Pizza with leftover Chappatis:
  1. Get 2 left over chappatis
  2. Prepare As required Pizza sauce or Tomato ketchup
  3. Get 100 grams Mozzarella cheese
  4. Prepare As required Vegetables of your choice like
  5. Prepare 1 small sized Finely chopped onions
  6. Prepare Few green capsicum finely chopped
  7. Get Few red and yellow finely chopped
  8. Take 1 small sized finely chopped tomato
  9. Prepare 3-4 black olives
  10. Take 2 tablespoons corns
  11. Prepare 3-4 Mushrooms
  12. Prepare Little Oregano
  13. Prepare 1 tsp Pizza herbs
  14. Get As per taste Red Chilli flakes
Instructions to make Cheese Burst Pizza with leftover Chappatis:
  1. Take one chappati. Spread little mozzarella cheese as shown in pic. Put another chappati above it and press it.
  2. Spread Pizza sauce or tomato ketchup.
  3. Arrange all veggies above it.
  4. Spread Mozzarella cheese on the top.
  5. Sprinkle red chilli flakes, oregano, pizza herbs and cover it.
  6. Heat non stick pan and put this pizza in it and cover it. Let the cheese melts. Lower the flame.
  7. When done remove it and cut it with pizza cutter.
  8. Serve it hot with tomato ketchup. As chappatis in between has cheese, your cheesy pizza is ready.
