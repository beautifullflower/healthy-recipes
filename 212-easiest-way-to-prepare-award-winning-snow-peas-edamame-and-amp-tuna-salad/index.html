The ingredients needed to make Snow Peas, Edamame & Tuna Salad:
  1. Prepare 150 g Snow Peas
  2. Make ready 1 cup Frozen Edamame Beans *OR Frozen Peas
  3. Get Salt & Black Pepper
  4. Prepare 1/6-1/4 Red Onion
  5. Get 1 can (95 g) Tuna *I used Tuna in Springwater, drained
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup Japanese Mayonnaise
  7. Make ready 2-3 teaspoons Lemon Juice
Steps to make Snow Peas, Edamame & Tuna Salad:
  1. Wash Snow Peas and slice diagonally into strips. Place in a mixing bowl, add Edamame Beans OR Peas. Today I used both. You don‚Äôt need to thaw them.
  2. Season with Salt and Black Pepper and mix to combine. Add all other ingredients and mix well.
