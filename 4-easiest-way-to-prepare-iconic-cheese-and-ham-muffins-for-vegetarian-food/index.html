The ingredients needed to make Cheese And Ham Muffins:
  1. Prepare 225 g self-raising flour, sifted
  2. Make ready 160 g chedder cheese, grated
  3. Take 175 g whole milk
  4. Prepare 60 g ham slices, roughly chopped
  5. Prepare 1 egg
  6. Prepare 50 ml olive oil
Instructions to make Cheese And Ham Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 200c.
  2. Add muffin cases to a 12 muffin mould or brush the moulds with oil.
  3. In a large bowl add the flour, 120g of the cheese and the ham. Mix it all together.
  4. In a different bowl whisk together the milk, egg and olive oil. Add to the flour and stir to combine.
  5. The batter should be quite thick and easily picked up with a tablespoon.
  6. Divide the batter into 10- 12 moulds and top each one with the remaining cheese.
  7. Bake for around 20 minutes or until golden brown and fully cooked through.
  8. Serve warm or cold.
