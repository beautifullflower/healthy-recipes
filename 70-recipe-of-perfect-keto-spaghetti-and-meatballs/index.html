The ingredients needed to make Keto Spaghetti and Meatballs:
  1. Make ready Noodles
  2. Prepare 2 oz Cream Cheese
  3. Make ready 4 large Eggs
  4. Prepare Spaghetti Meatballs
  5. Make ready 1 jar Rao’s Pasta Sauce
  6. Get 1 lb Hamburger
Instructions to make Keto Spaghetti and Meatballs:
  1. Preheat oven to 325
  2. Mix cream cheese with eggs in blender
  3. Pour mixture into 2 greased cookie sheets (1/2 of mixture in each pan)
  4. Place pans in oven is as level as possible (to ensure equal thickness noodles)
  5. Bake for 8 minutes
  6. Set aside pans to cool
  7. Roll hamburger into 16 balls
  8. Cook on medium until meatballs have browned
  9. Add sauce to meatball pan and cook on medium-low until sauce is warm
  10. Remove sheets of noodle mix from the pans and place on cutting board
  11. Cut sheets of noodle mix into linguine sized strips (pizza cutter works best)
  12. Mix noodles together with sauce and meatballs
