The ingredients needed to make Omelette Muffins:
  1. Take 8 Egg
  2. Make ready 35 ml Heavy cream/milk (any)
  3. Prepare 15 gm Nutmeg
  4. Make ready Salt & Black pepper
  5. Take Fillings (your favourite choice)
  6. Prepare 1 Brown onion
  7. Take 15 gm Garlic minced
  8. Make ready 2 strips Bacon
  9. Take 1 Capsicum
  10. Prepare 4 bunches Kale
  11. Get 50 gm Tasty cheddar cheese
Steps to make Omelette Muffins:
  1. Prepare heat over to 170 C
  2. Whisk the eggs, heavy cream, salt & pepper and nutmeg
  3. Chop onion, capsicum, bacon and kale
  4. Add garlic minced and cook all ingredients until golden soften; then set aside
  5. Butter or oil a non- stick muffin tin and place omelette fillings in each muffin tin
  6. Pour egg mixture into each muffin tin
  7. Bake at 170 C for 15 minutes or until fully cooked
