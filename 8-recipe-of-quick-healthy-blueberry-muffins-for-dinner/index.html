The ingredients needed to make Healthy Blueberry Muffins:
  1. Get 1 cup fresh whole blueberry
  2. Get 100 g plain flour
  3. Take 125 g applesauce (I used a homemade sauce)
  4. Prepare Pinch salt
  5. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Prepare 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Make ready 1 egg
  8. Prepare 60 ml milk
  9. Make ready 60 ml vegetable oil
Steps to make Healthy Blueberry Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 200c.
  2. Mix all the ingredients together until the mixture is smooth (except the blueberries).
  3. Mix in the the blueberries.
  4. Add muffin cases to a 12 muffin tray.
  5. Gently spoon the mixture into each muffin case. It'll give you 12 muffins.
  6. Make the muffins for 20-25 minutes or until baked through.
  7. Allow to cool before serving.
