The ingredients needed to make Baked oats medallions over nut apple donuts:
  1. Take 2 pc apple, cored and sliced like donuts
  2. Get 3-6 tbsp almond butter
  3. Make ready Dry ingredients
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup rolled oats
  5. Get 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
  6. Take 1/8 cup dried cranberries / prunes (chopped), or trail mix
  7. Take Wet ingredients

  9. Prepare 1/8 cup water
  10. Get 2 tbsp honey
  11. Get Optional ingredients

Instructions to make Baked oats medallions over nut apple donuts:
  1. Prepare baking pan/sheet/tray and line it with baking paper. Set it aside. Preheat oven (w/o baking tray), about 375Â°F | 190Â°C for about 10-20mins.
  2. On one large bowl, combine all dry ingredients [[and optional ingredients should you want them added – yes, you can add 'em all, but adjust wet ingredients with addition 1-2 tbsp water plus 1 tsp ground flaxseed]]. Set aside.
  3. On a medium bowl, combine all wet ingredients, and coarsely mash the banana at the same time. [[If you add all optional ingredients add 1-2 tbsp water + 1 tsp ground flaxseed]].
  4. Combine wet to dry ingredients. Whisk and mix well.
  5. Spoon mixture unto baking tray with baking paper. Flatten /form a dollop into a medallion. One dollop makes one medallion, and sizes doesn't have to be in uniform.
