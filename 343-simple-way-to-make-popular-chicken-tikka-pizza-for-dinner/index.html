The ingredients needed to make Chicken tikka pizza:
  1. Make ready Pizza dough
  2. Get Pizza sauce
  3. Make ready 1 mug small chicken in cubes
  4. Get 2.5 tbsp Tikka masala
  5. Take 1 tbsp oregano
  6. Prepare 250 gm capsicum
  7. Take 1 large onion
  8. Prepare 4-5 tbsp oil
  9. Prepare Mushrooms as required
  10. Take Black olives as required
  11. Get Mozzarella cheese as required
  12. Get Cheddar cheese as required
Steps to make Chicken tikka pizza:
  1. First marinate tikka masala on Chicken for 15 minutes then, take a pan add oil and chicken then cook chicken on medium low heat then add onions, capsicum and fry it lightly, then at the end add some black olives and oregano
  2. Take a pizza dough spread it in the oven tray then spread pizza sauce on it, then add cooked chicken and vegetables, add some mushrooms slices and both cheeses on the the top.
  3. Then bake the pizza on 180¬∞ for 20 minutes or until dough become golden brown and cheese get completely melt.
