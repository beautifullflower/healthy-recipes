The ingredients needed to make Roasted Asparagus with Mozzarella cheese: Keto approves:
  1. Prepare Bundle fresh asparagus
  2. Prepare 4 garlic cloves, minced
  3. Prepare 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  4. Get Salt/pepper
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  6. Get 3-4 tablespoons avocado oil or extra virgin olive oil
Instructions to make Roasted Asparagus with Mozzarella cheese: Keto approves:
  1. Wash and dry vegetable. Snap asparagus towards the end. It will naturally separate the stringy non edible portion of the stalk. Line on baking sheet and add olive oil, salt and pepper and toss.
  2. Mince garlic and add to asparagus. Bake 350 degrees until crispy to your liking. (I like mine extra crispy) Next add mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Place back into oven until cheese melts.
