The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Sauce:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup Unsweetened Peanut Butter
  2. Prepare 4 cloves Garlic
  3. Get 1 tbsp Chopped Ginger
  4. Take 2 tbsp Chilli Sauce
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp Honey
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  7. Prepare Salt (according to taste)
  8. Get 1/2-1 cup Water
Steps to make Peanut Butter Sauce:
  1. Add all ingredients, except water, into a food blender and blend it for a 30 seconds. Note - Start with adding little salt and add more after blending only if required.
  2. A thick paste would be formed. Add some water (1/4 cup) and blend again. Keep adding water and blending according to the desired consistency. I put around 1/3 cup, that’s why thick.
  3. After getting the required consistency, pour the sauce into a jar and use it as a salad dressing.
