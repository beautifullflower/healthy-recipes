The ingredients needed to make Keto Taco Salad:
  1. Make ready Ground turkey
  2. Get Bell peppers
  3. Get Spinach
  4. Get Hard boiled eggs
  5. Get Mushrooms
  6. Get 1 Avocado
  7. Get 1/2 onion
Instructions to make Keto Taco Salad:
  1. Gently brown the turkey meat in medium size saucepan and/or cast-iron skillet
  2. Prep bowls/plates with choice of greens, vegetables and eggs. Cut avocado in half and remove seed. Split between bowls.
  3. Once meat is finished cooking, add ingredients together in bowls and toss (with or without dressing). Top with sour cream, cheese and hot sauce as you please. Enjoy!!!
