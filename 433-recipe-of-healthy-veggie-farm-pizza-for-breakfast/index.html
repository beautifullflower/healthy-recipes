The ingredients needed to make Veggie farm Pizza:
  1. Get 1 Pizza base
  2. Get 1 tbsp pizza sauce (homemade)
  3. Take As required Mozrella cheese
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp Chopped Onion rings
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp Chopped tomato rings
  6. Get 2 tbsp Baby corns sliced
  7. Take As required Mushroom diced
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp Sweet corn kernels
  9. Prepare 2/3 tbsp Capsicum juilins
  10. Take 2 tsp Seasoning mix
  11. Prepare 1 tsp Black pepper powder
Steps to make Veggie farm Pizza:
  1. Slightly roast pizza base on tawa to make it's crust crispy.
  2. Spread pizza sauce on the base evenly. Spread grated cheese over it.
  3. Layer the veggies on the cheese. Onions,tomatoes first. Baby corns to fill between to rings and corn Mushrooms in the ring gaps. Capsicum in between.
  4. Add grated cheese over again. Sprinkle pepper powder, seasoning mix, chilli flakes(optional) and grill for 4 mins in microwave.
  5. Cut it and serve…
