Steps to make Moms quick pizza:
  1. Mix flour (both refined / wheat and rice), salt, and milk to make a batter (pancake-like consistency).
  2. Brush little oil on a non-stick frying pan and let it cook for 1 min to form the pizza base
  3. Once the base is ready, add the veggies, preferred toppings, cheese slices,s, and seasoning.
  4. Cover and cook for 2 to 3 mins until the veggies are cooked. Alternately put in the microwave for 1 min.
  5. Add seasoning on top and serve.
