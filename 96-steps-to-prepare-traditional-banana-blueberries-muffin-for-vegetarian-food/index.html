The ingredients needed to make Banana Blueberries Muffin:
  1. Make ready 195 grams all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 150 grams granulated sugar, plus 1 tbsp for muffin tops
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp fine sea salt (am using normal salt)
  4. Get 2 tbsp baking powder
  5. Take 80 ml vegetable oil
  6. Make ready 120 ml milk
  7. Take 1 large egg
  8. Take 2 ripe bananas, mashed with a fork
  9. Take 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  10. Get 6-8 ounces fresh blueberries
Steps to make Banana Blueberries Muffin:
  1. Pre heat oven to 200 C. For big topped muffins, line 8 standard size muffin cups with paper liners. Fill the remaining cups with 1 to 2 tablespoons of water to help the muffins bake evenly.
  2. Whisk the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in a large bowl.
  3. In another bowl, whisk the oil, mashed banana, egg, milk and vanilla until blended.
  4. Add the banana mixture to the bowl with dry ingredients then use a fork to combine. Fold in the blueberries.
  5. Divide the batter between muffin cups. Sprinkle a little sugar on top of each muffin.
  6. Bake muffins 15 to 20 minutes remaining until tops are no longer wet and a toothpick inserted into the middle of a muffin comes out with crumbs, not wet batter.
  7. Transfer to a cooling rack.
