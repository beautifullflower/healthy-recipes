The ingredients needed to make Pizza skewers:
  1. Get 300 g Chicken boneless
  2. Take 1 tbsp Oil
  3. Get to taste Salt
  4. Take 1/2 tsp Black pepper powder
  5. Take 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
  6. Take 1-2 tbsp Chilli sauce
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp Herbs
  8. Take 1 Red bell pepper
  9. Make ready 1 Green capsicum
  10. Get 1 large Onion
  11. Get 6-8 Mushrooms
  12. Get Dough recipe
  13. Prepare 1/2 kg flour
  14. Take 1 tbsp Yeast
  15. Get 1/2 cup Milk
  16. Get 1 tbsp Sugar
  17. Get 1/2 tbsp Salt
  18. Make ready 1 Egg
  19. Get 3 tbsp Oil
Instructions to make Pizza skewers:
  1. In a pan heat oil and add chicken, black pepper, garlic powder, chilli sauce, salt and herbs. And cook it well.
  2. Cut red bell pepper, green capsicum and onion into cubes.
  3. Take skewers and thread it with chicken, bell pepper, capsicum, mushroom and onion.
  4. Roll dough and cut strips of it.
  5. Spread pizza sauce on strips and wrap it around the skewers.
  6. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on it.
  7. Bake at 180 degree for 10-12 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with ketchup.
