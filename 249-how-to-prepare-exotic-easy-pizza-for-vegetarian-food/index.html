The ingredients needed to make Easy pizza:
  1. Prepare 300 g white flour
  2. Get 4 small tomatoes
  3. Get 300 g mushrooms
  4. Get 3/4 cup water
  5. Make ready 1 medium onion
  6. Take 4 cloves garlic
  7. Make ready 2 tablespoons basil
  8. Prepare 3 tablespoons tomato puree
  9. Make ready 1 vegetable stock cube
  10. Make ready 2 tablespoons olive oil
  11. Prepare 200 g mozarella cheese

Instructions to make Easy pizza:
  1. Get the flour (300 g or 1.5 cups)
  2. Add some oil, pepper, salt and basil to the flour, then add water while stirring it in with a spoon
  3. Soon you will have your dough, just knead it a bit now
  4. Now make the sauce
  5. Chop and stir fry the onion and garlic. Then chop and add the tomatoes and the mushrooms.
  6. After a while add the stock cube and the puree. Let simmer for 5 minutes
  7. Now roll the dough (as thin as you can) with a clean bottle and put it all together
  8. Place in oven
  9. Don't forget about it like I did
