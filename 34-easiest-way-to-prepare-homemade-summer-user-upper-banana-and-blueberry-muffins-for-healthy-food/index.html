The ingredients needed to make Summer user upper banana and blueberry muffins:
  1. Take 300 grams self raising flour
  2. Take 150 grams dark brown soft sugar
  3. Get 2 mashed ripe bananas
  4. Get 100 grams blueberries
  5. Get 1 beaten egg
  6. Take 120 grams Greek yogurt
  7. Take 70 ml semi skimmed milk
  8. Get 120 ml sunflower oil
Steps to make Summer user upper banana and blueberry muffins:
  1. Sift dry ingredients to big bowl
  2. Add mashed bananas and 100 grams bluberries (I squirt a bit of lemon juice so the bananas don't go brown after mashing)
  3. Incorporate rest of liquid ingredients 1 at a time
  4. Fill pre greased muffin pan with mixture and bake for 18-20 minutes in preheated oven 170Â°C. They are ready when a skewer inserted comes out clean but bear in mind banana and bluberries will make muffins quite moist
