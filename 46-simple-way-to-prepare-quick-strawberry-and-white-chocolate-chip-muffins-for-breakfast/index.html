The ingredients needed to make Strawberry and White Chocolate Chip Muffins:
  1. Take 150 g dairy free spread
  2. Make ready 150 g caster sugar
  3. Prepare 3 eggs
  4. Make ready 150 g self raising gluten free flour
  5. Take 1 teaspoon xanthan gum
  6. Prepare 100 g dairy free white chocolate chips
  7. Make ready 4 tablespoons chopped strawberries
Steps to make Strawberry and White Chocolate Chip Muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 oC - Beat together the caster sugar and the dairy free spread until light and fluffy - Add the three eggs individually, beating well between each addition - Add in the flour and xanthan gum - Mix until well combined
  2. Stir in the strawberries and chocolate chips - Split the mixture between 8 muffin moulds lined with paper cases - Bake in the centre of the oven for 20 minutes
