The ingredients needed to make Keto Seafood Stir fry:
  1. Make ready 1/2 kg shrimp
  2. Take to taste salt
  3. Prepare brocolli
  4. Prepare cauliflower
  5. Prepare carrot
  6. Take 1 tsp black pepper
  7. Make ready 1 knorr cube
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
  9. Take dash oregano
Instructions to make Keto Seafood Stir fry:
  1. Wash shrimps then marinate with salt, black pepper and any other spice you wish to use.
  2. Slice carrot, cauliflower and broccoli and set them aside
  3. Heat up frying pan and pour in olive oil and stir in shrimps after 3 minutes and stir
  4. Add knorr cube, salt, black pepper and oregano and stir in carrot cauliflower and broccoli stir for 1 minute and turn off heat and serve
