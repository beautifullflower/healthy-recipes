The ingredients needed to make Christmas Muffin from Switzerland:
  1. Make ready 250 g all-purpose flour
  2. Take 60 g brown sugar
  3. Prepare 20 g cocoa powder
  4. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
  5. Make ready 2 tsp. ground cinnamon
  6. Get 1/2 tsp. ground clove
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg
  8. Take 1,5 oranges (juice and zest)
  9. Prepare 250 g (unsweetened) yoghurt
  10. Prepare 50 g butter, softened
  11. Get 1 egg (M)
  12. Take Decoration:
  13. Get 50 g melted chocolate
  14. Take 0,5 orange fillet
Instructions to make Christmas Muffin from Switzerland:
  1. Preheat the oven 350 F, prepare the ingredients.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients.
  3. Mix the egg with softened butter, orange juice, grated zest and yoghurt in an other bowl.
  4. Combine the two mixtures.
  5. Line the muffin tin with the muffin cups and fill them. Bake the muffins at 350 F for 25-30 minutes.
  6. Decorate them with melted chocolate and orange fillets. And serve them, if you feel like, with honey or orange marmalade and orange chocolate milk.
