The ingredients needed to make BAKED pizza TORTELLINI:
  1. Take 1 lb frozen tortellini
  2. Make ready 30 oz spaghetti sauce
  3. Take 1 T minced garlic
  4. Take 2 C shredded mozarella cheese
  5. Get 1/2 C shredded Parmesan cheese
  6. Make ready 1 T Italian seasoning
  7. Make ready Sliced pepperoni
  8. Take Sliced mushrooms

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Place the cooked tortellini in a deep baking dish. Cover with the cheesy mixture, then and the pizza sauce, stirring to combine. Instructions to make BAKED pizza TORTELLINI:
  1. Pour first 3 ingredients into baking dish sprayed with non-stick spray. Top with pepperoni, mushrooms cheeses and parsley
  2. Add water if noodles are not all covered in sauce.
  3. Bake covered in a 400 degree oven for 25-35 minutes. Uncover and broil 2 minutes to brown cheese if desired.
