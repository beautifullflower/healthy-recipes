The ingredients needed to make Pizza Dhokla:
  1. Take 1 cup gram flour
  2. Take 1/2 cup curd
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  4. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  5. Take 1 packet eno (soda)
  6. Make ready 1 cup thinly sliced capsicum
  7. Get 1 cup thinly sliced onion
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp sweet corn
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp thinly sliced mushroom
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp chopped tomato
  11. Prepare 1 tsp oregano
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp pizza sauce
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp oil
  14. Get 1 cup mozzarella cheese
Instructions to make Pizza Dhokla:
  1. Take a pan
  2. Add oil
  3. Add capcicum
  4. Mix it well properly only 1 min.
  5. Now take a bowl
  6. Add gram flour
  7. Add curd,salt
  8. Mix it well.keep aside for 10 min.and then add water and whisk nicely.now add baking soda
  9. And take a bowl and grease the oil and pour over the batter
  10. Now we put batter in microwave for 2 min.and now dhokla is ready.now spread the pizza sauce over the dhokla.
  11. Now spread the stuffing over the dhokla.and add mushroom,tomato over it.
  12. Now add oregano and mozerella cheese over it.after that put it in to microwave to melt the cheese for 1 min.
  13. Now pizza dhokla is ready to serve.
