The ingredients needed to make Julie and Ben’s hot and sticky fudgey date (muffins):
  1. Take 2 cups flour
  2. Take 1 cup sugar
  3. Get 4 tsp baking powder
  4. Get 1 egg
  5. Make ready 1.5 cups milk
  6. Make ready 4 tbsp oil
  7. Prepare 12 dates
  8. Prepare 90 g fudge
Instructions to make Julie and Ben’s hot and sticky fudgey date (muffins):
  1. Sift the flour and baking powder four times
  2. Beat the egg lightly, pour in milk and oil.
  3. Make a well in the middle of the dry and pour in the wet
  4. Add more milk to create a floppy texture
  5. Quarter the dates and 100g of fudge cubes
  6. Mix together good and proper
  7. Pop into muffin cups 2/3 fills pop into the oven for 20 mins.
  8. Cool on a rack
