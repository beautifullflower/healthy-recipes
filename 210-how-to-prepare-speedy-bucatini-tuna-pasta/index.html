The ingredients needed to make Bucatini tuna pasta:
  1. Prepare 3-4 garlic cloves
  2. Take 1 red chilli
  3. Get 3-4 Anchovies
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp capers
  5. Get 8-10 cherry tomatoes
  6. Prepare 300 g tuna
  7. Prepare Bucatini pasta
  8. Get Lemon juice
  9. Make ready Olive oil
  10. Make ready Rocket salad

Steps to make Bucatini tuna pasta:
  1. Fry sliced garlic and chilli for couple of minutes
  2. Add chopped anchovies, cook for couple of minutes
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, cook for couple of minutes
  4. Add capers, cook for couple of minutes
  5. Add tuna
  6. After your pasta is cooked, pour it to the sauce mix. Add splash of lemon and olive oil and mix well
  7. To prepare salad- mix together rocket with a bit of lemon juice, olive oil and salt and put it on top of your pasta
