The ingredients needed to make Portobello mushroom pizza:
  1. Take 1 Portobello mushroom cap
  2. Get 1 tbsp heaping - pesto
  3. Take 1/2 tomato, sliced thin
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp heaping - shredded parmesan cheese
Instructions to make Portobello mushroom pizza:
  1. Preheat oven to 425°. Spray cookie sheet with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Wash mushroom and remove gills.
  3. Spread pesto inside mushroom. Then layer on tomato slices and cheese. - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/348902-pesto
  4. Bake 25 minutes or until cheese is golden.
