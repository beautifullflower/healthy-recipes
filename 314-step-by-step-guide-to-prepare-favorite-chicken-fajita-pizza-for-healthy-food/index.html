The ingredients needed to make Chicken Fajita Pizza😋🍕:
  1. Make ready For dough
  2. Get 1 1/4 cup flour
  3. Prepare 1 tsp instant yeast
  4. Make ready 1 tbs nido
  5. Take 1/2 egg beaten
  6. Take 1/2 tsp sugar
  7. Make ready 1 tbs oil
  8. Take 1/4 cup /as required luke water
  9. Make ready Fajita topping
  10. Take 1 tbs oil
  11. Get 150 grams chicken strips
  12. Take 1/2 tsp garlic powder/paste
  13. Take to taste salt
  14. Take 1 tsp red chilli powder
  15. Prepare 3/4 tsp chilli falkes
  16. Get 1/2 tsp oregano
  17. Prepare 1 tsp lemon juice
  18. Make ready 1/4 cup Mushrooms sliced
  19. Make ready 1/2 onion sliced
  20. Prepare 1/4 cup capsicum julien cut
  21. Make ready 2-3 tbs pizza sauce
  22. Make ready 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  23. Prepare 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
  24. Prepare black olives sliced
Instructions to make Chicken Fajita Pizza😋🍕:
  1. In a mixing bowl add flour salt sugar nido yeast and mix well
  2. Add oil and beaten egg and knead with luke water very well for 10 minutes
  3. Grease with oil cover it and let it rest for 1 hour in a warm place
  4. In a pan heat oil stir fry chicken strips then add all spices and lemon juice.
  5. Add Mushrooms capsicum onion and put aside
  6. With oily hands knead pizza dough again for 3 to 4 minutes set in a greased square pizza pan and shape it with soft hands
  7. Prick with a fork spread pizza sauce evenly put prepared chicken toppings and add cheese olives sprinkle oregano
  8. Bake in a pre heated oven for 12 to 15 minutes on 200 degrees. serve hot😍😋
