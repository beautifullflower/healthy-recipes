The ingredients needed to make Sig's Potato and Tuna Salad:
  1. Prepare 10-12 baby new potatoes in skin
  2. Make ready 1 small can tuna in spring water
  3. Prepare 2 tablespoons olive oil
  4. Get 3-4 tablespoons cherry vinegar
  5. Make ready Pinch salt and pepper
  6. Make ready 1 spring onion
  7. Make ready A few sprigs of fresh parsley
Instructions to make Sig's Potato and Tuna Salad:
  1. Wash the potatoes, cook until tender but not mashy or soft. Drain, cool in cold water. In the meantime make the dressing. Mix the oil, vinegar, pepper, salt well. Peel and slice the potatoes, add the oil and vinegar mix.
  2. Remove water from tuna, gently break it up and lift it under the potatoes. Add the chopped parsley and whites of the spring onion.
  3. Serve on its own or with other salads and sides.
