The ingredients needed to make Tetrazzini w Chicken and mushrooms:
  1. Prepare 400 gms any pasta cooked
  2. Prepare 1 roasted chicken, shredded
  3. Take 400 gms white and brown button mushrooms sliced
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup white wine
  5. Make ready 1 cup chicken stock
  6. Prepare 1 cup milk
  7. Get 2 tbsp all-purpose flour or whole wheat flour
  8. Prepare 1 medium onion chopped
  9. Prepare 3-4 garlic cloves chopped
  10. Prepare 250 gms shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheese
  11. Make ready 3 tbsp olive oil
  12. Get juice of 1/2 lemon
  13. Get to taste salt and pepper
Steps to make Tetrazzini w Chicken and mushrooms:
  1. Place a large ovenproof deep dish on stove top on medium flame, once hot, add 1 tbsp of olive oil, garlic and mushrooms, cook until mushrooms are cooked, remove into a bowl, reserve any liquid with stock. to the same pan, add oil, onions, once onions are translucent, add flour, mix for a minute or two.
  2. Add wine, stock and milk, continue stirring until the mix is slightly thickened, season with salt and pepper, to this mix now add chicken and mushrooms, check for seasoning, (at this stage, the mixture can be portioned and stored in freezer for later use) sprinkle generously with cheese.
  3. Pre-heat oven to 180 degrees Celsius, cover the pan with lid or aluminium wrap and bake for 30 minutes, remove wrap/ lid and continue to bake for another 15-20 minutes until top is slightly golden brown. cool and serve.
