How to Prepare Traditional Thin Crust Wheat Pizza for Healthy Food

The ingredients needed to make Thin Crust Wheat Pizza:
  1. Make ready For Pizza Base
  2. Make ready 1 cup Wheat Flour
  3. Take 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp Baking Soda
  5. Take 1/2 cup Yoghurt
  6. Get as per taste Salt
  7. Make ready 2 tsp Oil
  8. Make ready As required Butter for greasing
  9. Prepare for Topping–
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp Butter
  11. Get 1 tbsp giner garlic paste
  12. Make ready 1 medium onion sliced
  13. Take 1 medium capsicum sliced
  14. Take 1/2 cup boiled sweet corns
  15. Get 1/2 cup mushrooms chopped
  16. Get 100 gms paneer diced
  17. Get 1 tbsp oregano
  18. Take 1 tbsp chilli flakes
  19. Prepare 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  20. Get to taste Salt
  21. Make ready For homemade pizza sauce-
  22. Take 2 tbsp soya sauce
  23. Prepare 4 tbsp tomato ketchup
Instructions to make Thin Crust Wheat Pizza:
  1. For Wheat Pizza Base - In a bowl add wheat flour, baking powder, baking soda, oil and mix well. Now add yoghurt and start making a dough.
  2. Once the dough is ready, cover it with wet muslin cloth & keep it aside for 20 minutes.
  3. After 20 minutes, take the dough and divide it into 4 equal parts & roll it in round shape using a rolling pin and I have rolled it thin for thin crust. Using a fork spoon prick the base so it doesn't puff up while baking it.
  4. Heat a pan and put any steel or aluminum mould or plate which fits in this pan and grease it with butter and preheat on medium flame.
  5. Place your rolled pizza on this greased plate and cover it with lid and let it cook for 15 minutes on slow flame
  6. For toppings- Heat a pan add 1tbsp butter & let it melt. Now add ginger garlic paste, sliced onions and saut√© it.
  7. After onions turn to light pink color now add capsicum and boiled sweet corn & cook for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Now add chopped mushrooms and diced paneer and saut√© for another 2-3 minutes.
  9. Now add salt as per taste, chilli flakes and oregano and mix well.
  10. For Pizza Sauce - In a bowl add 2tbsp soya sauce and 4tbsp tomato ketchup. Your instant pizza sauce is ready.
  11. Take the freshly prepared wheat thin pizza base and apply this pizza sauce leaving the edges to the middle.
  12. Now start decorating your pizza with this sauted veggies and lastly add tones of grated mozzarella cheese and I like to sprinkle oregano and chilli flakes on top of it.
  13. Heat a pan add 1tbsp melted butter and place this pizza for 5 minutes on low flame, covering it with the lid.
  14. After 5 minutes you will notice the mozzarella cheese has completely melted and this the sign that pizza is ready to serve.
