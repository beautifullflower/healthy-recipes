The ingredients needed to make Egg Ham baked cups with Guacamole:
  1. Take 2 eggs
  2. Make ready 8 slices wafer thin ham (plain,smoked or honey roasted ham)
  3. Make ready Mixed herbs or Italian seasoning
  4. Make ready Guacamole ingredients
  5. Make ready 1 ripe avocado
  6. Prepare 1 medium tomato
  7. Make ready 1/2 onion (red or white)
  8. Take 1 lime
  9. Make ready Chilli flakes
  10. Make ready Seasoning
  11. Get Fresh parsley (or traditional Coriander if you prefer)
Steps to make Egg Ham baked cups with Guacamole:
  1. Preheat oven at 180c
  2. Get a muffin tray. You will use 4 slices of ham per muffin cup. First overlay 3 slices of ham around the sides of one of the muffin cups. Then place your 4th slice at the bottom of the cup. Repeat for your second one.
  3. Crack one egg into each of your ham casings and season with your Mixed herbs or Italian seasoning
  4. Bake for 20 minutes or until most of the white is cooked.
  5. Whilst eggs are baking prepare your guacamole. Half, de-stone and remove your avocado from its skins into a bowl. Mash.
  6. Finely chop onion and add to avocado
  7. Roughly chop tomato and parsley and add.
  8. Add as much chilli as you like. Squeeze over juice from your lime. Mix everything and season well.
  9. Once eggs are baked. Serve up with your guacamole and toast.
