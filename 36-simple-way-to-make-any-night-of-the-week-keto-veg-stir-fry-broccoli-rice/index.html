The ingredients needed to make Keto Veg Stir-Fry Broccoli Rice:
  1. Take Broccoli Rice
  2. Get 1 Broccoli diced (medium)
  3. Make ready 8 button mushroom diced (medium - large)
  4. Make ready 1 Tomato diced (medium)
  5. Make ready 1 Green bell pepper diced
  6. Get 1 Carrot diced [optional]
  7. Get 1 tsp Salt
  8. Take 1 tsp Black pepper
  9. Get 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  10. Get Topping
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp Lemon juice
  12. Get 1 slice Cheese [Optional]
Instructions to make Keto Veg Stir-Fry Broccoli Rice:
  1. Put diced broccoli pieces into food processor and run it till broccoli gets into rice size.
  2. In a frying pan on low-medium flame, put olive oil and let it sputter. Place the tomatoes in the pan and keep on stirring it. After 2 minutes add other vegetables (except “riced” broccoli) and keep on stirring them too.
  3. Add the “riced” broccoli in the pan once the vegetables are cooked. Stir fry the mixture for 2 minutes and then add lemon juice to it. Stir fry for a few more minutes on low-medium heat, till the smell of raw broccoli goes away and the rice gets dry.
  4. Transfer the vegetable stir-fry broccoli rice to a bowl. For cheese topping, cut the cheese slice into thin strips and place it in a criss-cross fashion on top of the rice. Serve hot.
