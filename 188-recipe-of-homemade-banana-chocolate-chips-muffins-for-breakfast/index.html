The ingredients needed to make Banana Chocolate chips muffins:
  1. Make ready 40 g unsalted butter
  2. Make ready 30 g sugar
  3. Prepare 5 g baking powder
  4. Take 100 g flour
  5. Prepare 1 egg
  6. Take 80 ml milk
  7. Prepare 1 banana
  8. Prepare 40 g dark chocolate chips
Instructions to make Banana Chocolate chips muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180C.
  2. Mix egg and sugar together until all sugar is dissolved
  3. Add in milk and mix well. Meanwhile, in a separate bowl, mash the banana and set aside.
  4. After that, add in melted butter to the mixture and mix well. When all the butter is combined, add in mashed banana and mix well.
  5. Now sieve in flour and baking powder, gently mix well. Then add in chocolate chips and mix well.
  6. Divide the batter into 6 muffins case. Carefully drop the case to remove excess air bubbles. Bake the muffins for 17-20mins or until fully cooked. Let them to cool completely before serving.
