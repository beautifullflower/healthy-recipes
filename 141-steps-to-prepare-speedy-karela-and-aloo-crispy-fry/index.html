The ingredients needed to make Karela and Aloo Crispy Fry:
  1. Take 250 gram desi bitter gourd finely chopped
  2. Take 1 medium potato chopped into small chunks
  3. Take 1 large onion chopped finely
  4. Prepare 2 fresh red chili chopped
  5. Take 1/3 tsp turmeric powder
  6. Prepare to taste Salt
  7. Prepare 1/3 tsp mustard seeds
  8. Get 1 Tbsp mustard oil
  9. Get 1 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder (fresh)
Instructions to make Karela and Aloo Crispy Fry:
  1. Gather all required ingredients.
  2. Heat oil in a wok. Add mustard seeds and allow to crackle them.
  3. Add karela and stir fry till softened to lessen bitterness.
  4. Add salt and turmeric powder and coat well.
  5. Mix onion, chopped chili and fry till onions soft.
  6. Add chopped aloo, mix well and cook covered till done. Switch off the flame and add roasted cumin powder and coat well. Serve with steamed Rice. - Thanks.
