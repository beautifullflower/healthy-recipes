The ingredients needed to make Prawn Fry Keto:
  1. Get 1 Pound Shell removed prawns washed and cleaned
  2. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon Ginger garlic paste
  3. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  4. Take 1/2 teaspoon Red chilli powder
  5. Take 3 tablespoons Olive oil
  6. Take 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder(optional)
Steps to make Prawn Fry Keto:
  1. Mix all the ingredients and keep it in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat the tawa and Fry the prawns on both sides till you get golden brown in colour.
  3. Serve it with Salad.
