The ingredients needed to make Cauliflower Upma:
  1. Get 250 gms Cauliflower
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp Ghee
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp Chana daal
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp Urad daal
  5. Get 1 tsp Mustard seeds
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp Hing
  7. Prepare 3 Dry red chillies
  8. Take 8-10 Curry leaves
  9. Take 1 Chopped onionc small
  10. Prepare 1 tsp Chopped ginger
  11. Prepare 1 Chopped tomato small
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp Green peas
  13. Get 8-10 Chopped cashews
  14. Get As per taste Salt
  15. Get 1/2 cup + 4 cups Boiling Water
  16. Make ready 1 tablespoon Lemon juice
  17. Prepare 1 tsp Chopped coriander leaves
Steps to make Cauliflower Upma:
  1. First, wash the cauliflower and dip in boiling water for 10 minutes. Then wash again and cut into florets.
  2. Then grind in pulsing motions till you have semolina like texture.
  3. Now heat ghee in a karahi. First, lightly fry the cashews and remove in a bowl. Then heat the same ghee add mustard seeds. Then add daals, hing, dry red chillies, chopped ginger, curry leaves, and saute' till they are aromatic.
  4. Then add to onions and fry well. Then add tomatoes, peas and cook till soft.
  5. Then add the Cauliflower, salt, Chilli powder and mix well. Add boiling water in small batches and cook covered till soft and aromatic.
  6. After cauliflower is well cooked, add lemon juice, adjust spices and remove from flame.
  7. Garnish with reserved chopped casheews and coriander leaves. Serve and enjoy!
