The ingredients needed to make Donut style muffins!!!! (No frying required!!):
  1. Take 200 g plain flour (sift)
  2. Take 140 g caster sugar
  3. Get 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  4. Take 100 ml vanilla yogart (or a plain yogart)
  5. Prepare 2 medium eggs
  6. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Take 140 g butter (unsalted) -melted

Steps to make Donut style muffins!!!! (No frying required!!):
  1. Pre heat oven to 190c (170c fan), line 10 cake cases in a tin
  2. Put caster sugar, flour, bicarbonate soda, in a large bowl
  3. In a jug add eggs, yoghurt, vanilla extract, and melted butter, whisk together
  4. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients, using a manual hand whisk, whisk together well until combined
  5. Half fill each cupcake case with cake mix, and then add around half a tsp of filling (I used nutella and jam as my fillings), then top up with my cake mix to the top of case
  6. Bake in middle of oven, for 15 minutes until golden..take out and leave to cool for 5minutes
  7. Pop some granulated sugar in a bowl… and a little melted butter in another bowl (melt around 40g unsalted), pop the top of each muffin into the melted warm butter and then dip into the sugar!
