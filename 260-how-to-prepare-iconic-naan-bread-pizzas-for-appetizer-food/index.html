The ingredients needed to make Naan bread pizzas:
  1. Take Plain naan bread
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp bbq sauce
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp soy sauce
  4. Get 1 tsp garlic salt
  5. Take 1 tsp chopped garlic
  6. Make ready Salt and pepper
  7. Make ready Diced ham
  8. Get Chopped mushrooms
  9. Make ready Pineapple chunks
  10. Get Cheddar cheese grated
  11. Prepare slices Mozzarella cheese cut in to
Instructions to make Naan bread pizzas:
  1. For the sauce add to a sauce pan and cook low heat, 2 tbsp of bbq sauce 1 tsp of garlic salt, 1 tbsp soy sauce, salt and pepper 1 tsp garlic chopped, simmer and stir.
  2. Take the plan naan bread and add the sauce from the sauce plan to the naan bread and spread around as think or thinly as preferred.
  3. Add the grated cheese, mozzarella, ham and mushrooms and pineapple if preferred to the naan bread and cook in the oven on 180 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes.
