The ingredients needed to make Poached Chicken and Egg Salad:
  1. Make ready 1 Chicken Breast
  2. Make ready 1 Boiled Egg
  3. Get 1/4 tsp Garlic paste
  4. Make ready to taste salt
  5. Prepare pinch Black pepper powder
  6. Take 2 cups water
  7. Prepare For the Salad
  8. Get 1 boiled egg
  9. Make ready 2 Lettuce leaves
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup Pomegranate
  11. Get 5-6 Strawberries sliced
  12. Make ready 1/2 tbsp Sunflower seeds
  13. Prepare 1/2 tbsp Flax seeds
  14. Prepare 5-6 Pecan nuts
  15. Take 10-12 Pistachios
  16. Take 1 pinch salt
  17. Take 3 tsp Lemon juice
  18. Get as needed Drizzle of Olive oil
  19. Get 2 Lemon slice (optional)
Instructions to make Poached Chicken and Egg Salad:
  1. Poach or boil the chicken breast with 2 cups water, salt, pepper and garlic.cover and cook for half an hour.
  2. After that on a grill pan put butter and grill the poached / boiled chicken breast. Dry roast the nuts and seeds for few minutes.
  3. Put the lettuce, pomegranate, strawberry on the plate, sprinkle salt, lemon juice, drizzle little olive oil.now place the grilled chicken breast and the boiled eggs.garnish and top with roasted nuts and seeds & lemon slices (optional)
