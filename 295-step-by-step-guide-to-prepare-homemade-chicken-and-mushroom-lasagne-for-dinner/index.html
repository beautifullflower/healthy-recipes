The ingredients needed to make Chicken and mushroom lasagne:
  1. Get 1 packet fresh lasagne sheets
  2. Get 325 g cooked chicken
  3. Make ready 1 large onion thickly sliced
  4. Get 1 garlic clove crushed
  5. Make ready 250 g mushrooms sliced
  6. Prepare 150 g grated mozzarella/ ceddar cheese
  7. Prepare 1 jar white sauce
  8. Take 1/2 tsp mixed herbs
  9. Make ready Salt and pepper
Steps to make Chicken and mushroom lasagne:
  1. Place onion in oven to slow roast at 180° for 30 min untill soften
  2. Cook the mushrooms garlic and mixed herbs in a large frying pan
  3. Chop chicken
  4. In a lasagne dish start layering the chicken onion mushrooms and white sauce untill you have 3 layers you should have a 3rd of a jar left to do the top layer at this stage
  5. The last layer sprinkle the grated cheese rinse the jar with water about a 3rd and pour around edges as this makes the sauce
  6. Place in oven for 30/40 mins
  7. Serve with rocket salad
