The ingredients needed to make Tawa Fish with seasonal Veggies:
  1. Make ready 250 gms basa phile
  2. Get 2 tbsp Chopped onion and garlic each
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp Butter, lemon juice, pepper salt, salt each
  4. Make ready as required Seasonal vegetables like carrot, beans, baby Corn, brokoli etc
  5. Take 4 tbsp Plain flour for coating
Instructions to make Tawa Fish with seasonal Veggies:
  1. First marinate the phile with salt, pepper salt and lemon juice for half an hour
  2. Now boil all the Veggies
  3. Then coat the fish with plain flour
  4. Now add some butter in a pan then add chopped garlic followed by onions then fry the fish very carefully as its very soft.
  5. Your basa base is ready. Add some lemon juice for better taste
  6. Again add some butter on pan stir fry the boiled Veggies with little bit of salt and pepper
