The ingredients needed to make sig's Blue, Parmesan, Goats and Cheddar Cheese Mini Muffins:
  1. Get 175 grams self-raising flour
  2. Make ready 1 cup butter ( 80g )
  3. Take 1/2 tsp vegetable stock powder , ( 1 teaspoon stock powder is equal to 1 small stock cube )
  4. Take 75 grams grated Cheddar cheese
  5. Make ready 75 grams blue cheese
  6. Make ready 40 grams finely grated parmesan cheese
  7. Take 40 grams soft goats cheese
  8. Make ready 1 egg,lightly beaten
  9. Make ready 250 ml milk
  10. Get 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  11. Prepare 3 green onions (spring onion) ,finely chopped
Instructions to make sig's Blue, Parmesan, Goats and Cheddar Cheese Mini Muffins:
  1. Grease a 24 hole mimi muffin or mini yorkshire pudding pan. Sift the flour and the 1/2 teaspoon ( 3ml) into a large bowl and rub in the butter.Stir in remaining ingredients. Spoon the mixture into the prepared pan. bake in modeate hot oven for about 20 minutes.
