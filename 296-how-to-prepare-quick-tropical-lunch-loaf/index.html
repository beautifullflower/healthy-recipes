The ingredients needed to make Tropical lunch loaf:
  1. Make ready 1 piece chicken fillet chicken wash and cut up in chunky pieces
  2. Prepare As needed olive oil
  3. Take 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  4. Get 3-4 cherry tomatoes cut up
  5. Make ready 1/2 grated carrot
  6. Prepare A few slices of cucumber
  7. Take 1 piece crisp butter lettuce shredded
  8. Prepare 1 knotted bread roll/loaf
  9. Take A few pieces of pineapple chunks

Instructions to make Tropical lunch loaf:
  1. Marinate chicken for a little and cook in a little olive oil, allow to cook, mix in a tablespoon of mayonnaise.
  2. Cut up the knotted loaf and fill with chicken filling and tropical salad (cherry tomatoes, grated carrot, cucumber, lettuce and pineapple)
  3. Serve with fruit juice and a side salad.
