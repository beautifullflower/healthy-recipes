The ingredients needed to make Muffins with tomato:
  1. Prepare 3 firm medium-sized tomatoes, diced
  2. Take 1 spring onion very finely chopped
  3. Prepare 1 dry onion, grated
  4. Take 100 g grated feta cheese
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp strained yogurt
  6. Get 2 eggs
  7. Take 1 cup self rising flour
  8. Make ready parsley, spearmint, salt, pepper
Steps to make Muffins with tomato:
  1. Mix all the ingredients well together into a thick batter.
  2. Brush your muffin tin with oil and sprinkle with breadcrumbs.
  3. Pour 2-3 tbsp of the batter and bake at 180Â°C for about 60 minutes until they are golden brown.
