The ingredients needed to make Keto Paneer Salad:
  1. Make ready 100 gm Paneer cubed (cottage cheese)
  2. Get 1/2 Zucchini diced
  3. Make ready 1/4 Cabbage shredded
  4. Prepare 5 Cherry Tomatoes quartered
  5. Get 2 piece Iceberg Lettuce
  6. Get 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  7. Take 1 tbsp Salt
  8. Prepare 1 tsp black pepper powder
Instructions to make Keto Paneer Salad:
  1. Heat a pan with oil and add paneer cubes. Fry paneer cubes for 3-5 minutes on low-medium flame. Transfer the cubes to a plate.
  2. Add cabbage to the same pan and fry for 5 minutes or until it just starts turning brown. Add salt, zucchini and cherry tomatoes and fry for 2-3 more minutes on low heat. Keep stirring. Switch off the gas and add paneer and black pepper and stir
  3. Line your serving bowl with lettuce leaves and transfer the salad to the bowl. Optional- add coriander chutney for taste.
