The ingredients needed to make Keto Broccoli Salad:
  1. Take 3 heads Broccoli cut into bite size pieces
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar
  3. Prepare 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  4. Prepare 2 Tbsp. Chopped chives
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup sliced Almonds
  6. Make ready 2/3 cup mayonnaise
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp. Apple cider vinegar
  8. Get 1 tbsp Dijon
  9. Get Salt & pepper
Instructions to make Keto Broccoli Salad:
  1. Boil 6 cups water, add broccoli and cook 2 min.Drain and cool.
  2. Combine wet ingredients for dressing. Toss all together.
