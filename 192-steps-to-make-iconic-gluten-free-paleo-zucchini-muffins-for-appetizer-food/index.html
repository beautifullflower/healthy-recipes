The ingredients needed to make Gluten free paleo zucchini muffins:
  1. Get 3 cup Grated zucchini (2 units)
  2. Get 3 cup Almond flour
  3. Get 1/3 cup Melted butter or ghee
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup Honey
  5. Prepare 3 Eggs beaten
  6. Make ready 2 tsp Cinnamon
  7. Get 1 tsp Baking soda
  8. Get 1/2 tsp Salt

Instructions to make Gluten free paleo zucchini muffins:
  1. Prepare grated zucchini and melted butter and set aside
  2. Mix almond flour, melted butter, honey and zucchini
  3. Add the beaten eggs, cinnamon, salt and soda and mix well
  4. Bake in silicone or paper-lined muffin tins at 180¬∞C for about 20 minutes
