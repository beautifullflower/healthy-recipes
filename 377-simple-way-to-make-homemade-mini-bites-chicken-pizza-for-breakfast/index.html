The ingredients needed to make Mini Bites Chicken Pizza:
  1. Make ready FOR THE BASE-
  2. Take 3 cups All Purpose Flour or Maida
  3. Take 2 tsp Dry Yeast
  4. Get 1 tsp Salt or to taste
  5. Take 2 tsp Sugar
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  7. Make ready 1 cup lukewarm Milk (or little more)
  8. Prepare FOR THE CHICKEN-
  9. Get 400 gm Chicken Boneless cubes
  10. Make ready 1 tsp Salt or to taste
  11. Get 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  12. Prepare 1 tsp Cumin powder
  13. Prepare 1 tsp Ginger Garlic powder
  14. Prepare 1 tbsp Red Chilli flakes
  15. Make ready 1 tsp Black Pepper Powder
  16. Prepare FOR THE TOPPINGS -
  17. Make ready 2 Onions thinly sliced
  18. Get 2 Capsicum thinly sliced
  19. Take 1/4 cup Mushrooms halved
  20. Make ready 1/2 cup Pizza Sauce
  21. Take 2 cups Mozzarella Cheese shredded
  22. Make ready 4-5 Cheddar Cheese slices
  23. Get 1 tsp Oregano or Italian seasoning
Instructions to make Mini Bites Chicken Pizza:
  1. Add sugar and yeast to warm milk and mix lightly. Allow it to prove for 4 to 5 minutes. It should be frothy and also a bit smelly. If not discard it and make a fresh one. This is really important for a good pizza base.
  2. In a mixing bowl, add flour, salt and the olive oil. Now add this yeast mix to the flour and make a smooth dough out of it. Knead this dough until it's smooth and apply a few drops of olive oil to it and cover it up with a cling wrap and leave it in a dark place in your kitchen to double up in volume.
  3. Meanwhile, cut the veggies and keep aside. In a pan, heat a tablespoon of olive oil and saute the garlic mince. Add the boneless chicken cubes to it. Add all the ingredients listed in it's preparation before covering with a lid.
  4. Saute them all on a low heat. Allow it to simmer until done. Next take out the dough and punch it down once to release the air. Grease a pizza pan and start making roundels of the dough.
  5. Make medium sized balls out of it. Similarly make all in this way covering the whole pan. Next spread some pizza sauce and the toppings. Bake this in a preheated oven on 180¬∞ for 18 to 20 minutes or little more. It depends on the oven temperature. So keep an eye after 15 minutes.
  6. After it is baked perfectly, it's time to add in the Cheese generously. Sprinkle shredded Mozzarella Cheese and tear the Cheddar slices on top of it followed by some Italian seasoning. Pop it back in the Oven just until the Cheese melts. Cheesyliciously yours to dig in now. Gooey and cheesy to the last bite.
