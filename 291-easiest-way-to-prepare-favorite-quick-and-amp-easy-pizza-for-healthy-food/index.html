Steps to make Quick & Easy Pizza:
  1. Spread tomato puree onto the tortilla
  2. Top with the sliced mushrooms, small diced red pepper, sliced spring onions, pepperoni and torn pieces of mozzarella
  3. Cook in a hot oven on a baking tray for 5-10 mins until bubbling on top and crisp round the edges
  4. Top with torn basil while still warm and serve with the rocket drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar
