The ingredients needed to make Easy peesy mix vegetable:
  1. Take oil
  2. Get cumin
  3. Get dried round chillies(gol mirch)
  4. Prepare chopped galic
  5. Make ready onion chopped
  6. Take tomatoes chopped
  7. Take Potato small pieces
  8. Get large carrot small pieces
  9. Take peas
  10. Take tinned Sweet corn(optional)
  11. Get Capsicum small pieces
  12. Take beans small pieces(optional)
  13. Take Salt
  14. Prepare Turmeric
  15. Prepare coriander powder
  16. Prepare red chilli
  17. Get chat masala
  18. Make ready ketchup
  19. Take water
  20. Get Garnish
  21. Make ready Coriander
  22. Take Butter
Instructions to make Easy peesy mix vegetable:
  1. Heat oil and add cumin seeds, chopped garlic and gol mirch.Fry few minutes
  2. Add onions and fry till soft and golden then add tomatoes and fry few minutes.
  3. Now add all the powdered spices and fry for few more minutes.
  4. Now add all the veggies except capsicum and make sure potatoes and carrots are of same size so it will cook properly.
  5. Add one cup of water to cover all the vegetables.
  6. Let it cook for ten to fifteen minutes check again if more water is required.Now add capsicum and let it cook for few more minutes.
  7. When vegetables are done add ketchup and mix well.
  8. Add some butter and dishout.
  9. Garish with coriander and serve.
