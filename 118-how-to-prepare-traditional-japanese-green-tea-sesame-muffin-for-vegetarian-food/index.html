The ingredients needed to make Japanese green tea sesame muffin♪:
  1. Make ready 50 g Unsalted butter
  2. Make ready 30 g Sugar
  3. Make ready 1 beaten egg
  4. Get 50 m soy milk or fat free milk
  5. Make ready 120 g flour
  6. Make ready 2 tsp baking powder
  7. Make ready 2 tsp green tea powder
  8. Get 2 tbsp honey
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp White sesame

Instructions to make Japanese green tea sesame muffin♪:
  1. Leave butter and egg to room temperature, then beat egg.
  2. Add sugar bit by bit to 1 beaten egg, mix well each time till almost stiff peak.
  3. Add milk to step#2, mix well.
  4. Add sifted flour and baking powder to step #3 mix well with a spatula. Add green tea powder, sesame and honey.
  5. Pour it into the cups, bake for 20min/Preheated 180℃.
  6. Done! Cool down and ready to eat!!
