The ingredients needed to make Double cheese pizza 😋:
  1. Make ready 4 cup maida
  2. Get 1 sachet instant yeast
  3. Prepare Pinch salt
  4. Take 1 tblspoon sugar
  5. Prepare 1 tblspoon oil
  6. Make ready Chicken boti
  7. Make ready 1 pao chicken boneless
  8. Take 1 tblspoon oil
  9. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon haldi
  10. Get 1 teaspoon paprika powder
  11. Take 1 teaspoon zeera powder
  12. Get 1 teaspoon lehsan adrak ka paste
  13. Make ready 3 tblspoon dahi
  14. Make ready Pinch salt
  15. Get Topping
  16. Take 1/2 cup capcicum
  17. Take 1 onion sliced
  18. Prepare 1/2 cup mushroom sliced
  19. Make ready 1/2 cup olives
  20. Get As required pizza cheese
  21. Get As required moziralla cheese
  22. Get 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  23. Make ready 1 teaspoon lal kuti hui mirch
  24. Get 3 tblspoon pizza sauce
Steps to make Double cheese pizza 😋:
  1. Sub se pahle oven preheat kar lain 15 minutes
  2. Aik bowl main maida dalen is mein salt,sugar,oil dalen aur 1/2 glass hot water mein yeast hl kar lain aur ye dalen aate mein aur aata goondh lain
  3. Aur dhak kar rise kar lain
  4. Chicken main lehsan adrak ka paste,haldi, paprika powder,dahi,zeera powder,oil dal kar maranid kar lain aur pan main dal kar chicken dry kar lain
  5. Phir shimla, onion,cut kar lain, mushroom, olives aik plate mein nikal lain
  6. Phir aata ko lekar pere banayein aur bail lain phir pizza sauce lagayen aur cheese dalen
  7. Phir shimla, onion, chicken, olives, mushroom, dalen ooper se cheese dalen aur kuti hui lal mirch,aur origano sprinkle karen aur 12 minutes baked karen aur yummy pizza ready 😋
