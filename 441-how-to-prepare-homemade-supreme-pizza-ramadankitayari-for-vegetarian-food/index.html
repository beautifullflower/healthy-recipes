The ingredients needed to make Supreme Pizza #Ramadankitayari:
  1. Prepare Ingridients for the dough
  2. Prepare 3 cups All purpose flour(meeda)
  3. Take 1 1/2 tsp Sugar
  4. Make ready 3 tbsp Oil
  5. Prepare 1 Egg
  6. Get to taste Salt
  7. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp Yeast
  8. Make ready Milk For kneading as required
  9. Prepare For the chicken
  10. Make ready 375 grams Chicken boneless
  11. Get 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  12. Make ready 1 tsp Black pepper
  13. Take to taste Salt
  14. Get For Forthe Sauce
  15. Take 1 1/2 cup Tomato puree
  16. Prepare 3 Fresh tomatoes chopped
  17. Take 1 tbsp Garlic chopped
  18. Make ready Onion 1 chopped
  19. Take to taste Salt
  20. Get 1 tbsp Sugar
  21. Take 1/2 tsp Thyme
  22. Get 1 tsp Oregano
  23. Prepare 1/2 tsp Basil
  24. Get For the topping
  25. Prepare 5-6 Black olive
  26. Get Onion 1 large
  27. Make ready 1/3 cup Mushroom
  28. Make ready 1 Capsicum
  29. Prepare 1/4 cup Sweet corn
  30. Prepare 11/2 cup Mozarella chesse
  31. Get 1 cup cheddar cheese
Steps to make Supreme Pizza #Ramadankitayari:
  1. All dough ingridients well knead and covered it then keep aside and leave for rise.
  2. In a apan, heat oil add chicken,Lemon juice,black pepper and salt.
  3. Cook on high flame 6 to 8 minutes,then remove and cool it.
  4. In a pan heat oil and add onion and garlic,fry for 1 min.
  5. Then add all sauce ingridients,cook for 1 minute and add 2 cups water and cook till boil then flame slow and cook 10 to 15 minutes.
  6. Then these sauce grind it and keep a side.
  7. Roll the dough, cover it and leave to rise for 10 minutes.
  8. After this on top spread the pizza sauce and bake for 4 to 5 minutes.
  9. Then chicken,olive,capsicum,onion,mashroom,sweetcorn,mozzarella cheese, cheddar chesse and bake 15 minutes.
  10. Then remove and serve
