The ingredients needed to make Semolina muffins:
  1. Take 1 cup semolina
  2. Take 1 cup yogurt
  3. Take 1 grated carrot
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup green peas
  5. Get 1/2 cup corn kernels
  6. Get 1/2 capsicum chopped
  7. Take 1 tablespoon chopped coriander
  8. Get 2 slice bread soaked and crumbled
  9. Get 1 tablespoon oil
  10. Take 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  11. Get 1 teaspoon oregano
  12. Prepare 1/2 cup grated cheese
  13. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon eno
  14. Get 1 teaspoon baking soda
  15. Make ready to taste Salt
  16. Take 1 cup warm water
Instructions to make Semolina muffins:
  1. In a pan dry roast semolina to light golden colour. In a mixing bowl add yogurt and a cup of water and whisk it. Add semolina and all ingredients apart from eno and prepare smooth batter.
  2. The batter should look runny and let it rest for 10 minutes. The semolina will soak the moisture and become soft.
  3. Preheat oven to at 180 ¬∞C. - Add Eno and stir the batter. The batter for savory semolina muffin should be of pouring consistency.
  4. In a muffin tray line it with muffin cases and fill each to 2/3. - Finally, sprinkle some grated cheese on top.
  5. Bakes muffins in a preheated oven at 180 ¬∞C for 20 to 25 minutes or check with tooth pick as different oven takes different time.
  6. Once baked take the muffins out from the oven and let them cool completely before serving. - Savory Semolina Muffin is ready
