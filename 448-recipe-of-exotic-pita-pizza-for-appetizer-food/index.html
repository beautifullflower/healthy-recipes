The ingredients needed to make Pita Pizza:
  1. Make ready 2 Whole Wheat Pocketless Pitas
  2. Prepare 4 Tbs. Tomato Sauce
  3. Get 2 Tbs. Black Olives, sliced
  4. Get 2 oz. Ham, diced
  5. Get 2 Tbs. Onion, diced
  6. Prepare 2 Mushrooms, diced
  7. Prepare 1/2 C. Fat Free Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
Instructions to make Pita Pizza:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°. Place pitas on a baking sheet.
  2. Place 2 Tbs. of tomato sauce on each pita.
  3. Place 1 oz. of ham on each pizza.
  4. Place 1 Tbs. of olives and 1 Tbs. of onions on each pizza.
  5. Then top each pizza with ¼ C. of cheese.
  6. Bake in oven for 10-15 minutes. Or until cheese is melted and edges of pita are crispy.
