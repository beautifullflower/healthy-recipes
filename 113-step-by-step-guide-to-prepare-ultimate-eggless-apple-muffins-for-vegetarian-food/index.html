The ingredients needed to make Eggless Apple muffins:
  1. Take 1 Apple
  2. Prepare 1 cup plain flour
  3. Make ready 1 cup oats
  4. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  6. Take 1/3 cup oil or butter
  7. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  8. Take 1 cup milk
  9. Prepare 1 tsp cinnamon powder
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
Instructions to make Eggless Apple muffins:
  1. Firstly peal Apple and cut into small pieces and mix with salt, 1 tsp sugar and cinnamon powder keep aside.
  2. In a bowl take plan flour, oats, baking powder, baking soda and sugar mix well.
  3. Now add oil and milk mix well.
  4. After that add Apple mixture.
  5. Now pour into muffins cup. I used medium size 11 cup.
  6. Now baked into preheat oven at 180 degree for 20 min.
