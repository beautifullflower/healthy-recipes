The ingredients needed to make Bluechocberrychip Muffins:
  1. Prepare 250 grams plain flour
  2. Prepare 3/4 tbsp baking powder
  3. Get 100 grams caster sugar
  4. Prepare Zest of 1 lemon
  5. Take Juice from half a lemon
  6. Get 200 ml milk
  7. Get 50 grams butter, melted
  8. Get 50 grams mixed nuts, chopped
  9. Take 20 grams demerara sugar
  10. Get 1 large egg, beaten
  11. Prepare 150 grams blueberries, rinsed
  12. Get 100 grams chocolate chips/chunks
Instructions to make Bluechocberrychip Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 200'C / gas mark 6. Line a 12 hole muffin tray with your muffin cases.
  2. Sift flour into a large bowl. Gently stir in the baking powder, caster sugar and lemon zest.
  3. In another bowl, combine the egg, milk butter and lemon juice. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Pour the wet mixture into the dry and fold to form a thick, bubbly batter (this may take time depending on arm strength!)
  5. Fold in the blueberries and chocolate chips. Now spoon the mixture evenly into the muffin cases.
  6. Finally, top off each muffin with a scatter of mixed nuts and sprinkle with demerara sugar to add crunch.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes until muffins are well risen and golden. Cool on a wire rack and indulge!
