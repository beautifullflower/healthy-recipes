The ingredients needed to make Avocado & Tuna Salad:
  1. Make ready 3 Small Avocado
  2. Take 2 tins tuna
  3. Prepare 1/2 red onion
  4. Get 2 celery sticks
  5. Take 1 spring onion
  6. Get 5 stalks coriander
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  8. Make ready to taste Salt and Pepper

Instructions to make Avocado & Tuna Salad:
  1. Drain the oil out the tuna tins. Add the tuna into the clean bowl.
  2. Fine diced red onion, spring onion, celery sticks. Add all the ingredients into the tuna bowl.
  3. Take the skin off the avocado, put the fleshy part of the avocado in the bowl. Add chopped coriander, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  4. Using the back of the fork or clean hands to mash up the avocado. Mix all the ingredients very well. Served the salad in the bowl or in the wrap.
