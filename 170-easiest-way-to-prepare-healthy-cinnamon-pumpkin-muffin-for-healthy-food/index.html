The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon pumpkin muffin:
  1. Take Spread:
  2. Take 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  3. Make ready 2 TB butter, softened
  4. Take 1 TB brown sugar
  5. Get 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
  6. Get Muffins:
  7. Take 2 cups all-purpose flour
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar
  9. Take 1 TB backing powder
  10. Prepare 1,5 tsp. ground cinnamon
  11. Get 1/2 tsp. ground ginger
  12. Take 3/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
  13. Take 2 pinches salt
  14. Get 1 cup mashed cooked pumpkin
  15. Get 1/2 cup butter, melted
  16. Prepare 2 eggs (M)
  17. Make ready 1 egg (S)
  18. Get Topping:
  19. Take 1/4 cup sugar
  20. Prepare 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
  21. Prepare 1/4 cup butter melted
Instructions to make Cinnamon pumpkin muffin:
  1. Combine the dry ingredients with the spices.
  2. Mix the mashed pumpkin with eggs and melted butter in an other bowl.
  3. Add the mixture with flour to the pumpkin mixture until combined.
  4. Line the muffin tin with backing cups and fill them. Bake the muffins at 400 F for 25 minutes.
  5. Prepare the spread. Mix the ingredients, cover it, until you serve the muffins.
  6. Prepare the topping by combining butter, sugar and cinnamon.
  7. Put the topping on each muffin.
  8. Serve them with spread.
