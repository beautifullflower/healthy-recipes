The ingredients needed to make Sourdough Spelt Muffin:
  1. Take 110 g starter fed or discard starter
  2. Make ready 1 egg
  3. Prepare 50 g cashew milk or dairy of your choice
  4. Take 25 g vegetables oil or oil of your choice/ butter
  5. Get 40 g brown sugar or add more if you want your muffin to be more sweet
  6. Prepare 40 g raisins
  7. Prepare 120 g Spelt flour
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon salt
  9. Take 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  10. Take 1 teaspoon baking soda
  11. Get Handful frozen or fresh berries
Steps to make Sourdough Spelt Muffin:
  1. Mix your wet ingredients
  2. Add your dry ingredient except frozen or fresh berries
  3. If you want muffin to be fluffy, let it sit. I had it rest in room temperature for 8 hrs.
  4. If you are not letting your mixture to rest, you may add your berries. Filll up muffin cup to 1/2 (if you let it rest)or 3/4 (norest). Preheat oven at 200C fan.
  5. Bake muffin for 15-18min.
