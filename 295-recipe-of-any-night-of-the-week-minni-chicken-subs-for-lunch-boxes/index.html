The ingredients needed to make Minni chicken subs for lunch boxes:
  1. Prepare fillet chicken washed and cut into strips
  2. Make ready tspns chicken spice
  3. Get Caesar salad dressing mix
  4. Prepare sliced mushrooms
  5. Take Sliced red onion
  6. Prepare olive oil
  7. Make ready mini sub buns sliced and toasted
  8. Prepare Some cheese for sprinkling over the top

Instructions to make Minni chicken subs for lunch boxes:
  1. Place chicken in a large bowl; sprinkle with salad dressing mix. In a large skillet, saute mushrooms and onion in oil for 3 minutes. Add chicken; saute for 6 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink.
  2. Spoon mixture onto bun bottoms; top with cheese. Put into oven for about 4minutes or until cheese is melted.
  3. Robot peppers are optional and can be added to toppings
