The ingredients needed to make Eggs with onions and cheese:
  1. Make ready 3 eggs
  2. Get 50 g tilster cheese
  3. Get 150 g baby onions
  4. Get 2 g vegeta
  5. Prepare 2 g paprika
  6. Get 200 g radishes raw
  7. Prepare 20 g sour cream

Steps to make Eggs with onions and cheese:
  1. Mix eggs into a bowl and put spices in it. In another bowl chop onions and mix it with sour cream until its liquid.
  2. Heat up a pan and when its really hot put eggs in. Scramble them and when they are 1/3 done put onions and cream in. Mix again and put spices. I prefer vegeta and paprika. Chilly and pepper would work also.
  3. When eggs are 2/3 done, put cheese on top so it melts into the eggs, but doesn't burn.
  4. Chop radishes and serve with eggs.
  5. Enjoy.
