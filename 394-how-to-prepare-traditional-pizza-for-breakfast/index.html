How to Prepare Traditional Pizza for Breakfast

The ingredients needed to make Pizza:
  1. Get Pizza dough
  2. Make ready 1 cup Boneless chicken
  3. Prepare 5 tbsp Pizza sauce
  4. Prepare 10 pieces Black olives
  5. Prepare Onion chopped in square
  6. Take 50 g Mushrooms
  7. Get 100 g Cheddar Cheese
  8. Take 100 g Mozzarella
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  10. Prepare Chicken tika masala
  11. Get 2 tbsp Oil
Steps to make Pizza:
  1. Pre heat oven over 180 c,Roll out pizza dough and put in pizza plate.
  2. Spread over pizza sauce,cheddar cheese just small portion.
  3. In pan heat oil and add ginger garlic paste,after 2 mins add chicken and cook.When water dissolved and chicken properly cooked,remove from heat.
  4. Over pizza roti,spread chicken,chopped mushrooms and cut in half olives.
  5. Cover with mozzarella and cheddar.
  6. Put in pre heated oven and cook for 30 mins.
  7. Take out from oven and let it cool for moment. Then slice and eat and enjoy.
