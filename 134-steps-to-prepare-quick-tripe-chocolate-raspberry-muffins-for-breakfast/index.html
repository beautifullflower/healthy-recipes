The ingredients needed to make Tripe chocolate raspberry muffins 🍫:
  1. Get 225 g plain flour
  2. Get 55 g Coco powder
  3. Get 1 tbsp baking powder
  4. Get 1 pinch sea salt
  5. Prepare 115 g brown sugar
  6. Prepare 2 eggs (beaten)
  7. Make ready 250 ml double cream
  8. Get 6 tbsp melted butter
  9. Make ready Raspberry jam
  10. Get Chocolate buttons (large)
  11. Make ready Chocolate ganache
  12. Get Mixed chocolate sprinkles
Instructions to make Tripe chocolate raspberry muffins 🍫:
  1. Method - Prep:20min  ›  Cook:20min  ›  Ready in:40min
  2. Pre heat oven to 180dc.
  3. Line muffin tins with 12 cases.
  4. Combine beaten eggs with cream and melted butter
  5. Combine flour, salt, sugar, baking powder and Coco powder.
  6. Mix everything together gently. Wanrning do not over mix
  7. Fill all muffin cases half way.
  8. Add a single chocolate button to the centre of each half filled casing.
  9. Add drop of jam on top of button (approx the same diameter as button)
  10. Add another chocolate button on top of jam in each tray
  11. Use remaining mix to fill all the muffin casings
  12. Bake in oven for 20 -25 mins until well resin and firm to touch
  13. Remove from oven and add ganache and chocolate sprinkles and leave to cool before serving.
