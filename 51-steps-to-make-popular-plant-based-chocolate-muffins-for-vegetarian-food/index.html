The ingredients needed to make Plant-Based Chocolate Muffins:
  1. Make ready 200 g flour
  2. Take 50 g cocoa powder
  3. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  4. Get 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  5. Take 1 pinch salt
  6. Get 125 ml oat milk
  7. Prepare 100 g sugar
  8. Make ready 80 ml oil
  9. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  10. Make ready 2 tbsp ground flax seed
  11. Make ready 6 tbsp water
  12. Prepare 1 banana
  13. Make ready 200 g dairy-free dark chocolate
Steps to make Plant-Based Chocolate Muffins:
  1. Blend the ground flax-seed & the water in small cup and set aside for 15 minutes.
  2. Sieve the flour, the cocoa powder, the bicarbonate of soda, the baking powder and the pinch of salt into a bowl, stirring & mixing well.
  3. Pour the oat milk, the oil, the vanilla extract, the flax-seed eggs and mashed banana in a separate bowl and mix them. As soon the mixture has the silky texture, add the flour mixture and the chopped bar of chocolate.
  4. Take a muffin baking form, filling with paper cups, and using ice-cream scoop to fill up with the muffin batter one by one.
  5. Bake the muffins in 180CÂ° preheated oven for 20-25 minutes.
