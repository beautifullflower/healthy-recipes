The ingredients needed to make Pizza:
  1. Get 1/2 cup wheat flour
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup curd
  3. Get 1/4 tsp baking powder
  4. Make ready 1/8 tsp cooking soda
  5. Prepare to taste Salt
  6. Take 2 tbsp oil
  7. Get For topping
  8. Get 1 medium sized chopped capsicum
  9. Prepare 1/4 mushroom
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped pineapple
  11. Take 1 medium sized sliced onion
  12. Make ready olives
  13. Take Jalapeño
  14. Take 1 tbsp chilli flakes
  15. Prepare 1 tbsp Pizza topping
  16. Prepare 1 tbsp mixed herbs
  17. Get Cheese
  18. Prepare leaves Mixed salad
  19. Make ready to taste Salt
  20. Take 1 tsp black pepper powder
  21. Prepare Pizza sauce for spreading
  22. Make ready Butter
Instructions to make Pizza:
  1. For the base. In a bowl take the wheat flour. Add baking powder, cooking soda, salt and 2 tbsp oil. Mix well. Now add curd and make a soft dough and leave for 10-12 mins
  2. Now arrange all the toppings.
  3. Make 3-4 equal parts of the dough as per your choice. Start rolling out the dough as per your choice of thickness. And make small holes all over.
  4. On the side take a pan add salt and place a stand inside and put a plate on top.
  5. Put the rolled dough on top of the plate and in low to medium heat cook for 10 mins. Prepare all the bases just like this
  6. Once the base is ready take it out on to a separate plate
  7. First spread butter and pizza sauce on top of the base.
  8. Put all the toppings on top. Sprinkle chilli flakes and pizza topper, cheese and garden leaves.
  9. Again in pan place the pizza and cook in low medium heat until the cheese has melted and the base becomes crispy. Prepare all your pizzas just like this.
  10. Your delicious yummy yummy pizza is ready. Enjoy
