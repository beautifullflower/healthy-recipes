The ingredients needed to make Banana Oat Chocolate chip muffins:
  1. Prepare 60 g Rolled Oats x
  2. Make ready 260 ml Milk x
  3. Take 225 g Plain flour x
  4. Take tsp Baking powder x3
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp Salt x
  6. Get 85 g Plain chocolate chips x
  7. Take 1 egg, beaten with a fork
  8. Prepare 4 very ripe and very large bananas
  9. Get 90 ml Vegetable oil x
  10. Prepare 1 tsp Vanilla essence x
Steps to make Banana Oat Chocolate chip muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 190-200 degrees Celsius
  2. In a medium bowl, combine oats and milk and set aside
  3. In a large bowl, sift flour, bp, and salt. Stir in chocolate chips
  4. Add to oat and milk mixture the egg, vanilla and oil. Stir well. Mash up bananas well then add to the mixture
  5. Pour wet mixture into dry. Stir until combined. Batter should have a good dropping consistency - not too thick and not too thin
  6. Fill muffin cups 3/4 full. Bake for around 20 minutes, until tops are lightly browned and feel firm to touch - - N.B. You could replace chocolate chips with 110g of dried fruit like apricot, cranberries, or raisins
