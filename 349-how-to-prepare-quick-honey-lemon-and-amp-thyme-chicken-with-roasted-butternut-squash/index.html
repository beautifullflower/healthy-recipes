Steps to make Honey lemon & thyme chicken (with) roasted butternut squash:
  1. Coat chicken breasts in olive oil and season well with salt and pepper. Place in a baking pan.
  2. Peel and cut butternut squash into thick slices and oil and season.
  3. Roast the chicken and butternut squash in the same pan for 30 mins.
  4. In the meantime mix together finely grated rind on lemon, thyme and honey. And prepare any desired side dishes (I love new potatoes and a fresh crunchy red cabbage salad)
  5. At 30 minutes. Remove the butternut squash from the pan.
  6. Drain off most ofthe excess juices and oil from the chicken into a cup for later. - Cover the chicken with the lemon honey glaze and return to oven for another 8 -10 minutes. (Basting every few minutes)
  7. Once chicken is cooked. Serve up with your roasted squash, potato and salad.
  8. For additional honey glaze return the juices and oil previously removed from the chicken back to the remaining glaze in the pan to loosen. Pour over as needed.
