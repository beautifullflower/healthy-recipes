The ingredients needed to make Banana raisin muffins:
  1. Get 50 g butter*
  2. Take 50 g oil*
  3. Prepare 1 egg*
  4. Get 1 banana, mashed*
  5. Get 50 g milk*
  6. Take 100 self raising flour
  7. Get 3 g baking powder
  8. Get 50 g sugar
  9. Get 1/2 cup raisins
  10. Prepare oats
Instructions to make Banana raisin muffins:
  1. Begin by preheating the oven to 170°c. Line a muffin tray with 6 muffin cases.
  2. First melt the butter. Then add all wet ingredients (starred *) to the butter.
  3. Next add all remaining ingredients except the oats, and stir gently till just mixed.
  4. Fill each muffin case well so that all mixture is used in each case. (Mixture may come 0.5cm near to the top which is fine)
  5. Sprinkle the tops with oats, then bake for 25 minutes, or till golden browned on both top and bottom.
