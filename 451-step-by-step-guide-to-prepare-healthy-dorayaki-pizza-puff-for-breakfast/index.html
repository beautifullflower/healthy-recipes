The ingredients needed to make Dorayaki pizza puff:
  1. Make ready Pizza dough
  2. Get 280 g bread flour
  3. Take 190 g milk
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp sugar
  5. Prepare pinch salt
  6. Take 20 g butter
  7. Prepare 1 tsp dry active yeast
  8. Take Pizza stuff
  9. Prepare mushroom
  10. Prepare sliced tomato
  11. Prepare sausage
  12. Get bell pepper
  13. Get pizza sauce
  14. Prepare mozzarella cheese
Instructions to make Dorayaki pizza puff:
  1. Prepare pizza dough according to bread maker instruction
  2. Roll the dough into round 7 inch diameter
  3. Apply pizza sauce, topping amd mozzarella cheese.
  4. Roll another piece of round flatted dough and place on top. Then try to seal the edge of tje dough.
  5. Bake at 170C for 20 min
