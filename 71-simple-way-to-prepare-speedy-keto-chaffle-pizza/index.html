The ingredients needed to make Keto Chaffle Pizza:
  1. Get 1 large egg
  2. Make ready 1 cup finely shredded mozerella cheese
  3. Get Sliced pepperoni
  4. Take 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  5. Get 1/2 tsp marjoram
  6. Get 1/2 tsp oregano
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp basil
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp almond flour (optional)
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup Ragu Simply No Sugar Added Roasted Garlic Pasta sauce
Steps to make Keto Chaffle Pizza:
  1. Mix 1 large egg with 1/2 cup of finely shredded mozerella cheese and 1/2 tsp of almond flour (optional).
  2. Pour egg/cheese chaffle batter onto waffle iron and cook until golden brown. 4-6 min.
  3. Place chaffle on aluminum foil on a baking sheet. Top the chaffle with sauce, spices, cheese, and pepperoni spread evenly.
  4. Bake at 400 deg farenheit for 5-10 min or until cheese is melted
