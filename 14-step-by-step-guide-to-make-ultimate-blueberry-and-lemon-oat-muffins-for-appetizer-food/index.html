The ingredients needed to make Blueberry and Lemon Oat Muffins:
  1. Prepare 240 ml dairy free milk
  2. Take 55 g brown sugar
  3. Get 2 tablespoons honey or agave syrup
  4. Prepare 1/2 large apple peeled and chopped
  5. Take 1 tablespoon vegetable or olive oil
  6. Take 1 egg
  7. Prepare 135 g gluten free oat flour
  8. Make ready 70 g gluten free self-raising flour
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking powder
  10. Make ready 100 g blueberries tossed in a little gluten free flour
  11. Make ready zest of one lemon
Steps to make Blueberry and Lemon Oat Muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 200 oC - Place the dairy free milk, sugar, honey, apple, oil and egg in a blender - Blitz until smooth
  2. In a large bowl place the oat flour, self-raising flour and baking powder - Pour in the contents of the blender and mix until well combined - Stir in the blueberries and lemon zest
  3. Line a deep muffin tin with paper cases - Split the mixture between the 6 muffin cases - I topped my muffins with a sprinkle of cinnamon, brown sugar, gluten free oats and a few more blueberries
  4. Bake in the oven for 25 minutes - Remove from the oven and allow to cool
