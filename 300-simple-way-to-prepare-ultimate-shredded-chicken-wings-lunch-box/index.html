The ingredients needed to make Shredded Chicken wings Lunch box:
  1. Take Rice
  2. Prepare Shredded 2 chicken wings
  3. Get Teriyaki sauce
  4. Make ready Scallion
  5. Get Avocado
  6. Prepare Cherry tomatoes
Instructions to make Shredded Chicken wings Lunch box:
  1. Shred roasted chicken wings
  2. Reheat chicken in a small pan
  3. Add sauce and half of sliced scallion, cook together
  4. Bring your favorite bento box, Put rice, chicken in and sprinkle half of scallions on the top.
  5. Put sliced avocado and tomatoes or any other vegetables
  6. Enjoy!
