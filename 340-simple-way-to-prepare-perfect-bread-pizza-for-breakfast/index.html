Simple Way to Prepare Perfect Bread pizza for Breakfast

To get started with this particular recipe, we must prepare a few ingredients. You can have bread pizza using 6 ingredients and 11 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Bread pizza:
  1. Prepare 2 brown bread slices
  2. Take 1/2 cup Capiscum + Mushrooms+ onions + Tomatoes
  3. Prepare 2 tsp Butter/oil for greasing pan
  4. Make ready 2 tsp Oregano
  5. Get 2-3 tsp Pizza sauce
  6. Get 2 Amul cheese slices
Steps to make Bread pizza:
  1. Keep these ingredients ready and chop all the veggies and keep it ready before you start preparing bread Pizza(you can use veggies of your own choice)
  2. Take 2 brown bread slices(You can use any bread)
  3. Spread pizza sauce on the bread slices
  4. Add amul cheese slices on it
  5. Layer bell peppers, onions, mushrooms and tomatoes as desired
  6. Grease a pan/griddle with 1-2 tsp of butter/oil.
  7. Place the bread slices on pan.. Cover and cook till cheese melts and the bread is toasted…
  8. Remove it and place it on a plate…
  9. Sprinkle oregano…. And slice it into wedges
  10. Serve bread pizza hot or (accompanied with tomato ketchup)
  11. Nom nom
