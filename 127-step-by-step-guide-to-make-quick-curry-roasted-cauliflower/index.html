The ingredients needed to make Curry Roasted Cauliflower:
  1. Take 1/2 head cauliflower break them into small florets
  2. Prepare 2 teaspoon olive oil
  3. Get 1/2 teaspoon curry powder
  4. Take 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  5. Take 1/4 teaspoon cumin
  6. Take 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  7. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon sea salt or to taste
Steps to make Curry Roasted Cauliflower:
  1. Put the cauliflower florets in a large microwave-safe bowl and microwave for about 4-5 minutes.
  2. When done, transfer the florets to a large mixing bowl. Add in olive oil and toss. Then, add rest of the ingredients to the mixing bowl and toss again to coat.
