The ingredients needed to make Pizza Cake!:
  1. Take 1.5 cup flour
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup yogurt
  3. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking powder
  4. Prepare 3 g dry yeast
  5. Take 1 teaspoon sugar
  6. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  7. Get 2 bacon
  8. Make ready 1/2 tomato and sweet paprika
  9. Prepare 3-4 brown mushroom
  10. Prepare 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  11. Prepare 300 g or more cheese
Steps to make Pizza Cake!:
  1. Make pizza dough. No need rest time.
  2. Apply butter to the cake mold and dust flour. Spread dough and put it in 12cm cake mold.
  3. Fry brown mushroom with butter and cut ingredients.
  4. Put cheese on bottom then add tomato, tomato sauce, mushroom and bacon. Then put more cheese as much as you can put!
