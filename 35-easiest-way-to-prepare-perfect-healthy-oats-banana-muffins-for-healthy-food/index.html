The ingredients needed to make Healthy Oats Banana Muffins:
  1. Get Dry ingredients
  2. Make ready 230 g (1 3/4 cup) oats flour
  3. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  7. Prepare Wet ingredients
  8. Prepare 3 ripe bananas
  9. Make ready 100 ml coconut oil
  10. Get 40 g honey
  11. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  12. Get 2 eggs, room temperature
  13. Get Others
  14. Make ready Raisins, walnuts, pecans, chocolate chips
Steps to make Healthy Oats Banana Muffins:
  1. Add all of the dry ingredients into a bowl and mix well. Set aside.
  2. In a bowl, mash the bananas into a fine puree. Then add the rest of the wet ingredients and whisk just to combine.
  3. Add flour mixture into wet mixture and mix just until well combined.
  4. Scoop batter evenly into lined muffin tray of 12.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 200¬∫C (400¬∫F) for 18 minutes or until fully cooked. Leave for 10 minutes before transferring them on a rack to cool completely.
  6. Enjoy! :)
