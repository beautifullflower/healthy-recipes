The ingredients needed to make Bread pizza:
  1. Make ready Bread slices 6
  2. Take Chicken cubes 250 gm
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp Tikka masala
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Vinegar
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp Chilli garlic sauce
  7. Get 1/4 cup Ketchup
  8. Prepare Capsicum
  9. Get Onion
  10. Get Mushrooms
  11. Get Cheddar cheese
  12. Take Mozeralla cheese
Instructions to make Bread pizza:
  1. Marinate the chicken with ginger garlic paste, tikka masala nad vinegar
  2. Cook the chicken with little oil
  3. In a bowl add chilli gralic sauce and ketchup mix well
  4. Take a slice spread ketchup mix, chicken, veggies and cheese
  5. Bake for 10 mins and serve
