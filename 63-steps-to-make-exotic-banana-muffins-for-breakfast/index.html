The ingredients needed to make Banana Muffins:
  1. Get 4 extra ripe Bananas
  2. Prepare 1 egg
  3. Get 1 tblsp vanilla essence
  4. Get 75 g butter melted
  5. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  6. Get 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  7. Get 1 1/2 cup cake flour
Instructions to make Banana Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 230 degrees. Mix (I blended) banana, egg and vanilla essence. Add butter and sugar and mix. Add bicarbonate of soda and mix.
  2. Fold in flour but don’t over mix, just fold in. Using a ladle scoop mixture into muffin tray.
  3. Bake at 230 degrees for 5 minutes. Reduce to 180 degrees and bake for further 10 minutes till done and a tester comes out clean.
