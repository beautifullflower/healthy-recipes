The ingredients needed to make Cheesy.Tomato.Mushroom Pizza:
  1. Prepare 1 pizza base
  2. Get 2 onions
  3. Make ready 4 tomatoes
  4. Make ready 2 capsicum
  5. Take 6 mushroom
  6. Make ready to taste Salt
  7. Prepare to taste Pepper
  8. Take 1/2 tsp Oregano
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp Chilli flakes
  10. Make ready 1 cup grated pizza cheese
  11. Take 2 tbsp pizza.pasta sauce
  12. Take 2 tsp tomato ketchup
Steps to make Cheesy.Tomato.Mushroom Pizza:
  1. Take the pizza base
  2. Spread pizza.pasta sauce on the base
  3. Put chopped vdgetables
  4. Spread tomato ketchup
  5. Sprinkle salt.pepper.organo and chilli flakes
  6. Cover with grated cheese
  7. Pre heat the oven
  8. Bake 10 minutes or till cheese melt
  9. Serve hot
