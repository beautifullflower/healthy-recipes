The ingredients needed to make Blueberry and banana muffins:
  1. Take 1 cup oil - I use olive oil
  2. Make ready 3 eggs
  3. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar (brown is best)
  4. Make ready 1 cup peeled and mashed banana (about 2-3 whole bananas)
  5. Make ready 1 cup blueberries
  6. Prepare 1.5 cups self-raising flour
  7. Make ready Half teaspoon grated nutmeg
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
Steps to make Blueberry and banana muffins:
  1. Heat oven to 175 degrees C. Oil muffin tin or line with patty cake paper cases.
  2. Mash bananas and wash blueberries
  3. Add oil, eggs and sugar to a mixing bowl and whisk until combined and mixture lightens (hand whisk is fine)
  4. Add remainder of ingredients and mix gently with a spatula or wooden spoon.
  5. Divide muffin batter evenly between patty cake papers.
  6. Sprinkle with granulated sugar if you prefer a crunchy top to the muffin
  7. Bake in the oven for approximately 20-25 mins. Muffins will be springy to the touch when done.
  8. Cool slightly then remove to a cooling rack.
  9. Enjoy! Makes 12-15 muffins.
