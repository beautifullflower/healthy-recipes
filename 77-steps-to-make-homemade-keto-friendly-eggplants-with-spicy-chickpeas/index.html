The ingredients needed to make Keto Friendly Eggplants with spicy chickpeas:
  1. Prepare 1 onion chopped
  2. Prepare 4 garlic cloves chopped
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp fresh ginger, peeled and chopped
  4. Prepare 4 tbs ghee/coconut butter
  5. Get 2 tsp ground cumin
  6. Get 2 tsp ground coriander
  7. Take 1 tsp ground turmeric
  8. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala
  9. Take 1 tsp mild curry powder
  10. Get 2 cups/150 g. Mushrooms/ chopped
  11. Prepare 1 large tomato(chopped)
  12. Get 1 cup/250 g. canned chickpeas, drained
  13. Get 2 aubergines/ narrow eggplant, splint in half
  14. Prepare Freshly ground black pepper
